# Hearst

## Summary
Hearst is the Bitcoin mining infrastructure operator behind Vancelian's **Cloud Mining by Hearst** exclusive offer. Founded in **2021** by Adrien Nejkovic and Olivier Nejkovic (Bitcoin miners since 2015) with engineer Loïc Ricci, Hearst provides Mining-as-a-Service to public and private institutions, headquartered in the United Arab Emirates with offices in Hong Kong and France. The Vancelian offer is specifically backed by Hearst's **Ethiopia site at Legetafo (Addis Ababa region)** — a 20,000 m², 20 MW hydroelectric-powered Bitmain S21 Pro+ mining facility.

## Key Information

### Company
- **Founded:** 2021 by Adrien Nejkovic, Olivier Nejkovic and Loïc Ricci
- **Headquarters:** United Arab Emirates
- **Offices:** Hong Kong (Asia-Pacific), France (Europe)
- **Mission:** Mining-as-a-Service for institutions and (via Vancelian) for retail investors

### Scale
- **10,000+** miners under management
- **2+ Exahash** total hashrate
- **500+ BTC** mined to date
- Deploys mining infrastructure across **7 countries** — i.e. the countries where Hearst installs and operates mining machines in data centres (this is the operational footprint, not the count of legal entities or office locations). Hearst is the UAE leadership market and ranks among the top 30 mining operators globally.
- **10 blockchains** supported

### Mining sites
| Site | Capacity | Energy mix |
|---|---|---|
| Ethiopia (Legetafo) | 20 MW | 100% renewable hydroelectric |
| Norway | 15 MW | Fully renewable hydroelectric |
| Oman | 140 MW | Government-licensed BTC site |
| Kazakhstan | 140 MW | Government-licensed BTC site |
| UAE | 140 MW | Government-licensed BTC site |
| USA | 20 MW | Grid mix + flare gas |
| Brazil | 20 MW | Grid mix + flare gas |

### Ethiopia site (the one backing the Vancelian offer)
- **Location:** Legetafo, Addis Ababa region
- **Surface:** 20,000 m²
- **Capacity:** 20 MW
- **Energy:** Hydroelectric, sourced from the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam (GERD)
- **Machines:** Bitmain S21 Pro+ (hashrate **234 TH/s**, power **3,531 W** per machine)
- **Uptime:** >93%
- **Carbon impact:** 0% emissions claim (fully decarbonised)

### Technology stack
- Air-cooling and immersion-cooling
- Both fixed data centres and mobile container deployments
- Strategic partnership with **Bitmain** (manufacturer of mining hardware)
- Additional partnerships with Gigatons and Gigatech for renewable energy and carbon reduction

## FAQ
**Q: Where do my Cloud Mining funds physically go?**
A: To Hearst's Ethiopia mining site at Legetafo (Addis Ababa region) — a 20,000 m², 20 MW facility powered entirely by hydroelectric energy from the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam, equipped with Bitmain S21 Pro+ machines.

**Q: In how many countries does Hearst operate?**
A: **7 countries**, meaning the countries where Hearst deploys and operates mining infrastructure in data centres. This is the operational footprint, not the number of legal entities or offices.

**Q: When was Hearst founded?**
A: **2021** by Adrien Nejkovic, Olivier Nejkovic and Loïc Ricci.

**Q: How many BTC has Hearst mined?**
A: 500+ to date.
