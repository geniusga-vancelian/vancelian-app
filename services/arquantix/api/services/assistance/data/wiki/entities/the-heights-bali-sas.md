# THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS

## Summary
**THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS** is the French company created specifically to operate Vancelian's **7 Luxury Villas in Bali (The Heights Munduk)** exclusive offer (closed since 6 October 2025 at the €4M cap). It owns a 1.3-hectare site in Munduk freehold via Indonesian subsidiaries and is delivering a 7-villa luxury resort scheduled for completion end-2026, with operation 2026-2029 and resale 2028-2029. Founded by Vancelian co-founders Gaël Itier (CEO) and Julien Halimi.

## Key Information

### Company
- **Legal name:** THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS (French société par actions simplifiée)
- **Founders:** Gaël Itier (CEO of Vancelian), Julien Halimi (Co-Founder of Vancelian)
- **Operating structure:** Owns the land and develops the project via **Indonesian subsidiaries** (full freehold ownership)

### The project (The Heights Munduk)
- **Location:** Munduk, Bali, Indonesia (highland village, 1,000 m altitude)
- **Site area:** 1.3 hectare
- **Configuration:** 7 villas total
  - 2 × **3-Bedroom Deluxe** villas (305 m² each)
  - 3 × **2-Bedroom Privilège** villas (193 m² each)
  - 2 × **2-Bedroom Premium** villas (179 m² each)
- **Architectural signature:** suspended jungle road accessed by silent electric buggy, central reception lobby with raised fish pond, indoor fireplace
- **Service tier:** managed by Bali Management Villa, with 24h butler service, on-demand private chef, electric buggy transfers

### Operational team
- **Developer:** Conceptive Construction (5-year-old French company in Bali; previous projects: Villa Jungle Loft Canggu 2024, The Heights Bingin 2025, The Heights Melasti 2025)
- **Architect:** Eben Lontoh (Indonesian; previous: Alila Kothaifaru Maldives 2022, Alila Villas KohRussey Cambodia 2018)
- **Maître d'œuvre / contractor:** PT. Hamengku Karya (HMK) — Indonesian luxury hotel contractor since 1981, portfolio includes Kempinski Bali, Anantara Vacation Club, Ramayana Resort, Samara Hills Lombok

### Liability structure
- **THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS** alone bears the loan, the debt, the project risk, and the obligation to repay capital and interest.
- It has a **best-effort obligation** to secure the investment.
- **Vancelian (Automata France SAS, DASP E2023-087)** facilitates the financing through its regulated platform and provides custody — **Vancelian is NOT responsible for repayment of capital or interest**.

### Land security
- Current land valuation: ~**USD 650,000**
- Government rezoning expected: agricultural (green zone) → tourism (red zone), expected to materially increase land value

## FAQ
**Q: The Bali offer is closed — what happens to my money now?**
A: THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS continues to develop the resort per the schedule (delivery end 2026, rental phase 2026–2029, resale 2028–2029). You receive your 10.2%–11% APR daily into your Vancelian balance throughout the 4-year commitment.

**Q: Who is THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS?**
A: A French company (SAS) created specifically for this project by Vancelian's CEO Gaël Itier and co-founder Julien Halimi. It owns the Munduk land freehold via Indonesian subsidiaries and is solely responsible for repaying the loan.

**Q: What is the actual land value backing my investment?**
A: USD ~650,000, with expected appreciation when the land is reclassified from agricultural to tourism zone.

**Q: Who built it?**
A: French developer Conceptive Construction, Indonesian architect Eben Lontoh, and Indonesian contractor PT. Hamengku Karya (HMK) — a 40+ year veteran of luxury hotel construction in Bali.

> **Title contradiction note:** the Vancelian FAQ describes Julien Halimi as "Head of RWA at Vancelian"; the Bali brochure describes him as "Co Founder" of Vancelian; the Dubai Solaria brochure describes him as "Managing Director Asia" of Solaria. All three are accurate (different roles at different entities) but worth flagging for Kephren — Julien Halimi wears multiple hats.
