# Conceptive Construction

## Summary
**Conceptive Construction** is the French high-end real estate developer in charge of designing and building the **The Heights Bali** project (7 luxury villas at Munduk). Based in Bali, the company combines biculturual French/Indonesian expertise with rigorous quality control and is a major player in upmarket villa development on the island.

## Key facts
- **Founded:** active for 25+ years in Bali real estate development (5+ years under the Conceptive Construction brand specifically).
- **Base:** Bali, Indonesia (French-led management).
- **Specialty:** turnkey luxury villa development — from land selection through architecture, construction, interior design, and rental management.
- **Vancelian role:** project developer for The Heights Bali (the underlying asset of the closed Vancelian "7 Luxury Villas in Bali" exclusive offer).

## Reference projects
- The Heights Bingin (8 villas)
- The Heights Melasti (Ungasan)
- Villas Apricot, Riptide, Kasadya, Barsa (Bingin & Seminyak)
- The Heights Bali — Munduk (in progress, Vancelian co-financed)

## Service scope
Conceptive Construction delivers an end-to-end programme:
- Rigorous **land selection**
- **Bioclimatic architecture and construction**
- **Interior design and furniture**
- **Rental villa management**
- **Legal and administrative support** for acquisition and permits

## Quality and supervision
- **10-year structural guarantee** on construction.
- **End-to-end supervision** of every phase: planning, execution, finishings.
- Collaboration with both local Indonesian and international architects/engineers.

## Why it matters for Vancelian investors
The credibility of The Heights Bali rests substantially on Conceptive's track record. The 10-year structural guarantee, the rigorous quality control, and the demonstrated ability to deliver upmarket villa programmes in Bali are core elements of the project's risk mitigation. Conceptive's French management combined with deep local execution experience reduces typical execution risks of cross-border luxury real estate.
