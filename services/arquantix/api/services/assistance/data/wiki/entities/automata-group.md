# Automata Group

## Summary
**Automata Group** is the **UK-based holding company** that owns the **Vancelian brand** and ecosystem. It is the parent of multiple operational subsidiaries across Europe and the UAE, and the ultimate parent of Automata ICO Limited (issuer of the AKTIO token).

## Brand lineage of the retail app

| Period                   | Commercial brand | Note                                                                                              |
| ------------------------ | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2017+                    | **Automata**     | MVP name at project inception                                                                     |
| 2021–2024                | **Akt.io**       | Brand used at ICO and first retail app launch. AKTIO whitepaper (April 2022) refers to this name. |
| 2024 (interim)           | **RAYN**         | Short-lived rebrand abandoned after trademark conflicts with other companies claiming the name    |
| **Q3/Q4 2024 → present** | **Vancelian**    | **Definitive and current commercial brand**                                                       |

Throughout these changes, **Automata Group (UK) remained the holding** and continues to own the Vancelian brand.

## Group structure (as of April 2026)

**Automata Group (UK)** — historical holding
- 100% owner of **Automata ICO Limited** (Ireland) — AKTIO token issuer; stays under UK holding.
- Historical subsidiaries operating the Akt.io platform (Automata Wealth, Automata Pay, Automata Exchange, Automata France, Automata Germany, Automata Dubai, Automata AI, etc.).

**Vancelian Group ADGM** (Abu Dhabi) — new holding being built separately (also referenced as "Automata Holding ADGM")
- 100% of **Automata France SAS** (France) — PSAN E2023-087, applying for PSCA MiCA.
- 100% of **Automata FZE** (Dubai) — VARA In-Principle Approval December 2025.

See `concepts/regulatory-roadmap.md` for the full regulatory transition plan.

## Role of Automata Group in AKTIO economics
- Funds raised in the AKTIO Initial Offering were **loaned by Automata ICO Ltd to other members of the Automata Group** for development of the technology and ecosystem (per Whitepaper V.4.0.0, April 2022).
- AKTIO is accepted across the ecosystem and benefits from integration with services provided by Automata Group subsidiaries.
