# Automata ICO Limited

## Summary
**Automata ICO Limited** is the Irish company that issued the **AKTIO token**. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary of **Automata Group (UK)**, the holding company behind the Vancelian brand.

## Key facts
- **Legal name:** Automata ICO Limited
- **Registration number:** 690280 (Ireland)
- **Registered address:** 3rd Floor Ormond Building, 31-36 Ormond Quay Upper, Dublin 7, D07 Ee37, Ireland
- **Operational address:** Unit 3d North Point House, North Point Business Park, New Mallow Road, Cork, T23 AT2, Ireland
- **Managing Director:** Stephane Perrin
- **Parent:** Automata Group (UK) — 100% ownership
- **Website:** www.akt.io (historical brand; current brand is Vancelian)
- **Contact:** info@akt.io

## Role
- **Sole issuer of the AKTIO token** (ERC-20 on Ethereum, ticker AKTIO).
- Holds the legal note qualifying AKTIO as a **utility token**.
- Irish jurisdiction for AKTIO regulatory framework.

## Regulatory status
- **Registered with the Central Bank of Ireland (CBI)** as a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) for AML / CFT purposes, under the Criminal Justice (Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing) Amendment Act 2021.
- Commenced business in Ireland on 12 March 2021.
- Subject to CBI supervisory authority for AML/CFT.
- Also holds an Italian branch used to provide Italian PSAN services via AOM.

## Corporate position (as of April 2026)
- Automata ICO Ltd **remains under Automata Group UK** and will **not** be transferred to the new Vancelian Group ADGM holding currently being set up.
- AKTIO is a decentralised ERC-20 token on Ethereum — no replacement program announced. The previously envisaged "VNC Token" migration was **not executed**: no VNC issuance, no AKTIO buyback initiated by Automata ICO Limited.
