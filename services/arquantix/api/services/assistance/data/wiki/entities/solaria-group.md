# Solaria Group

## Summary
Solaria is the **French luxury real estate group** that operates the **Dubai Villa – Al Barari** exclusive offer for Vancelian. Founded in **December 2021** by co-founders and historical shareholders of Vancelian, Solaria designs, finances and executes high-end real estate projects in iconic destinations (Cannes, Dubai, Bali, Niseko, Tulum). For each Vancelian Exclusive Offer it counterpartys, Solaria alone bears the debt, the operational risk, and the obligation to repay capital and interest — Vancelian only provides the regulated investment platform and digital asset custody.

## Key Information

### Company
- **Legal name:** SOLARIA
- **Registration:** RCS Antibes 908 978 893
- **Founded:** December 2021
- **HQ countries of operation:** 5 (France, UAE, Indonesia, Mexico, Japan)
- **Track record:** 20+ projects completed internationally, €50M+ total invested, 30% average gross project margin, average operation duration 24 months

### Management team
- **Alexandre Lahouti** — CEO (Americas, Europe & Middle East). 30+ years entrepreneurial experience, builds turnkey luxury real estate from the French Riviera to Dubai, Mexico, Marrakech.
- **Julien David Halimi** — Managing Director Asia. Vancelian co-founder, drives Solaria's RWA (real-world asset) expansion strategy across Asia.

### Liability structure for Vancelian Exclusive Offers
- **Solaria** receives the loan amount on subscription, bears the debt, and assumes the project risk.
- Solaria has a **best-effort obligation** to repay capital and interest at maturity.
- **Vancelian (Automata France SAS)** is a **DASP registered with the AMF (E2023-087)** and provides only the platform and custody.
- **Vancelian is NOT responsible for repayment of capital or payment of interest.**

### Operational pillars
1. **Land management** — sourcing and securing strategic plots
2. **Construction** — building with quality, sustainability and efficiency
3. **Renovation** — revitalising existing properties to contemporary luxury standards
4. **Design & Furniture** — interiors with noble materials and bespoke furniture

## FAQ
**Q: Who actually owes me my money in the Dubai Villa Al Barari offer?**
A: Solaria (RCS Antibes 908 978 893). It alone is responsible for repaying capital and paying interest. Vancelian provides the platform and custody but is not financially responsible.

**Q: Has Solaria completed projects before this one?**
A: Yes — 20+ projects internationally, €50M+ invested, with projects in Tulum (Mexico), Cannes (France), Bali (Indonesia), Dubai and Niseko (Japan). Average operation duration is 24 months.

**Q: When was Solaria founded?**
A: **December 2021** (per the official Solaria brochure — earlier Vancelian FAQ wording of "2022" has been corrected).
