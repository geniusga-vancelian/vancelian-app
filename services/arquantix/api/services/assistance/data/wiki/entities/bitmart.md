## Summary

BitMart is Vancelian's secondary liquidity provider, handling 35% of transactions (15% of total volume). It was the first platform to list the AKTIO token. Recognized by CoinGecko for crypto trading education, it features multi-layer security infrastructure, enhanced account protection, and regular audits.

## Details

### Role
- Secondary execution venue for Automata France's own-account interposition trades
- **Transaction share**: 35% of transactions, 15% of global volume
- Strategic backup to Scrypt and primary execution partner

### AKTIO Listing
- **First-mover advantage**: BitMart was the first platform to list the AKTIO token
- Makes BitMart a strategic venue for AKTIO trading and liquidity
- Supports ecosystem development and token discoverability

### Security & Infrastructure
- **Multi-layer security**: advanced technical infrastructure with multiple security layers
- **Enhanced user account protection**: robust account safeguards and access controls
- **Regular audits**: internal controls and regular security audits
- **Industry recognition**: recognized by CoinGecko for crypto trading education initiatives

### Selection & Execution
- **Selection criteria**: sufficient daily transaction volume for operational liquidity on relevant crypto pairs
- **Best execution**: orders routed to achieve competitive pricing and execution quality
- **Compliance**: aligned with Automata France's execution policy

### Fallback & Redundancy
- In case of BitMart's unavailability, volume is redistributed to remaining liquidity providers (primarily Scrypt)
- Redistribution maintains execution quality and operational continuity
- Ensures client orders are fulfilled without significant delays
