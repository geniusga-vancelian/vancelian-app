# Vancelian glossary

## Short answer

This glossary defines key Vancelian financial and crypto terms including rates (fixed vs. variable), regulatory designations (DASP/AMF), payment methods (Visa card), and vault mechanics. Use this reference when encountering technical or financial language on the Vancelian app or help center.

## Details

### Exclusive offers & investment terms (normalized vocabulary)

These 7 terms have precise definitions at Vancelian. They must never be used interchangeably — each describes a distinct concept.

**Commitment period** (FR: Durée d'engagement)
The total duration during which your capital is locked. Fixed at the time of deposit. Known in advance. The Vancelian app displays the exact remaining commitment before each deposit. Example: Cloud Mining = 48 months per deposit; Dubai Villa = until 1 May 2027; Future Vault = 12 months.

**Maturity date** (FR: Date d'échéance)
The date when the project ends and all investors' capital is returned. Fixed for the entire project regardless of individual entry date. Example: Dubai Villa matures on 1 May 2027 — whether you entered in October 2025 or March 2026, you receive your capital at the same date. Not all products have a maturity date (Cloud Mining has a per-deposit commitment instead).

**Exit window** (FR: Fenêtre de sortie)
A semi-annual period (typically May and November, lasting 2 weeks) during which investors can submit requests to exit early or enter (via freed capital). Exit windows are a scheduled mechanism — they open regardless of whether the collection is open or closed. See [[how-do-project-exit-windows-work]] for full details.

**Early exit right** (FR: Droit de sortie anticipée)
The right for an investor to leave a project before the commitment period ends. Exercised during an exit window. **Conditional**: requires a new incoming investor to take over the exiting capital. Subject to the early exit fee if applicable, and to a minimum lock-up (typically 6 months from deposit).

**Early exit fee** (FR: Frais de sortie anticipée)
The fee applied when exercising the early exit right before a specified threshold. Typically: **5% before 24 months, 0% after 24 months.** This fee applies only when the early exit right is exercised — it does not apply at maturity.

**Collection status** (FR: Statut de la collecte)
Whether a project is currently accepting new direct deposits. Two states: **Open** (funding cap not reached, deposit anytime) or **Closed/Completed** (cap reached). When closed, new deposits are only possible during a deposit window following an exit window, if exiting investors have freed up capital. The deposit window operates first-come, first-served. Important: a closed collection does NOT mean the project is discontinued or that exit windows stop — existing investors continue to receive returns and can exit during windows.

**Committed capital** (FR: Capital engagé)
Capital that is currently locked in an investment and cannot be withdrawn outside the normal maturity or early exit process. Capital is committed until: (a) the maturity date is reached, OR (b) the investor successfully exercises their early exit right and a new investor takes over. Once exit is validated, the capital is no longer committed.
