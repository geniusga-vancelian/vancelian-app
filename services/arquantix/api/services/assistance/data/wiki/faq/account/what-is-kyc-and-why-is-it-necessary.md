# What is KYC and why is it necessary?

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### **What does the KYC verification consist of?**
From the application, you will be required to provide:

- A **valid identity document**

- A verification **video** to complete this step

**This procedure can only be completed through the mobile application.**

**How does the verification work?**

This entirely digital and automated system is based on advanced technology that verifies:

- The legitimacy of your **identity document**

- The **match** between your document and your facial recognition video

These checks are carried out by our partner.

**Why is this step mandatory?**

Without completing this step, you will not be able to access the main features of the application.

Although the procedure may seem strict, it complies with current **regulatory obligations, **necessary to** protect all our users.**

It is also important to note that regulations evolve regularly.

As a result, we may contact you in the future to update your information or request additional documents.
