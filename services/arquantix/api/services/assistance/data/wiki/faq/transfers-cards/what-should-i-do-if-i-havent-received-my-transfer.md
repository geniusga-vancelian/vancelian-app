# What should I do if I haven’t received my transfer?

## Short answer
Transfers made to your Vancelian account are processed through the SEPA network. We do not accept SWIFT transfers, payments made in a currency other than euro (EUR), or transfers made via PayPal.

## Details
Transfers made to your Vancelian account are processed through the **SEPA** network.

We do not accept **SWIFT** transfers, payments made in a currency other than **euro** (EUR), or transfers made via **PayPal.**

Any transfer that does not meet these conditions will be automatically rejected, and the funds will be returned to the issuing bank.

 

#### **What are the processing times for a SEPA transfer?**

Vancelian only supports standard SEPA transfers.

Instant transfers may still be processed within the same timeframe as standard transfers (typically 1 to 3 business days, and in some cases up to **5 business days,** depending on interbank processing times).

Transfers made on Friday afternoons, or before a weekend or public holiday, may appear with a slight delay.

If this timeframe has not yet passed, please wait until the end of the processing period before taking further action.

 

#### **What if the transfer does not appear in my account?**

If, after checking in the app, the transfer does not appear either in your balance or as “pending”, we recommend first contacting your issuing bank to confirm:

- That the transfer was sent in EUR via the SEPA network

- That the bank details (IBAN and account holder name) exactly match those of your Vancelian account

- That the transfer has not been blocked, rejected, or returned by your bank (some institutions may require additional approval)

#### **When should I contact Customer Support?**

If your bank confirms that the transfer was successfully sent and more than 5 business days have passed, please contact our Customer Support team for verification.

To help us process your request, please include the following information:

- Date and time of the transfer

- Exact amount sent

- Name of the issuing bank

- IBAN of the sender

- Full proof of transfer (in PDF format), including all transaction references (ID, number, or operation code)

- [Contact Customer Support](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

Our team will review the situation and get back to you as soon as possible.
