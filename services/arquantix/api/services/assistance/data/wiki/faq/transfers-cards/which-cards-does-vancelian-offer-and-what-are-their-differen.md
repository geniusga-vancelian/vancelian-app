# Which cards does Vancelian offer and what are their differences?

## Short answer
Vancelian offers two types of payment cards, each available in two colours: black or white. These cards have the same features and the same limits; only their design and cost differ.

## Details
Vancelian offers **two types** of payment cards, each available in **two colours**: black or white.

These cards have the same features and the same limits; only their design and cost differ.

**Benefit for Platinum and Elite members**

Platinum and Elite members receive their **first Metal card for free.**

Only the delivery fees (€9.90) remain at the customer’s expense.

For any renewal or additional order, the standard price of €49.90 will apply.

To learn more about the statuses and benefits of the Privilege Club, please [consult this section.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club)

**Are there any differences between the cards?**

There is no functional difference between the two types of cards.

The limits, fees and benefits depend exclusively on your loyalty level (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum or Elite), and not on the card type you choose.

You can find all details on [card limits and fees here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/12671439479953-Limits)
