# How can I set up recurring deposits in the vaults?

## Short answer
Recurring deposits allow you to automatically fund your Flexible Vault or Future Vault on a schedule you choose (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly). You must first complete your investment profile to activate this feature. Once set up, deposits process on your chosen schedule and begin generating returns the same evening or next day.

## Details
Recurring deposits allow you to automatically fund your Flexible Vault or Future Vault according to a frequency you choose.

To activate a recurring deposit, you must first [complete your investment profile.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39596990004241-How-do-I-complete-my-investment-profile)

Here is how to configure them directly from your application.

**How to activate a recurring deposit in a vault?**

- Open the **vault** concerned (Flexible or Future)

- At the top of the page, under the vault image, click on **Deposit**

- By default, the selected option is **Instant deposit**

- Click on the **menu icon** (bottom left) then select **Recurring deposit**

**Set up your deposit:**

- Deposit **amount**

- Funding **source**: EUR account or USDC wallet

- Start** date**: “Now” or manual selection via the calendar

- **Frequency:**

- Every day

- Every week

- Every 15 days

- Every month

- Click **Verify** to display the summary: start date, frequency and any fees

- Click **Confirm** to validate your recurring deposit

**If you activate an immediate deposit, a confirmation will appear in your vault:**

The deposit will be processed and will begin generating returns the same evening or the following day depending on the execution time.

**Where can I find my recurring deposits?**

You can view all your automations from several places:

**From the home screen:**

- Click on **More**

- Then select **Automations**

- You will find the full list of your recurring deposits

**From a vault:**

- Scroll down the page

- Access the** Recurring deposits** section

**Modification, suspension, or cancellation of a recurring deposit**

The options for modifying, suspending, or cancelling a recurring purchase are detailed in our dedicated article: [How do I set up a recurring purchase (DCA) in the Vancelian application?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/34653043561617-How-to-set-up-a-recurring-purchase-DCA-in-the-app)

You will find all the information regarding editable parameters, the management of missed executions, and the temporary suspension of an automation.
