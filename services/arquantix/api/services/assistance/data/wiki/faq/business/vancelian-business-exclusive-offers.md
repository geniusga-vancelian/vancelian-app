# Vancelian Business — Exclusive Offers

## Short answer
Vancelian Business clients can access the same **Exclusive Offers** as retail investors, with current rates **up to 11.1% APR**, paid **daily**. Each offer has a **commitment period between 18 and 48 months** depending on the project (Dubai Villa Al Barari: 18 months; Bali villas / Cloud Mining: 48 months). Returns are **not compounded** (paid out daily, not reinvested into capital). Capital is subject to project-specific exit windows and the standard digital asset risk.

## Details

| Feature | Value |
|---|---|
| Current top rate | up to **11.1% APR** |
| Compounding | **No** (daily payouts) |
| Earnings | Daily |
| Interests withdrawal | 24/7 |
| Deposit & withdrawal fees | **Free** |
| Commitment period | **18 to 48 months** (depends on project) |

### Available offers
- **Dubai Villa Al Barari** — 18-month commitment, APR 10.7%–11.5% by tier (until 1 May 2027). See [[../exclusive-offers/how-does-the-dubai-villa-al-barari-exclusive-offer-work]].
- **7 Luxury Villas in Bali** — closed since 6 October 2025 at €4M cap; existing investors continue to receive 10.2%–11% APR for the remaining 4-year term. See [[../exclusive-offers/what-is-the-7-luxury-villas-in-bali-exclusive-offer]].
- **Cloud Mining by Hearst** — 4-year commitment, deposits via twice-yearly windows, APR depends on tier. See [[../exclusive-offers/how-does-the-exclusive-offer-cloud-mining-by-hearst-infrastr]].

### Selection process
Each Exclusive Offer is **selected by Vancelian's Investment Committee** following due diligence on legal documentation, market study, financial projections and the project counterparty.

## Caveats
- **No capital guarantee.** Each offer is operated by a dedicated counterparty (SPV) (e.g. Solaria for Dubai, THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS for Bali) which alone bears the debt and the obligation to repay capital and interest. Vancelian is the regulated platform — see [[risk-warning-summary]] and [[../exclusive-offers/project-sponsor-responsibilities-al-barari]].
- Returns are **paid out daily, not compounded** — corporate treasurers should plan for active redeployment.
- Exit windows open twice a year and are matched against new investor demand (queue mechanism).
