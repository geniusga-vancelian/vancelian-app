# Where can I find the “Gainer” and “Loser” cryptoassets on the app?

## Short answer
A dedicated Gainers and Losers section shows trending crypto-assets updated hourly by algorithmic analysis. Access it from Crypto > Digital asset market, Products > Digital assets, or the home page Crypto section. Browse lists including All coins, My coins, Popular, Watchlist, Gainers, and Losers.

## Details
A dedicated section for rising (gainers) and falling (losers) digital assets is available on the app. There are several ways to access this information:

 

#### **1. From the Crypto section**

- Tap Crypto in the shortcuts at the bottom of the screen.

 

#### **2. From the Products section**

- Tap Products in the shortcuts at the bottom of the screen (on the right).

- Select Digital assets.

- Tap the title Digital asset market.

 

#### **3. From the home page**

- Tap Crypto (between Savings Projects and Crypto Baskets).

- Then select the Buy + button.

 

You will find several lists of cryptoassets available on the app, with an overview of their price and trend. **You can navigate between these lists:**

- All: all cryptos available for purchase on Vancelian

- My coins: trends of the cryptoassets you already hold

- Popular: the most purchased cryptos on the app right now

- Watchlist: your selected cryptoassets (how to add them to this list)

- Gainers: cryptoassets showing an upward trend

- Losers: cryptoassets showing a downward trend

**What is a rising or falling digital asset?**

Using our algorithmic analysis, we select a list of digital assets with upward or downward trends and make them available on the app.

This list is established based on market trend analysis and updated every hour according to a wide range of criteria defined and adjusted by our Algo traders.

 

#### **How is this defined?**
Our algorithmic analysis allows us to determine whether a digital asset is showing a downward, neutral, or upward trend in the short or medium term.

- **A rising digital asset is defined by:**

- An upward trend in the market

- The increase being measured in the short term

 

- **A falling digital asset is defined by**:

- A downward trend in the market

- The decrease being measured in the short term
