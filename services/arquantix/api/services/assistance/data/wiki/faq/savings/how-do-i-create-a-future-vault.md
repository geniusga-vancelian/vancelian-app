# How do I create a Future Vault?

## Short answer
To create a Future Vault, go to Savings Projects, tap the + icon, select Future Vault, accept the Terms and Conditions, name your project (and optionally add a photo), then make your first deposit in EUR or USDC. You can only create one Future Vault at a time, but you can make multiple deposits into it with the same locking and return conditions.

## Details
#### How to create a Future Vault from the app:

- From the main page, tap on Savings Project

- Tap the **+** in the top right corner and select Future Vault

- A window will appear with information about the Vault

- You must **accept** the Terms and Conditions

- Tap on Open a Future Project

- Give it a **name** of your choice, you can also personalise it with a **photo** now or later

- Tap on Make my first deposit

- Read the information window, then tap Accept and Deposit

- Choose whether to deposit in **EUR** or **USDC**, and enter the amount*

 

#### How many Future Vaults can I create?
You can only create one Future Vault at a time.

However, you can make multiple deposits into it, and you can personalise it at any time if your savings goal changes by clicking on the three dots in the top right corner, then selecting Customise.

 

#### Can I close my Future Vault?
If there are no funds left in your Vault, you can close your Future solution directly in the app.
Go to your Future Project, scroll to the bottom of the page and select Close this Future.
 

 

**There is no investment cap. However, a technical limit may apply per deposit or withdrawal depending on the amount. This will be shown at the time of the operation. You can make multiple transactions if needed.*
