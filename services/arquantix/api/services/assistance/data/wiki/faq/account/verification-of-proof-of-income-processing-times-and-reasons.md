# Verification of proof of income: processing times and reasons for refusal

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### **How long does the verification of my proof of income take?**
Each proof of income is manually reviewed by our dedicated team.

This process may take up to **3 working days**, depending on the volume of ongoing requests.

Once the verification is completed, the result will be communicated to you:

- By **notification** directly in your application

- By **email **sent to the address associated with your account

Thank you for your patience during this period.

**Why was my proof of income refused?**

Your document may be refused if one of the following criteria is not met:

- The document is not dated within the last 3 months

- The file is not part of the list of accepted documents

- The document format is not valid (only PDF, JPEG, JPG or PNG are accepted)

- The content of the document does not clearly show the required information

We invite you to consult the full list of accepted documents before submitting a new file:

[Consult the list of accepted documents.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/41602968269201-Which-documents-are-accepted-as-proof-of-income)

**Which documents are not accepted?**

The following documents cannot be used as proof of income:

- A statement of a single bank transaction

- A bank account detail slip (RIB)

- An employment contract

- An invoice

**How can I submit new proof of income?**

If your document has been refused, you can upload a new one directly from the application:

- Go to your **Profile**

- Access the **Account levels **section

- **Submit** your document

Make sure that the new document meets the [criteria indicated here ](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/41602968269201-Which-documents-are-accepted-as-proof-of-income)before submitting it.
