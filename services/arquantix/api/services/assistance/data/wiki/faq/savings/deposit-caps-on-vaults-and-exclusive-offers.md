# Are there deposit caps on the vaults and Exclusive Offers?

## Short answer
There is **no maximum deposit cap** on the Flexible Vault, the Future Vault or the Exclusive Offers. However, a **technical per-transaction limit** may apply to exclusive offers (typically tied to the remaining capacity of the collection). If your total desired amount exceeds this per-transaction ceiling, you can split it across several successive deposits.

## Details

### No global maximum
You are free to allocate as much as you wish, in aggregate, into:
- the **Flexible Vault** (no cap),
- the **Future Vault** (no cap),
- any **Exclusive Offer** currently open (no overall user cap).

### Per-transaction technical limit (exclusive offers)
When you tap **Deposit** on an exclusive offer and enter your amount, the app will display the maximum you can allocate in that single transaction if:
- your amount exceeds the **remaining capacity of the collection** (the offer's funding target), or
- an internal per-transaction technical ceiling applies.

In that case, simply confirm within the displayed ceiling and repeat the operation to reach your target total.

### Withdrawals — same principle
The same technical per-transaction limit may apply when you request a withdrawal from a vault or an Exclusive Offer. Break the withdrawal into several operations following the same logic as deposits.

## Caveats
- The per-transaction technical ceiling is visible in-app at the moment you enter the amount — we do not publish a fixed number because it varies with the remaining capacity of the offer.
- Deposit thresholds at the **account level** (linked to your KYC verification level) are a separate topic — see [[../account/how-to-raise-my-deposit-limits]].
