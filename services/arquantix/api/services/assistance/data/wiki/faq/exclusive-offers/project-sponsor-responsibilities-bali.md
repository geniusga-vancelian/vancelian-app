# Project counterparty & responsibilities — Bali villas

## Short answer
The 7 Luxury Villas project is operated by **THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS**, a French company created specifically for this project by **Gaël ITIER** (CEO of Vancelian) and **Julien HALIMI** (Head of RWA at Vancelian). It owns the land freehold via Indonesian subsidiaries and works with developer **Conceptive Construction** and architect **Eben Lontoh**. **The Heights Bali alone** is responsible for repaying capital and paying interest, bears the debt, and assumes project risk (best-effort obligation). **Vancelian (Automata France SAS)** is a **DASP registered with the AMF** and only provides the platform and custody — **Vancelian is not responsible for repayment of capital or interest**.

## Details
#### Who is The Heights Bali?

The Heights Bali is a French company (**THE HEIGHTS BALI SAS**) created specifically for this project by **Gaël ITIER** and **Julien HALIMI**, respectively **CEO** and **Head of RWA at Vancelian.** This setup ensures optimal coordination between both entities while maintaining a clear separation of financial responsibilities.

**For this 7-villa project in Bali, The Heights Bali operates through Indonesian subsidiaries that:**

- Own the land under freehold (full ownership)

- Manage relationships with renowned local partners (including French developer Conceptive Construction, architect Eben Lontoh, etc.)

- Oversee the construction of the villas

 

#### Responsibility Structure

Our two-tier structure ensures a clear division of roles and responsibilities:

**The Heights Bali:**

- Is solely responsible for repaying your loan and paying interest

- Uses the funds raised to develop the real estate project

- Bears the debt and assumes the associated project risks

- Has a best-effort obligation to secure your investment

**Vancelian (Automata France SAS):**

- Is a Digital Asset Service Provider (**DASP**) registered with the **AMF**

- Facilitates the investment through its platform

- Ensures custody of the digital assets

- Is NOT responsible for the repayment of capital or the payment of interest

 

*Full details of the structure can be found in the [Terms and Conditions](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers-tou/exclusives-offers-theheightsbali) you accept when making your investment.*
