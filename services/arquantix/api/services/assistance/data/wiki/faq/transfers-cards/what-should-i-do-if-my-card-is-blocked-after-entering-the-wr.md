# What should I do if my card is blocked after entering the wrong PIN?

## Short answer
If you enter an incorrect PIN or CVV three times, your card will automatically be blocked for security reasons. This block is temporary and can be lifted directly from your Vancelian app.

## Details
If you enter an **incorrect PIN or CVV three times**, your card will automatically be blocked for security reasons.

This block is **temporary** and can be lifted directly from your Vancelian app.

**How to unblock your card:**

- Go to the **Card** section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Select **Unblock PIN/CVV**

- **Confirm** the action when prompted by the app

- Enter the **verification code** received by SMS to complete the unblocking

Once this step is validated, your card will be immediately reactivated and usable again.

**Good to know**

Make sure you are using the correct PIN ([you can retrieve it in the app if needed](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8668942842769-How-can-I-view-or-change-the-PIN-of-my-card)).

If another incorrect attempt is made, the block will automatically be reactivated to protect your account.

If your card still appears blocked after this process, check the other possible reasons for payment refusal in [this article.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/12765670796305-Why-was-my-card-payment-declined-or-marked-as-pending)
