# What are the documents accepted as proof of residence?

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### Accepted documents as proof of address:

- Water, gas, electricity, internet and landline phone bills only 

- Home insurance certificate

- Government-issued documents (property tax, tax notice, etc.)

*Please note that Tax notices are issued once a year and are therefore valid for one year from the issuance date*

- Only bank statements issued within the last 3 months showing transactions confirming regular use of the account
*Confirmation of account opening, charges statements, balance statements, IBAN, bank correspondence are not accepted as PoA.*

- Residenical rent receipt / landlord's rent receipt not older than 3 months

**Please note that your document must be dated within the last 3 months (except for Tax notices) and submitted in PDF, JPEG, JPG or PNG format*. Screenshots of documents are not accepted.**
 

#### We cannot accept the following documents:

- Bank statement or account summary from a provider not affiliated with a banking group (e.g. N26, Wise etc.)

- Confirmation of account opening, charges statements, balance statements, IBAN, bank correspondence

- Mobile phone bill

- Insurance certificates other than home 

- Insurance-related invoices, letters or correspondence

- Deferred invoices

- Promotional emails

- Payslips
 

**To upload a photo or file, please ensure that you have authorised our app to access your camera and files.*

***Your document may be rejected if it exceeds 10MB or is not in the correct format.*

 

Any falsified document will result in immediate account termination in accordance with our [Terms of Use.](https://vancelian.com/en/terms-conditions-vancelian-platform)
