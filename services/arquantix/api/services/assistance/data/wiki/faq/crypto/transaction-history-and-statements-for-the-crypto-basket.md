# Transaction history and statements for the Crypto Basket

## Short answer
View your Crypto Basket transaction history by scrolling to the Transaction history section in your basket and selecting the specific month to see deposits, withdrawals, and service fees. Click any transaction to view details and download a PDF statement.

## Details
#### **To view your transaction history:**

- Go to your **Crypto Basket**

- Scroll down to **“Transaction history”** and click on the **title **to see the details of deposits, withdrawals, and service fees within your Crypto Basket

Transactions are organised by **month.**

- Click on the **transaction** of your choice

- In the details, you can **download the transaction statement** in PDF format by clicking on the arrow to the right of **“Summary”**
