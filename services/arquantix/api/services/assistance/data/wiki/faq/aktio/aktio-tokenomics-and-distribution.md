# AKTIO tokenomics and supply distribution

## Short answer
AKTIO has a **capped maximum supply of 100,000,000 tokens**, distributed across three allocations: **32% Sale** (31,964,270 AKTIO), **22.3% Team & Partners** (22,275,730 AKTIO), and **45.8% Community & Operations** (45,760,000 AKTIO). Sale tokens vest over 21 months; Reserve tokens (Team & Partners + Community & Operations) release progressively over 60 months from Q1 2022.

## Details

### Overall supply breakdown

| Allocation | Amount | % of max supply | Release period |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sale** (ICO) | 31,964,270 AKTIO | 32.0% | 21 months |
| **Team & Partners** | 22,275,730 AKTIO | 22.3% | 60 months |
| **Community & Operations** | 45,760,000 AKTIO | 45.8% | 60 months |
| **Total** | **100,000,000 AKTIO** | **100%** | — |

### Aktio Coin Reserve
The **Reserve** (Team & Partners + Community & Operations = 68,035,730 AKTIO) is managed by Automata ICO Ltd and released over a **5-year (60-month) period** from Q1 2022. The Company does not commit to releasing the full Reserve; the potential release is capped to reassure market participants.

### Use of funds
Capital raised via the AKTIO Initial Offering was **loaned by Automata ICO Ltd to other members of the Automata Group** for development of the technology and ecosystem — covering app development, community-building, regulatory licensing and marketing.

## Caveats
- Figures reflect the **tokenomics as designed in the whitepaper (April 2022)**. The maximum capped supply (100M) and the Sale allocation (31.96M) are fixed; actual on-chain circulating supply may differ from the theoretical vesting schedule above.
- Reserve releases are discretionary and occur on a quarterly basis as needed.
