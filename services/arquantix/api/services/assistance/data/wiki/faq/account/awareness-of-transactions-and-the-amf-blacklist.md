# Awareness of Transactions and the AMF Blacklist

## Details
At Vancelian, the security of our users is a top priority.

As part of the fight against fraud and illegal financial practices, it is essential to remain vigilant when carrying out transfers, especially when interacting with third parties or service providers outside Vancelian.

**Why is vigilance important?**

Some platforms, companies, or financial services operate without authorisation and may appear on the blacklist of the** Autorité des Marchés Financiers (AMF).**

This list includes suspicious or fraudulent entities that have no approval to offer investment services.

Before making any transaction to a third party or another platform, we strongly recommend checking whether the recipient appears on this list.

[Consult the official AMF blacklist](https://www.amf-france.org/en/warnings)

**What are the risks if I send a transaction to an unauthorised intermediary?**

Sending a transfer to an unregulated entity may result in:

- A **total and irreversible loss** of the funds sent

- No possible **recourse** in case of an issue

- A high risk of **fraud or scam**

- The misuse of your personal data by a **malicious actor**

We remind you that any transfer initiated from your Vancelian account is final: **once processed, it cannot be cancelled or recovered.**

**How can I stay protected?**

To secure your transactions:

- **Verify** that the intermediary is authorised by a financial authority (AMF, ACPR, etc.)

- **Consult the AMF blacklist** before sending any funds

- **Be wary** of promises of very high returns, pressure to act quickly, or unusual requests

- Do not hesitate to [contact our customer support](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support) if you have any doubts before making a transaction

**Important reminder**

Vancelian cannot be held responsible for operations carried out towards unauthorised platforms.

We encourage all users to adopt a careful and informed approach to protect their capital.
