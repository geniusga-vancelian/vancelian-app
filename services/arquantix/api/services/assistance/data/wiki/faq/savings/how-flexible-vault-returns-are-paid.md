# How and when are Flexible Vault returns paid?

## Short answer
Returns on the Flexible Vault are credited **daily** directly into your Flexible Project. They are automatically reinvested (compounding your capital), but you can withdraw them at any time, and you can also spend your Flexible balance directly with the Vancelian card. The first return is credited the day after deposit; if your deposit lands after the daily distribution has been triggered, the first return arrives **two days later.**

## Details
You'll receive **daily returns,** which are deposited directly into your Flexible Project.

These returns are **withdrawable at any time,** just like your invested capital. Otherwise, they are **automatically reinvested,** increasing your interest-generating capital day by day.

You can also use your Flexible balance to make **payments directly with the Vancelian card.**

### Timing of the first return
*If you make a deposit into the Flexible solution, your returns will begin to be credited from the next day. This allows us time to collect your funds and invest them accordingly.*

*If your deposit is made after the daily interest distribution has been triggered, the first return will be credited two days later — this accounts for the time needed to collect and allocate your funds.*
