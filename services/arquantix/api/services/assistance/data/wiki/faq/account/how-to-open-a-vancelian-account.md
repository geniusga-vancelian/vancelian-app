# How do I open a Vancelian account? (step-by-step onboarding)

## Short answer
Opening a Vancelian account happens entirely from the mobile application and takes, in most cases, only a few minutes. The mandatory steps are: (1) download the app and register with your email and phone, (2) complete identity verification (KYC), (3) provide a proof of residence. A proof of funds is required only later, when you want to raise your deposit limits beyond the standard thresholds — not at account opening.

## Process

### Step 1 — Download the Vancelian app
Download the Vancelian application on the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). See [[how-can-i-download-the-vancelian-app]] for details and device requirements.

### Step 2 — Create your account
Open the app and follow the guided registration flow:
- Enter your **email address** and create a password.
- Confirm your **mobile phone number** with a one-time code (SMS).
- Read and accept the Vancelian terms and conditions.

### Step 3 — Identity verification (KYC) — mandatory
This step is **required** to activate your account and access any service. It is run inside the app with our partner Onfido.

Accepted documents:
- Passport
- National identity card
- Driving licence (card format only)
- Residence permit issued by an EEA country

You will be asked to scan your document (front and back if applicable) and take a short selfie or video. The result is usually returned immediately; in case of manual review, it may take up to **3 working days**. See [[how-can-i-complete-my-identity-verification-kyc-in-the-appli]].

### Step 4 — Proof of residence — mandatory
Upload a recent document confirming your home address (see [[what-are-the-documents-accepted-as-proof-of-residence]] for the accepted list and formatting rules).

### Step 5 — Activation and first deposit
Once KYC and proof of residence are validated, your account is active. You can make your first EUR deposit by bank transfer (SEPA) and start using the Vancelian products — savings vaults, crypto, or exclusive offers — within the standard deposit thresholds applicable to your account level.

### Step 6 — Proof of funds — only if you want to raise your deposit limits
A proof of funds (proof of income or origin of funds) is **not required to open your account**. It is required only later, when you want to raise your deposit limits beyond the standard thresholds. This is handled via a dedicated flow inside the app.

See [[how-to-raise-my-deposit-limits]] for the exact procedure and [[which-documents-are-accepted-as-proof-of-income]] for the list of accepted documents (payslip, tax notice, notarial deed, proof of share sale, crypto transaction statement, etc.).

## Indicative timelines
- Identity verification: immediate in most cases, up to 3 working days if manually reviewed.
- Proof of residence: reviewed by the compliance team, usually within 1–2 working days.
- First SEPA deposit: credited typically within 1–3 working days depending on your issuing bank.

Timelines are indicative and may vary with document quality and verification volumes.

## Caveats
- The account cannot be activated while KYC or proof of residence is pending.
- Customer support **cannot** fast-track or complete the KYC step on your behalf — the flow must be completed from the app.
- If a step appears stuck, see [[why-does-a-step-in-my-registration-appear-to-be-stuck]].
- If your identity verification is refused, see [[why-was-my-identity-verification-refused]].
- Vancelian does not accept US Persons — see [[us-persons-and-use-of-the-vancelian-app]].
- Your account level defines your deposit thresholds and available services; see [[what-are-the-account-levels]].
