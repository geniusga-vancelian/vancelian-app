# How do I complete my investment profile?

## Short answer
Your investment profile is essential to access Flexible or Future Vaults and helps Vancelian understand your financial objectives, situation, and risk tolerance. You complete it via a 9-question mandatory questionnaire that assesses your investor type. The profile is valid for 2 years and generates a suitability report confirming that our products match your profile.

## Details
At Vancelian, your investment profile is essential to access certain savings products such as **Flexible** or **Future Vaults.** It helps us better understand your objectives, financial situation, and risk tolerance, so that we can suggest a suitable strategy. Your investment profile is determined via our dedicated **questionnaire.**
 

#### Where can I find the questionnaire?
The questionnaire is accessible in several cases:

- When opening a Flexible or Future Vault:
Completing the questionnaire is **mandatory** to finalise the vault opening. It will be automatically presented to you at the time of opening.
See also: [How do I create a Flexible Vault ](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29321073557521-How-do-I-create-a-Flexible-Vault)and [How do I create a Future Vault?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29320752674705-How-do-I-create-a-Future-Vault)

- When depositing into an already opened Flexible or Future Vault:
If your investment profile is no longer valid or has not yet been completed, you will be asked to complete it to validate the operation.

- At any time from your account:
You can complete it in advance from the app:
Profile > Investment Profile

What does the questionnaire involve?

The questionnaire includes **9 mandatory questions** designed to understand your personal situation, investment objectives, and risk tolerance.

Each question is presented as a multiple-choice question. Before submission, you can review your answers to correct any mistakes.

**There are no right or wrong answers. The important thing is to answer honestly so that the document accurately reflects your situation.**

Once the questionnaire is submitted:

- It **cannot be modified**

- It remains **valid for 2 years**

**How can I access my investment profile in the app?**

From Profile > Investment Profile, you can view:

- The **title** of your profile, which reflects your investor type

- A **description** summarising your investment approach

- Remaining **validity period**

- Your **investment horizon**

- The **suitability report,** generated after completing the questionnaire
This regulatory document confirms the suitability between your profile and the products offered. It can be viewed and downloaded at any time.

You will also find a **recommended product allocation***, suggesting an ideal distribution between the different investment options offered by Vancelian, according to your profile.

 

**This allocation does not constitute personalised advice or investment advice under applicable regulations. You remain solely responsible for your own investment decisions.*
