# How does the Future Vault work?

## Short answer
The Future Vault is a **diversified pocket** with a 1-year commitment period: your funds are allocated across underlyings **defined upfront at the moment of deposit** (Mining, Exclusive Offers, EURC reserve). Each day, the pocket generates interest paid in EURC and automatically reinvested unless you withdraw them. Your capital is locked for 12 months, after which funds remain invested but are fully withdrawable. The distributed rate is fixed.

## Details
The Future Vault allows you to allocate your savings to responsible-themed investment products, with a **1-year commitment period.**

Each day, the pocket generates interest through the allocation **defined at the moment of deposit**, which Vancelian rebalances over time within the agreed framework. Our approach aims to diversify sources of return while maintaining an optimal balance between **performance and risk management.**

Your savings are allocated within the Future Vault as follows:

- Mining (or [Cloud Mining](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers))

- Our [Exclusive Offer](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers) Solaria (The Heights Bali is part of legacy allocations for pre-October 2025 positions only — new subscriptions no longer participate in this offer)

- A reserve in EURC (a stablecoin tied to the euro)

*The EURC reserve is **non-remunerated** — as a euro stablecoin under **MiCA**, EURC cannot be deployed in yield-bearing activities without explicit regulatory approval. The reserve ensures liquidity for deposits and withdrawals. See [[how-vault-liquidity-and-returns-work]] for the full vault mechanic.*

The **rate distributed to clients is fixed**, and small variations on the gross yield generated by the allocation are absorbed by Vancelian's intermediation margin — see [[vancelian-rate-smoothing-and-margin]].

You can view your Vault’s allocation at any time by going to your Future Vault and clicking on the three dots in the top right corner > **More details about the offer.**

The allocation history is also available in this section.

 

#### How do I deposit into the Future Vault?
You can make a deposit starting from **1 EUR** or approximately **10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**

If the deposit is processed instantly, please note that withdrawals from the Vault may require up to **12 hours** of processing time. You’ll receive an automatic notification once the operation is complete.

 

#### How will I receive the returns?
You’ll receive daily returns, paid directly into your balance in EURC.

These returns can be **withdrawn at any time.** If not withdrawn, they will be **automatically reinvested,** increasing the capital that generates interest each day.

You can also **spend your returns directly from the Future Vault using your Vancelian card.**

*If you deposit into the Future solution, your returns will begin accruing the next day. This allows us time to collect your funds and allocate them accordingly.*

*If your deposit is made after the daily return distribution time, the first return will be paid two days later. This delay ensures your funds are properly collected and made available.*

 

#### How does unlocking my deposits work?
Deposits into the Future Vault are **locked for 12 months.** Once unlocked, your funds will continue to generate returns, but the full amount becomes withdrawable at any time and is no longer subject to a commitment period.

All deposits made within the same month will be unlocked on the **1st day of the following month, one year later.**

*Example: If you make several deposits in December 2024, all those funds will be unlocked on 1 January 2026.*

 

#### Can I create more than one Future Vault?
No, you can only create **one** Future Vault.

However, you can make multiple deposits into the same Vault. Each deposit will follow the same locking and return conditions.

## Risks
The Future Vault is not a regulated savings account as understood in traditional finance. It is based on digital assets and subject to specific risks:
- The distributed rate is fixed at subscription, but the **underlying allocations carry their own risks** (Cloud Mining performance, Exclusive Offer counterparty, EURC peg). See [[are-there-any-risks-of-capital-loss]] for a full breakdown by component.
- The indicative rate may change between subscription windows — your subscribed rate is contractual for your engagement.
- **Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future performance.** The value of an investment can go down as well as up, and you may not recover the amount invested.
- Your capital is **locked for 12 months** — you cannot access it during the commitment period.
- For a detailed explanation of vault mechanics, see [[how-vault-liquidity-and-returns-work]]. For the rate-stability mechanism, see [[vancelian-rate-smoothing-and-margin]].
