# How to withdraw funds from the Crypto Basket

## Short answer
Withdraw from your Crypto Basket by clicking Withdraw, using the slider to select your amount in EUR or USDC, and confirming. Fees are indicative and displayed before confirmation; you cannot make other deposits or withdrawals while processing is underway.

## Details
#### **To withdraw the funds allocated to your Crypto Basket:**

- Click **“Withdraw”** on your Crypto Basket home page and select the **amount** you wish to withdraw by moving the displayed slider.

- Choose **EUR** or **USDC.**
You will see an **estimated amount,** the corresponding **percentage,** and the [applicable fees.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4419810818193-The-trading-fees)

- Once you have entered the desired amount, click **“Withdraw”** again at the bottom of the page to confirm.

Please note that while the withdrawal is being processed, you will not be able to make another withdrawal or deposit. You will need to wait until the withdrawal is fully completed.
