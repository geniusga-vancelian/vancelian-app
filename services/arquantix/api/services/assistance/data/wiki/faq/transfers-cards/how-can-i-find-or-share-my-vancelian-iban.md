# How can I find or share my Vancelian IBAN?

## Short answer
Your personal Vancelian IBAN is automatically assigned once your level 2 verification has been completed. It allows you to receive SEPA transfers directly into your Vancelian account from a bank account in your name.

## Details
Your personal Vancelian IBAN is automatically assigned once your [level 2 verification](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/7834100629265-Address-verification-all-you-need-to-know-about-processing-times-and-rejections) has been completed.

It allows you to receive SEPA transfers directly into your Vancelian account from a bank account in your name.

 

#### **If this is your first deposit**

When making your first deposit, the app will automatically prompt you to initiate a transfer from the home page.

Simply select your bank from the drop-down list — your IBAN and the transfer instructions will appear automatically.

This step confirms that the sending bank account matches your Vancelian account.

You can also consult [this article.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/28665769522321-Account-approval-via-bank-transfer)

 

#### **If you have already made your first deposit**

You can find your IBAN at any time from several sections within the app:

- From the home page, below the total balance, tap Deposit

- Then select Bank transfer

- Your bank details will appear with a copy icon on the right to easily retrieve your IBAN

 

- From the home page, tap More

- Then select IBAN

- Your bank details will appear with a copy icon on the right to easily retrieve your IBAN

 

- From your Profile (top left)

- In the section My Account

- Then select IBAN

Information to share with your bank

To make a transfer to your Vancelian account, please use the following details:

**ModulR FS Europe Ltd**

**6th floor**

**2 Grand Canal Square**

**Dublin 2**

SEPA transfers may take up to **5 business days** to be credited, depending on interbank processing times.

 

#### **Tip**

The copy icon next to your IBAN lets you quickly share it with your bank or anyone wishing to make a transfer to your Vancelian account.
