# Who are the founders of Vancelian?

## Short answer
Vancelian was founded by three French entrepreneurs from the worlds of **finance,** **technology,** and **investment.** **Their shared goal:** to make investment opportunities, once reserved for professionals, accessible to a wider audience through a rigorous approach and the use of blockchain technology.

## Details
Vancelian was founded by three French entrepreneurs from the worlds of **finance,** **technology,** and **investment.**

**Their shared goal:** to make investment opportunities, once reserved for professionals, accessible to a wider audience through a rigorous approach and the use of blockchain technology.

[Gaël Itier – CEO & Founder](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gaelitier/?locale=en_US)

A graduate engineer from the École Polytechnique in Sophia Antipolis, Gaël Itier specialised in applied mathematics for finance and algorithmic trading.

After starting his career at BNP Paribas Investment Banking, he contributed to the digital transformation of the banking and wealth management sectors.

Winner of the BFM/Saxo Bank “Talents du Trading” competition in 2014, he launched his first company in 2017 with the ambition of democratising investment through blockchain technology.

With solid experience in both real estate and crypto-assets, he now applies his expertise at Vancelian to build a more accessible, transparent, and efficient investment model.
 

#### [Jean-Yves Guillou – CIO & Co-Founder](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jean-yves-guillou-12b58a19/?locale=en_US)
Holder of a Master’s degree in Market Finance from the Paris School of Business, Jean-Yves Guillou began his career with the Arkéa Group (ProCapital) before joining the strategic research firm BBSP in Paris, working alongside the Allan Howard fund (Brevan Howard – Switzerland).

A specialist in macro markets and Forex strategies, he has collaborated with major financial institutions such as Goldman Sachs, BlueCrest, and BlackRock.

His deep understanding of international markets and risk management brings Vancelian a structured and disciplined approach to asset selection and performance monitoring.
 

#### [Julien Halimi – Head of RWA Strategy & Co-Founder](https://www.linkedin.com/in/julien-halimi-45b768201/)
An entrepreneur driven by innovation and value creation, Julien Halimi brings over 20 years of international experience across the luxury, design, and high-end real estate industries.

Before joining Vancelian, he led numerous projects between London, Dubai, and New York, combining aesthetic precision, operational rigour, and collaborations with renowned architects and designers.

Multilingual and rooted in a multicultural vision, he draws on this global experience to strengthen Vancelian’s development and international presence.

As Head of Real World Assets, he leads Vancelian’s expansion strategy into tokenised real-world assets, particularly in the real estate sector. His mission is to democratise access to a new generation of rare, tangible, and high-performance investments.

 

**At Vancelian, we rely on an agile structure that brings together experts in key fields; market finance, software development, customer experience, and compliance, supported by a team of **50 dedicated professionals** committed to excellence every day.**
