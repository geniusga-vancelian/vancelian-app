# How do I freeze my card?

## Short answer
Blocking your card immediately stops it from being used in case of loss, theft, or suspected fraud. This action prevents any new payment or withdrawal from being authorised.

## Details
Blocking your card immediately stops it from being used in case of **loss, theft, or suspected fraud.**

This action prevents any new payment or withdrawal from being authorised.

**Steps to block your card**

- Go to the **Card** tab (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- In the shortcuts under the image of your card, tap **Freeze card**

Once the card is blocked, its visual will appear **frozen**, indicating that it is deactivated.

From that moment, no new payment can be made with this card.

**Important:**

Payments already authorised before the block cannot be cancelled and will still be charged.

**How to unblock my card**

You can unblock your card at any time from the same section by tapping **Unfreeze card.**
