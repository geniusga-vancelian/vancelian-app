# What is the exclusive offer: Dubai Villa – Al Barari?

## Short answer
The Dubai Villa – Al Barari offer lets you finance the acquisition and renovation of Villa A22 (5 bedrooms, expanding from 693 m² to 1,142 m²) in The Nest, Al Barari's exclusive Dubai development. You deposit in EUR or USDC, which lock in BTC value at your entry rate (eliminating crypto volatility), and earn 10.7%–11.5% APR (indicative, depending on Privilege Club tier), paid daily in EURC. Estimated asset sale January 2027.

## Details
The exclusive Dubai Villa – Al Barari offer allows you to participate in the financing of a **high-end real estate project in Dubai.**

It involves the acquisition and complete transformation of a **5-bedroom luxury villa,** located by the lake in The Nest, the most** exclusive development** of Al Barari.

**Who is who on this project.** "Al Barari" is the **location** — an exclusive district of Dubai where the villa stands. The **borrower** on the BTC loan is **Solaria**, the SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) that carries out the acquisition and renovation of the asset. In short: you lend in BTC to Solaria, which develops a villa located in Al Barari.

The financing takes the form of a **BTC loan.** The funds raised will be used to finance the architectural transformation and enhancement of this exclusive villa, **to be delivered for the 2026–2027 season.**

Deposits in this offer can be made in** EUR** or **USDC** and are converted into **BTC at the EUR value** at the time of the operation to avoid BTC fluctuation risk during the project.

In return, you will benefit from a net annual yield starting from** 10.7%*,** depending on your[ loyalty level.](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships)

Profit distribution is made in **BTC automatically converted into EURC** to protect against volatility, at the value on the day of the transaction.

This project relies on **experienced and certified** local partners specializing in the luxury real estate market in Dubai, to ensure proper execution of the works and the enhancement of the property.

You will find more details in the brochure attached to this article.
