# Who advises Vancelian on French and European law?

> **Regulatory escalation:** the chatbot must escalate any specific legal question to Vancelian support — it does not summarise legal advice on behalf of the firms.

## Short answer
Vancelian is supported by two complementary external legal partners: **D&A Partners** in France (financial and digital-asset law, PSAN structuring, tax), and **De Gaulle Fleurance** in Europe (MiCA, ESMA / EBA dialogue, cross-border passporting, CASP authorisation). Together with the internal compliance function, they form Vancelian's regulatory and legal framework.

## Details

### Why two legal partners?
The two firms cover complementary scopes:
- **D&A Partners (France)** — operational French legal anchor: tax, financial law, PSAN structuring, day-to-day compliance support.
- **De Gaulle Fleurance (Europe)** — strategic European institutional support: MiCA, ESMA / EBA dialogue, cross-border passporting, CASP authorisation.

Together, they give Vancelian both **local legal robustness** and **European institutional credibility**.

---

### D&A Partners — French legal counsel

**Website**: https://dnapartners.fr/en/

D&A Partners is a French law firm specialised in financial law and digital-asset regulation, recognised as one of the first cabinets to structure PSAN registrations with the AMF.

**Scope of work for Vancelian**:
- Legal and tax structuring of Vancelian's French entities.
- PSAN registration and ongoing compliance with the AMF framework.
- Tax analysis for Vancelian products (Cloud Mining, Vaults, Exclusive Offers, AKTIO).
- Contractual review of client-facing terms (CGU, CGUPM) and commercial documentation.
- Support for the PSAN → CASP transition under MiCA.

**Value to Vancelian**: secures the legal and tax structuring of Vancelian in France, from inception through MiCA preparation.

---

### De Gaulle Fleurance — European and international counsel

**Website**: https://www.degaullefleurance.com

De Gaulle Fleurance is a European business law firm with a recognised practice in financial regulation, digital assets, and innovation.

**Scope of work for Vancelian**:
- Strategic accompaniment on the PSAN → CASP transition under MiCA.
- Dialogue with French and European authorities (AMF, ESMA, EBA) during the authorisation process.
- Cross-border structuring and international deployments (including UAE / ADGM).
- Alignment of Vancelian's product framework with MiCA, DORA, and TFR requirements.

**Value to Vancelian**: secures the institutional dialogue toward CASP authorisation and gives Vancelian access to European regulatory discussions.

---

### Strategic positioning — the compliance framework
Three pillars combine to form Vancelian's regulatory and legal framework:
1. **Internal compliance function** — guarantor of the AML/CFT dispositif and AMF rule compliance (see [[vancelian-compliance-team|Vancelian's compliance team]]).
2. **National legal counsel** — D&A Partners, French financial law, PSAN and tax expertise.
3. **European institutional counsel** — De Gaulle Fleurance, MiCA and European regulatory expertise.

This framework gives Vancelian:
- **Local robustness**: French legal and fiscal anchoring via D&A Partners.
- **European reach**: MiCA alignment and regulator dialogue via De Gaulle Fleurance.
- **Anticipation**: the ability to adapt products to upcoming regulations (MiCA, DORA, TFR) ahead of deadlines.

### What this means for clients
- **Legal security** — every offer (Cloud Mining, Vaults, Exclusive Offers) is legally validated before launch.
- **Regulator dialogue** — Vancelian has access to the AMF, ESMA, and EBA through its advisors.
- **Regulatory anticipation** — Vancelian adapts its products to upcoming regulations (MiCA, DORA, TFR) ahead of deadlines.
