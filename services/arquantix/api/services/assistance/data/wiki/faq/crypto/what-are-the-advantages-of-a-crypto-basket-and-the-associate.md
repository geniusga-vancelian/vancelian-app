# What are the advantages of a Crypto Basket and the associated risks?

## Short answer
It’s a smoother and more efficient way to accumulate multiple cryptocurrencies at once — a clear advantage for investors looking to build a long-term reserve in digital assets.

## Details
#### **What are the advantages of a Crypto Basket compared to buying individual assets?**

- Instant diversification: several assets purchased in a single transaction.

- Simplified management: no need to manually track each market.

- Automatic rebalancing: the allocation strategy is maintained regularly.

- Full transparency on fees.

It’s a smoother and more efficient way to accumulate multiple cryptocurrencies at once — a clear advantage for investors looking to build a long-term reserve in digital assets.
 
#### **What are the associated risks?**
Crypto Baskets are subject to the **volatility** of the digital asset market. Their value may rise or fall rapidly.
