# What is Vancelian token locking?

## Short answer
Token locking lets you lock AKTIO coins for 3 or 12 months to earn loyalty points that unlock Privilege Club benefits. Locking for 3 months earns 2 points per token; 12 months earns 4 points per token. Your coins are frozen during the period and cannot be unlocked early.

## Details
Token locking allows you to lock your AKTIO coins for a set period in order to earn loyalty points.

You can choose to lock your AKTIOcoins for 3 months or 12 months, depending on your preference:

**Time**
3 months of locking
12 months of locking

**Points during locking**
2 points per token
4 points per token

**During the selected locking period:**

- Your AKTIO coins are frozen and cannot be used.

- It is not possible to unlock them before the end of the period.

- Once the locking period ends, the associated loyalty points will be automatically lost. To earn these points again, you will need to **lock your tokens once more.**

To learn more about loyalty points and their benefits, please visit our [Club Privilege FAQ](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/categories/29129169287057-Privilege-Cub) and also on [our website](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships)
