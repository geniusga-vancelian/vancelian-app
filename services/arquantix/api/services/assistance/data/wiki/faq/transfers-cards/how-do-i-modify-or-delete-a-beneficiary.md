# How do I modify or delete a beneficiary?

## Short answer
You can manage your beneficiaries directly from the Vancelian app.

## Details
You can manage your beneficiaries directly from the Vancelian app.

#### Steps to follow:

- From the home screen, tap** "More"** in the four options below your total funds.

- Select **"Beneficiaries"**.

- Choose the beneficiary you want to modify or delete.

- Tap **"Edit"**.

- Depending on your need:

- Make the necessary changes (e.g., correct a name or IBAN), then confirm.

- Select **"Delete beneficiary"** at the bottom of the page to remove them from your list.

 

#### **Always verify your beneficiary information:**

Before making a transfer, it is important to check that the information provided is correct, especially the beneficiary’s name. Make sure to enter it exactly as it appears on the recipient’s bank account details.

For more information, see our dedicated article: [What is beneficiary verification?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39614974441361-What-is-beneficiary-verification)
