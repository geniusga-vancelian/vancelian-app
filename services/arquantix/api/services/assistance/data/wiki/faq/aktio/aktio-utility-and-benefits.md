# What is AKTIO used for? Utility and benefits

## Short answer
AKTIO is a **utility token** that gives holders access to the **Privilege Club loyalty programme** in the Vancelian application. Holding or locking AKTIO earns **Loyalty Points** that help clients progress through the Privilege Club tiers (Bronze → Elite), which unlock better savings APYs, lower trading fees, free metal cards and higher referral commissions.

## Details

### What AKTIO is today — a loyalty utility inside the Privilege Club
The single most important thing to understand about AKTIO today is that its **primary utility is to accelerate your progression through the Privilege Club**, the loyalty programme embedded in the Vancelian application. Holding AKTIO in your Vancelian Digital Wallet — without locking or staking — allocates **Loyalty Points** in proportion to the volume held; this is the **AKTIO Holding Service**. On top of that, the **AKTIO Locking Wallet Service** lets you lock your AKTIO for **3 months or 12 months** to earn additional Loyalty Points, with the longer lock producing more points. Those Loyalty Points are what drive your tier progression from Bronze up to Elite — see [[privilege-club-tier-benefits]] for the full benefit table by tier.

Concretely, the tier benefits at Elite include Flexible Vault APY up to 6.43%, Future Vault APY up to 8.31%, trading fees as low as 0.25%, a free VISA Metal card, a referral commission up to 15%, and a set of free ATM and transfer benefits. The client case for holding AKTIO is therefore straightforward: it is a lever to reduce fees and lift yields across the rest of the Vancelian product line.

### Whitepaper-era utility (2022 design) — what the original design envisioned
The April 2022 AKTIO whitepaper described a broader set of utilities, designed for the original Akt.io platform. They included a **25% discount on exchange fees** when fees were paid in AKTIO through a settings-based autonomous fee payment, **higher deposit and withdrawal limits** for AKTIO holders, **access to staking in trading and yield features** (WealthBot, Vault), **peer-to-peer and scheduled payments** on the Ethereum network, and **frictionless payment within the ecosystem** through merchants and service providers accepting AKTIO as means of payment.

This context matters because it is still referenced in older materials, and it is important to state clearly that **since the rebrand to Vancelian in Q3/Q4 2024, the client-facing product lineup has evolved**. The authoritative expression of AKTIO utility today is the **Privilege Club** as described above. Legacy concepts — WealthBot, Wealth Card, the old tier names — are no longer applicable in the form they took in 2022.

### Why AKTIO matters for the platform as a whole
From a platform design perspective, AKTIO is doing three things simultaneously. It **accelerates adoption** by giving holders an incentive to actively use Vancelian's wealth and crypto services, rather than sitting passively on the app. It **creates fidelity and retention** through the Privilege Club, which aligns the long-term interests of users and the platform. And it **carries community value** as a decentralised asset with external secondary-market liquidity — AKTIO trades on **BitMart**, the only active external listing since 2025 (the original 2022 listing on Bittrex Global closed in 2023). This external liquidity is what gives AKTIO a public market price distinct from Vancelian's own internal pricing.

### AKTIO mobility inside the Vancelian app (EEA clients)
For EEA clients, the set of actions possible on AKTIO inside the Vancelian app is specific and worth stating precisely, because it differs from the other crypto-assets listed on the platform.

| Action | Available |
|---|---|
| **Buy** AKTIO in-app (EUR / crypto → AKTIO, market order) | ✅ |
| **Sell** AKTIO in-app (AKTIO → EUR / USDC / crypto, market order) | ✅ |
| **Deposit** AKTIO obtained externally (e.g. on BitMart) into the Vancelian app | ✅ |
| **Withdraw** AKTIO from the Vancelian app to an external wallet | ❌ Not supported |

In practice, the **exit route for EEA clients** is to sell AKTIO in-app against EUR, USDC, or another withdrawable asset, then withdraw that asset through the standard withdrawal flow. There is no direct AKTIO-to-wallet withdrawal route from the Vancelian app for EEA clients.

### Other important limitations worth knowing
Three additional nuances are worth flagging explicitly. First, Vancelian **cannot convert AKTIO directly into fiat in a single step** — the path is always `AKTIO → EUR/USDC` via the trading page, followed by a fiat withdrawal (or a USDC withdrawal if preferred). Second, the **ICO Launchpad → BitMart withdrawal route** is reserved exclusively for historical ICO investors **residing outside the EEA** who never had access to the Vancelian app — see [[how-to-withdraw-aktio-to-bitmart]] for the detailed flow. Third, the **legacy AKTIO Staking Service** has been **closed to new subscriptions since 21 October 2024**; current AKTIO utility is expressed through Holding and Locking inside the Privilege Club.

## Caveats
AKTIO is a **utility token**, not a share or a regulated financial investment product — it does not entitle the holder to dividends, voting rights, or any guaranteed return. Loyalty-point formulas and tier thresholds are set by Vancelian and can evolve over time; the Privilege Club page is always the authoritative reference for the current parameters.
