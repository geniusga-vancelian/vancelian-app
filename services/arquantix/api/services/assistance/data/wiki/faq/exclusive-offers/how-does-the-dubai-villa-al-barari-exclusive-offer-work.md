# How does the Dubai Villa - Al Barari exclusive offer work?

## Short answer
The offer has a **fixed maturity date of 1 May 2027** for all investors regardless of entry date, and is capped at **€11.6 million**. Minimum deposit is **1 EUR or ~10 USDC**, with daily payouts at an **APR between 10.7% and 11.5%** depending on your Privilege Club tier. Lock-up is **6 months minimum**. **Exit windows** open **twice a year** — if you wish to withdraw your committed capital before maturity, you can submit an exit request during these windows (5% fee if held less than 2 years, free after). The **collection** may be open (direct deposits accepted) or completed (cap reached) — check the app for current status. If completed, the collection reopens only when capital is freed by exiting investors via a deposit window.

## Details
**Who is who on this project.** The project is located in **Al Barari**, an exclusive district of Dubai. The **borrower** on the BTC loan is **Solaria**, the SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle) that carries out the acquisition and transformation of the villa. Your BTC is lent to Solaria; Solaria pays the interest funded by the development and eventual sale of the asset.

### The loan mechanic in one paragraph
This project is structured as a **BTC-denominated loan to Solaria** with a **EUR reference value locked at deposit**. When you deposit in EUR or USDC, your funds are converted to BTC at the current market rate, and that EUR amount is locked as the reference value that will be reimbursed at maturity — regardless of where the BTC price moves during the life of the loan. The **interest is generated in BTC** by Solaria and automatically converted into EURC, credited daily to your Available wallet. At maturity, your **capital is returned in BTC calculated on the basis of the initial EUR reference** — so you recover the same euro value initially invested. This is why the product is described as *"crypto-native, insulated from BTC volatility"*. For the full mechanic across all BTC Lending projects, see [[how-exclusive-offer-btc-lending-works]].

You can make a deposit in **EUR** or **USDC** starting from amounts detailed below.

You can make a deposit starting from **1 EUR** or approximately **10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**
 

#### What are the main features of this offer?

- **End date : **1st May 2027

- **Commitment period:** **You do not choose your commitment duration** — it ends on **1 May 2027** for all investors, regardless of joining date. The effective duration varies depending on when you enter (for example, 18 months for entry in October 2025, 17 months for November 2025, etc.). The Vancelian app shows you the exact remaining commitment before each deposit. The only way to recover your capital before this date is to exercise your right of **early exit via an exit window** — which requires a new investor to take over your position.

- **Offer limited** to 11,6 million euros.

- **Flexibility:** Minimum 6-month lock-up period. You have the option to deposit and withdraw twice a year.

- **Annual return (APR):** Between 10.7% and 11.5%, depending on your Privilege Club status. **Rates are indicative and may change — always verify the live rate in the Vancelian app.**

- **Payment frequency of returns:** Daily.

How does the investment cap work?

Each investor is free to choose the amount that suits them, within the available offer limit.

Investments are available starting from **1 EUR** when depositing in EUR, or approximately **10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**

 

#### Collection status and how to invest
The collection (collecte) can be in one of two states:

- **Open**: the funding cap (€11.6M) has not yet been reached. You can deposit directly via the app at any time — no need to wait for a window.
- **Completed / closed**: the cap has been reached. New deposits are only possible during a **deposit window**, which opens after an exit window closes, if exiting investors have freed up capital. The deposit window operates on a first-come, first-served basis and closes automatically once the freed amount is fully subscribed.

Check the offer page in the Vancelian app to see the current collection status.

#### Exit windows and early withdrawal
- **Exit windows** open **twice a year** (typically May and November), for 2 weeks.
- To be eligible, your funds must have been locked for at least **6 months**.
- Exit requests are matched against new incoming investors and processed first-come, first-served.
- At maturity (1 May 2027), your capital is returned — no exit window needed.

For more information, please refer to the [General Terms and Conditions of Use.](https://vancelian.com/en/terms-conditions)

 

#### Are there any fees associated with withdrawals?
After an initial commitment of 6 months, you will be eligible for privileged early exit conditions, subject to a **5% fee.**

Whether you choose to exit partially or fully from the offer, the exit must occur during the designated exit windows.
