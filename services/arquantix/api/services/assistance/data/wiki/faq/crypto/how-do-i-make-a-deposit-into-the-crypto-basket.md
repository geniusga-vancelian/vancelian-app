# How do I make a deposit into the Crypto Basket?

## Short answer
Add funds to your Crypto Basket anytime with a minimum deposit of 100 EUR or equivalent in USDC. Trading fees are indicative and vary by loyalty level, displayed during checkout before you confirm the transaction.

## Details
Once your Crypto Basket has been created, you can **add** funds to it.

The minimum required deposit is **100 EUR** or the **equivalent in USDC**, and[ transaction fees](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4419810818193-The-trading-fees) will be applied according to your loyalty level.

**To proceed:**

- From the home page, tap on **Crypto Basket**

- Select the **basket** you want to invest in (Top 2 or Top 5)

- Tap **Invest**

- Enter the **amount** you wish to deposit (in EUR or USDC)

- Tap **Review**

- Check the** transaction summary:**

- Deposit amount

- Estimated fees

-  Basket allocation

- Tap **Confirm** to validate your investment
 

A confirmation message will appear indicating that your deposit has been successfully registered. You will then be invited to configure the[** rebalancing frequency**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39641593607953-What-is-rebalancing) of your basket (monthly by default, adjustable in the app).

You can** add funds** to an existing Crypto Basket at any time.

Please note that while your deposit is being processed, you will not be able to make another deposit or a withdrawal. You must wait until the processing is fully completed.

 

#### **Set up a recurring deposit in a Crypto Basket**
If you would like to fund your Crypto Basket automatically at **regular intervals**, you can set up a **recurring deposit** directly from the application.

This feature allows you to schedule automatic deposits according to a defined amount and frequency, without having to perform the operation manually each time.

To learn how to configure it and see the available settings, please refer to the dedicated article:

[How do I set up a recurring deposit in a Crypto Basket?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39642732958609-How-do-I-make-a-deposit-into-the-Crypto-Basket)
