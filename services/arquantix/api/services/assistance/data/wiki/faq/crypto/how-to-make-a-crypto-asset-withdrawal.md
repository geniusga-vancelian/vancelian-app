# How to make a crypto asset withdrawal?

## Short answer
Withdraw crypto from Vancelian by completing KYC and proof of residence verification, then set up a two-factor authentication app. Select Send, choose your crypto and network (Ethereum ERC-20 or Base for USDC only), enter the destination wallet address, confirm the amount and fees, and approve via email and authenticator codes. Withdrawals take several hours to appear in your external wallet.

## Details
To withdraw cryptoassets from the Vancelian application, you must first complete the following steps:

- Level 1: Identity verification (KYC)

- Level 2: Residence verification

- Set up an Authenticator app (e.g. Google Authenticator or Microsoft Authenticator). You will find instructions [here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/19685868023313-Crypto-assets-withdrawal-How-to-set-up-my-Authenticator)

#### **Steps to withdraw cryptoassets:**

- From the** Home page**, tap **Send**

- Select Blockchain transfer

- Choose the **cryptoasset** you wish to withdraw

You can check the list of [supported cryptoassets here](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16581376806161-Can-I-withdraw-crypto-assets-from-the-Vancelian-application)

- Select the available network:
Ethereum (ERC-20): all cryptoassets on [this list](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16581376806161-Can-I-withdraw-crypto-assets-from-the-Vancelian-application) can be withdrawn via the Ethereum network
BASE: only available for USDC withdrawals

- Enter the destination wallet address
If you enter an incorrect address or a non-supported network, your funds will be permanently lost.

- Enter the **amount** you wish to withdraw

- Check the **transaction summary,** make sure the sender and recipient wallet addresses are correct, and then accept the terms before proceeding

- Validate the operation with **two-factor authentication:**

- Code received by **email**

- Code generated by your **Authenticator app**

- Tap Next to confirm

#### **Withdrawal fees :**

Withdrawals in cryptoassets are subject to execution fees, which include:

- **Variable network fees**

- An **estimate of the total fees,** displayed directly on the transaction page (on the button before validation)

- **Automatic security:** if the actual fees significantly exceed the initial estimate, the operation may be cancelled

Fees are deducted from the amount you wish to withdraw:

- It is not possible to make a withdrawal below the minimum fee amount.

- Additional fees may be charged by the receiving platform.

- Only **Ethereum (ERC-20)** and **BASE** (USDC only) networks are accepted.

- The minimum withdrawal amount and fees may be adjusted without prior notice depending on network congestion.

Processing times :

Depending on network activity, confirming a transfer on the blockchain may take **several hours** before appearing in the balance of your external wallet.
