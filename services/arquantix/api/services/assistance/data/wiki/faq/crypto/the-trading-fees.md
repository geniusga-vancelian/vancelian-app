# The trading fees

## Short answer
Trading fees on Vancelian are indicative and based on membership level: Bronze 0.95%, Silver 0.75%, Gold 0.665%, Platinum 0.45%, and Elite 0.25%. Your exact fee depends on your loyalty status, which you can upgrade in the app settings.
