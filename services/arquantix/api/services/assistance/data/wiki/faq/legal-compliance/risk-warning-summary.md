# Vancelian risk warning: what every investor must know

## Short answer
**Investing in digital assets through Vancelian carries the risk of partial or total loss of capital.** Vancelian does not provide personal investment advice, does not monitor whether a product fits your situation, and does not guarantee service availability or transaction reversibility. **Digital assets are NOT covered** by EU investor compensation schemes (Directive 97/9/EC) or deposit guarantee schemes (Directive 2014/49/EU). You alone are responsible for your investment decisions, your tax situation, and the security of your account credentials.

## Details

### Capital risk
Past performance does not predict future performance. The value of an investment and any returns can **go down as well as up**, and you may not recover the full amount you invested. Digital asset prices can change rapidly and without warning, including outside business hours — crypto markets trade 24/7, and sharp moves can occur at any time of day or night.

### Liquidity risk
Digital assets may have **limited liquidity**, which can make it difficult or impossible to sell or exit a position at the moment you want to — especially during periods of rapid price movement, when buy-side demand can dry up precisely when you most need to transact.

### Availability risk
Vancelian does **not guarantee** that the application will be available at any specific time, nor that there will be no service outages or network congestion. It may therefore be impossible to buy, sell, store, transfer or receive digital assets at the exact moment you wish to — this is a structural limit of any digital platform, not a Vancelian-specific caveat.

### No personal advice — you decide
Vancelian **does not provide personal investment, financial, trading, tax or legal advice**. Information published by Vancelian is factual or procedural — never advisory. **You alone are responsible** for deciding whether any investment fits your objectives, your financial situation and your risk tolerance. This point is worth being explicit about because the chatbot and support channels are not a substitute for a regulated advisor.

### No monitoring relationship
Vancelian is **not your broker, intermediary, agent or advisor**, and has **no fiduciary obligation** towards you. Vancelian does not check whether your activity is consistent with your financial goals — that monitoring is not part of the service.

### Tax responsibility
The tax treatment of digital assets is uncertain and depends on your country of residence. **You are responsible** for determining and paying any tax due on your activity on Vancelian. Where required by law, Vancelian will report your transactions and may withhold tax on behalf of the relevant authorities.

### Third-party risk
Vancelian relies on third parties — payment providers, custodians, banking partners such as Modulr. You may be subject to those third parties' own terms and conditions, and Vancelian may not be held responsible for losses caused by them.

### Security risk
It is impossible for Vancelian — or any platform — to eliminate all security risks. **You are responsible for the safety of your password and credentials**, and you may be held responsible for all transactions on your account whether you authorised them or not. Because **digital asset transactions are typically irreversible**, losses from fraud or unauthorised transactions may not be recoverable.

### Risks specific to digital assets
Digital assets themselves carry a set of specific risks that a client should understand before subscribing. Faults, defects, hacks, exploits, protocol failures or unforeseen events can affect any digital asset. Transactions are typically irreversible, so accidental or fraudulent transactions may be unrecoverable. A digital asset can also become technologically obsolete over time, and transactions may be delayed beyond their scheduled settlement date. Attack vectors known to affect digital assets include DDoS attacks, Sybil attacks, phishing, social engineering, hacking, smurfing, malware, double-spending, majority-mining and other consensus attacks, misinformation, forks, and spoofing.

### No EU investor protection — this is the single most important point
Digital assets held with Vancelian are **NOT covered** by EU investor protection schemes. Specifically, they are not covered by the **EU investor compensation schemes under Directive 97/9/EC** of 3 March 1997, nor by the **deposit guarantee schemes under Directive 2014/49/EU** of 16 April 2014. This is the fundamental difference between a crypto investment and a bank deposit — there is no scheme that reimburses you in case of insolvency.

### Currency, communication and legal risk
Three additional risk dimensions complete the picture. **Currency risk**: EUR/crypto exchange rate fluctuations affect your gains and losses directly. **Communication risk**: electronic communications can fail, be delayed, be intercepted, or fail to reach the intended destination. **Legal risk**: changes in laws and regulations can materially affect the value of digital assets, often unpredictably and differently across markets.

### Conflict of interest in Exclusive Offers
A conflict of interest arises when a personal interest interferes with a role within the organisation. Vancelian has identified specific situations in its Exclusive Offers that may present a conflict-of-interest risk and details them in the **Terms and Conditions of each relevant Exclusive Offer**. Investors should consult those T&Cs before subscribing to any Exclusive Offer.
