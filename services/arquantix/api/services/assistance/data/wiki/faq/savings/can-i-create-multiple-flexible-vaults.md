# Can I create multiple Flexible Vaults?

## Short answer
Yes — you can create up to **5 Flexible Vaults**, allowing you to organise your savings around different goals. You can rename or customise any vault at any time from the three-dot menu in the top right corner.

## Details
Yes, you can create up to **5 Flexible Vaults,** allowing you to manage your savings and goals more effectively.

You can personalise your Flexible Vault at any time if your savings goal changes by tapping the three dots in the top right corner, then selecting **Customise.**
