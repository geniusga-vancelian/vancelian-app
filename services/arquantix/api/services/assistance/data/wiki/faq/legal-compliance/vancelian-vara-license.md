# Is Vancelian licensed in Dubai / UAE?

## Short answer
Automata FZE, the Vancelian group entity in the UAE, received In-Principle Approval (IPA) from VARA in December 2025. The full licence is expected imminently and will enable the Vancelian Dubai app launch, offering crypto trading, custody, and investment services under VARA regulatory supervision.
## Details

### Entity and regulator
- **Entity:** Automata FZE (Dubai)
- **Regulator:** VARA — Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority (Dubai)
- **Legal counsel:** W3C
- **IPA obtained:** December 2025
- **Full license status:** imminent (as of April 2026)

### VARA-licensed services (upon full licence)
The VARA licence covers four service categories:

- **Broker Dealer** — custody and crypto exchange services
- **Management & Investments** — portfolio management and investment services
- **Lending & Borrowing** — crypto-backed lending and borrowing
- **Advisory** — investment advice on virtual assets

### Dubai app launch
The Vancelian Dubai app is planned for launch upon full licence obtainment. The app will provide UAE-based clients with access to crypto trading, custody, and investment services under VARA regulatory supervision.

### Relationship with the European entity
Automata FZE (Dubai) and Automata France SAS (France) are both 100% owned by Vancelian Group ADGM (Abu Dhabi holding). They operate under separate regulatory frameworks but share the same platform technology. European clients continue to be served by Automata France under its AMF PSAN registration; UAE clients will be served by Automata FZE under the VARA licence.

## Caveats
- Until the full VARA licence is officially granted, Automata FZE cannot offer regulated virtual asset services to the public.
- This page reflects the status as of April 2026. The VARA licence announcement is imminent — check vancelian.com for the latest.
- Regulatory matters are complex — for specific questions about your eligibility or jurisdiction, contact Vancelian support.
