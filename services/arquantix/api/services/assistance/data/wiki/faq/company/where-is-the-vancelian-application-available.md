# Where is the Vancelian application available?

## Short answer
To do so, we maintain registrations with various European regulators.  For this reason, we can only offer our services in countries within the **European Economic Area (EEA)***, which are not only part of the European territory but are also regulated as countries of the **European Monetary Union.** Our services are also available in the **Overseas Departments and Regions (DROM)****.

## Details
Vancelian is committed to providing the best possible experience for its clients.

To do so, we maintain registrations with various European regulators. For this reason, we can only offer our services in countries within the **European Economic Area (EEA)***, which are not only part of the European territory but are also regulated as countries of the **European Monetary Union.**

Our services are also available in the **Overseas Departments and Regions (DROM)****.

For certain territories located near the EEA, such as Andorra and Monaco, our services are not available as they are not part of the EEA.

We hope to expand our services to other territories outside the EEA in the near future. However, we are currently working on the legal aspects, and obtaining licences is a lengthy process.

 

***EEA countries:**Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Cyprus, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Iceland, Ireland, Italy, Latvia, Liechtenstein, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, Norway, Poland, Portugal, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, and Sweden.

*Due to regulatory reasons, our services are not available in the Netherlands.*

****Overseas Departments and Regions (DROM):**Guadeloupe, Martinique, French Guiana, Réunion and Mayotte.

*Please note: The Overseas Collectivities (COM) are not eligible for our services.*
