# My card has expired: how can I order a new one?

## Short answer
When your card approaches its expiration date, you will receive email and in-app notifications 30 days and 15 days before expiry. You can order a replacement card directly from the Card section in the Vancelian app.

## Details
When your card approaches its expiration date, you will be notified by **email and via an in-app notification** at the following times:

- **30 days **before the expiration date

- **15 days** before

- **The day** before the expiry date

**How to order a new card?**

As soon as you receive the notification, you can order your new card directly from your Vancelian application:

- Go to the **Card** section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Choose the card you prefer (PVC or Metal) and the desired colour (Black or White)
[*You can find all information about the different cards and their costs here.*](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8666567993745-Which-cards-does-Vancelian-offer-and-what-are-their-differences)

- Click** Order **to confirm your request

Your order will then be confirmed automatically.

Once your new card is activated, the old one will automatically be marked as **expired** in the application.

If you wish, you can **delete the expired card** from the same section, even if you are not ordering a new card.

**What are the delivery times?**

Your new card is delivered by Royal Mail Post and then forwarded by your national postal provider in your country.

As a reminder, the estimated delivery time is between **12 and 20 working days** from the validation of the order.

You will receive a notification and an email as soon as the card is ready to be dispatched.
