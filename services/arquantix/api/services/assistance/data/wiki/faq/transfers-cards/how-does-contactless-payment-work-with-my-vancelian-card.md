# How does contactless payment work with my Vancelian card?

## Short answer
Your Vancelian payment card allows you to make contactless payments anywhere VISA is accepted, within the limits of authorised merchants. What are the contactless limits?

## Details
Your Vancelian payment card allows you to make **contactless** payments anywhere VISA is accepted, within the limits of authorised merchants.

**What are the contactless limits?**

- Up to **5 consecutive payments** without entering your PIN

- Maximum amount per transaction: **25€**

Beyond these limits, **inserting the card into the terminal and entering the PIN will be required.**

**Tips and important points**

- If a contactless payment is **declined**, try inserting your card into the terminal and entering your PIN.

- Use your card regularly **with the PIN** to avoid declines related to security limits.
