# Address verification: all you need to know about processing times and rejections

## Short answer
Verifying your proof of address confirms your residential address and validates your Level 2 verification. This step is mandatory to use your account. Depending on the status shown in the app, your document may be under review or have been rejected.

## Details
Verifying your proof of address confirms your residential address and validates your **Level 2** verification. This step is mandatory to use your account.

Depending on the status shown in the app, your document may be **under review** or have been rejected.
 

#### **Why is my address verification still pending?**
If your document is marked as “**under review**”, this means our team is manually checking your document.

You will be notified by **e-mail** once the verification is complete.

This process can take up to 3 business days. If verification is still pending after 3 business days, please contact us at support@vancelian.com.

**Why was my proof of address rejected?**

If your document is rejected, the reason will be displayed directly in the app.

To ensure your document is accepted, please make sure it meets the following requirements:

Required conditions:

The address on the document must match the one provided during registration.

The document must:

- Be one of the [accepted document types](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/5161612703505-What-are-the-documents-accepted-as-proof-of-residence)

- Be in your **full name**

- Be dated within the last **3 months** (except tax notices, valid for 12 months)

- Be in PDF, JPEG, JPG, or PNG **format**

- Be **original** and **unmodified:**
→** Screenshots** are not accepted
→ **Converted** or **edited** documents (cropping, annotations, etc.) will not be accepted
 

#### **Which documents are refused?**

- Bank statements or account summaries issued by non-bank affiliated institutions (e.g., N26, Wise, etc.)

- Account opening certificates, fee statements, balance statements, RIB/IBAN

- Mobile phone bills

- Insurance certificates (except home insurance)

- Insurance-related correspondence

- Deferred invoices

- Promotional emails

- Payslips

Verification of your document may take up to** 3 business days**.
 

#### **Need Help?**
If you encounter any issues submitting or validating your document, please contact our team at support@vancelian.com.
