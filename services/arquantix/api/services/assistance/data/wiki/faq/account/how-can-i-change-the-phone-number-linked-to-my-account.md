# How can I change the phone number linked to my account?

## Short answer
For security reasons, changing your phone number cannot be done directly in the app. All requests must be sent to our customer support team so they can be processed manually.

## Details
For security reasons, changing your phone number cannot be done directly in the app.

All requests must be sent to our **customer support team** so they can be processed manually.

**How to request a phone number change?**

To modify the phone number associated with your account:

- Contact our **customer support team** by email at the following address: support@vancelian.com

- Attach to your message **a bill** corresponding to your new phone line

**What conditions must be met?**

The bill must:

- Be in your **full name** and match the **address** registered on your Vancelian account

- Be dated within the last** 3 months**

- Clearly state the **number** of the new phone line

- Be submitted in its **original format **downloaded directly from your operator’s website (no screenshots, photos, or modified documents will be accepted).

**Good to know**

- Prepaid numbers are not accepted.

- The number must be nominative and linked to a phone line registered in your name.

- Any request that is incomplete or does not meet the above criteria may be refused.

- The change will be applied manually by our team once the request is approved.

**How to reconnect after changing your phone number?**

Once your phone number has been updated by our team, you will need to reset your login in order to access your account.

**Here are the steps to follow:**

- On the app login page, click on **Forgot PIN** at the bottom of the screen

- Then select **Change account** at the bottom of the next page

- Click on **Log in**

- Enter your **new phone number**, then **confirm**

- You will receive a **verification code **by SMS on this number to finalise the login

**Without completing these steps, you will not be able to reconnect to your account using your new phone number.**

 

*Vancelian reserves the right to accept or refuse any phone number modification request.*
