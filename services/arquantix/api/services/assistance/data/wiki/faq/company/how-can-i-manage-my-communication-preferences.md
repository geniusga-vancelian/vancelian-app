# How can I manage my communication preferences?

## Short answer
The Vancelian application allows you to choose which emails you wish to receive in order to tailor communications to your preferences.  From the application settings, you can select the types of information you would like to continue receiving.

## Details
The Vancelian application allows you to choose which emails you wish to receive in order to tailor communications to your preferences.

From the application settings, you can select the types of information you would like to continue receiving.

**How can I access communication preferences?**

To modify your preferences:

- Open the **Vancelian** application

- From the home screen, go to **Profile**

- Scroll down to the** Settings** section

- Select **Communication preferences**

- You will then be able to choose the** topics** that interest you.

- Use the **sliders** to enable or disable the communications you wish to receive.

 

#### **Good to know**
Certain communications related to the operation of your account or to security may continue to be sent even if marketing communications are disabled.
