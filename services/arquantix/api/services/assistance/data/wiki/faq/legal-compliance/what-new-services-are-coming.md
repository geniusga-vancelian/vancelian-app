# What new services is Vancelian planning to offer?

## Short answer
Beyond its current crypto-asset services, Vancelian is working toward offering **traditional stock exchange** products (ETFs, equities, bonds, commodities) via a MiFID 2 agent partnership starting late 2026, **its own IBAN and payment services** via an EMI licence (target mid-2027), and **wealth management / financial advisory** (life insurance, structured products) via a CIF entity. The long-term vision is to become a regulated digital private bank covering both digital and traditional assets.

## Details

### Traditional stock exchange — MiFID 2 (target: late 2026)
Vancelian plans to offer access to **ETFs, equities, bonds, commodities, and precious metals** through a tied-agent partnership with a MiFID 2-licensed principal (such as Assetera). This allows a quick market entry without requiring Vancelian's own MiFID 2 licence.

- **Planned launch:** September-October 2026
- **Model:** tied agent (revenue share with principal)
- **Products:** ETFs, equities, bonds, commodities, precious metals
- **Own MiFID 2 licence:** application planned for Q4 2026, obtainment horizon Q4 2027 / Q1 2028

In the UAE, an equivalent Cat 3 Asset Manager agent model is planned for the Dubai app (October-November 2026).

### Own IBAN, cards, and multi-currency accounts — EMI (target: mid-2027)
Currently, Vancelian's payment services (IBAN, transfers, VISA card) are operated through the partner **Modulr Finance B.V.** Under an EMI (Electronic Money Institution) licence from the ACPR, Vancelian would internalize these services.

- **File submission:** end June 2026
- **Expected obtainment:** June-July 2027
- **What changes for clients:** own Vancelian IBAN, multi-currency accounts, potentially crypto-collateralized fiat loans, direct card issuance
- **Regulator:** ACPR (Bank of France)

### Wealth management & financial advisory — CIF (target: H1 2027)
Vancelian plans to create a dedicated Financial Investment Advisory entity ("Automata CIF") to integrate **traditional financial products** — life insurance, securities accounts, structured products, real estate club deals, private equity — alongside its existing digital asset offering.

- **Model:** acquisition of an existing CIF firm with an established client base
- **Regulated by:** AMF / ORIAS
- **Impact:** access to traditional savings products favored by European investors

### Long-term: digital private banking (2029-2030 horizon)
Once the MiCA + EMI + MiFID 2 trilogy is in place, Vancelian will have most capabilities of a banking establishment except credit. The long-term ambition is to obtain a **Simplified Credit Institution** licence (ACPR/ECB) and converge toward a full digital private banking model.

> **Important:** all timelines above are targets based on Vancelian's regulatory roadmap and are subject to change based on regulatory processing times. This page will be updated as milestones are reached.

## Caveats
- All timelines are indicative and subject to regulatory approval.
- New services will only be available once the corresponding licences are obtained.
- Product details, pricing, and availability may change — always verify on vancelian.com or in the app.
- For questions about current available services, see [Where and how is Vancelian regulated?](where-and-how-is-vancelian-regulated.md).
