## Details
You can update your email address at any time directly from your application.

**Steps to follow:**

- From the home page, click on **Profile** (top left)

- Select **Personal Details**

- Scroll down to the **Email address** section

- Click on **Edit**

- Enter your new **email address twice**

- Confirm by clicking on the **Send verification link button**

- Check your emails to confirm your new email address via **the link**
