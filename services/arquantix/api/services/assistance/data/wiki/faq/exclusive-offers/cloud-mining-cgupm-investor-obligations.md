# What is the CGUPM and what are my obligations as a Cloud Mining investor?

## Short answer
**CGUPM** = "Conditions Générales d'Utilisation – Programme de Mining" — Vancelian's General Terms & Conditions for the Cloud Mining Programme. By subscribing, you accept that **rewards earned from the mining activity are variable and not guaranteed**, that **capital is locked until contract maturity (31 December 2028 for all new and migrated positions)**, and that you are responsible for declaring your gains under the tax law of your country of residence. Vancelian's role is to manage contracts and ensure transparency — it cannot guarantee a target rate. Vancelian may update the CGUPM; updates require your acceptance to keep using the service.

## Details

### What the CGUPM covers
The CGUPM defines the full set of rules for the Cloud Mining service: subscription terms, commitment period (maturity set at 31 December 2028 for all active contracts — pre-migration contracts remain under their original terms, visible in your client account), reward distribution mechanics, exit windows and fees, fund custody, transfer rules, and the limits of Vancelian's liability.

### Your obligations as an investor
By accepting the CGUPM you commit to:
- **Reading and accepting the terms** before any subscription.
- **Understanding** that the rewards earned from the mining activity are variable, not guaranteed, and that capital is locked over the subscription period.
- **Providing accurate information** during onboarding and KYC.
- **Respecting local laws** applicable in your country of residence.
- **Declaring your gains** to your local tax authority. Vancelian does not provide personalised tax advice.

### Vancelian's role and responsibilities
- **Vancelian LTD (UAE-ADGM)** acts as the **contractual counterparty** of the Cloud Mining programme.
- **Automata France SAS** operates as the **PSAN-regulated intermediary** for European clients (subscription flow, EURC custody via Fireblocks, app display).
- Their joint responsibility covers **proper contract management** and **transparency of information**.
- Vancelian does **not** guarantee a target rate and is **not** liable for performance fluctuations tied to mining activity (BTC price drops, difficulty rises, energy cost spikes).
- Vancelian declines liability for losses caused by market fluctuation, mining difficulty changes, or energy cost increases that may impact the rewards generated by the mining activity.

### Updates and notifications
Vancelian reserves the right to update the CGUPM. Updates are communicated:
- By **email**.
- Via the **client area** in the Vancelian app.

You must accept the new terms to continue using the service.

### Consequences of non-compliance
- **Fraud or abuse** triggers immediate contract suspension and may lead to permanent account closure.
- **Failure to meet CGUPM obligations** (e.g. providing false information, regulatory non-compliance) can lead Vancelian to terminate the contract.

### Confidentiality
Vancelian respects EU data protection law (GDPR). Investor information is not sold or shared outside contractual needs.

### Disputes and applicable law
In case of dispute, the competent jurisdiction is defined by the CGUPM in force at the time of subscription. Refer to the official document for details, or contact customer support.

### Accessing your signed CGUPM
A copy of the accepted CGUPM is accessible from your Vancelian client area at any time, or on request to customer support.

## Caveats
- The CGUPM is the **authoritative source** for contractual rules. This wiki page is a summary for navigation — always check the latest CGUPM in your client area for any specific clause.
- Tax obligations are **jurisdiction-specific**. The chatbot must escalate tax questions to a Vancelian human advisor.
