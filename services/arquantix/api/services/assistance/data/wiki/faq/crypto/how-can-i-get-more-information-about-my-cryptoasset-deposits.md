# How can I get more information about my cryptoasset deposits and withdrawals?

## Short answer
View your crypto deposit and withdrawal details on the Vancelian app by selecting the cryptoasset and scrolling to the Transactions section. Each transaction shows the amount, date, status, network, and links to download a PDF statement or view the transaction on a blockchain explorer.

## Details
To view the details of your cryptoasset transactions (deposits or withdrawals) on the Vancelian app, simply follow these steps:

- From the Home page, go to Crypto.

- Select the** cryptoasset** in question.

- At the top of your wallet page, you will see the **total amount held.**

- Scroll down to the Transactions section (at the bottom of the page) to access your deposit and withdrawal history.

- Select a transaction to view its details.

Information available for each transaction includes:

- The transaction** amount**

- The **date and time**

- The **status** (e.g., Completed)

- The **network used** (Ethereum ERC-20)

- The option to **download a PDF statement** via the icon on the right of Summary

- A direct link to the **relevant blockchain explorer** (e.g., Etherscan for Ethereum), allowing you to view details such as the transaction hash or wallet address
