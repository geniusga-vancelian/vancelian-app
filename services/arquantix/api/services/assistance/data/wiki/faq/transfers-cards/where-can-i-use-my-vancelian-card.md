# Where can I use my Vancelian card?

## Short answer

## Details
Vancelian cards are **Visa payment cards.**

**They can be used to:**

- Make online or in-store payments with merchants that accept the **Visa** network

- Withdraw cash from ATMs

**Online and in-store payments**

You can use your Vancelian card on most online retailers and Visa-compatible payment terminals.

All payments are debited directly from your available balance in the application.

**Important note: **For security reasons, certain payments may be **automatically declined** (depending on the merchant’s location or the services offered).
 

#### **Use abroad**
Your Vancelian card can be used abroad in all regions covered by the **international Visa** network.

However, for security purposes, a payment made from an unusual location may be **blocked.**

**In this case, it is recommended to:**

- [Contact Vancelian customer support](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support) before your trip to inform us of your travel **dates and destination**

- If you are already abroad and a payment was declined, contact customer support immediately so we can **check your card and unblock** it if necessary
