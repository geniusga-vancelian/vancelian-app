# View the performance or allocation of your Crypto Basket

## Short answer
Open your Crypto Basket in the app to view a performance chart with filters for different time periods (day, week, month, year, or all time). Scroll down to see the current allocation breakdown, or tap on "Current basket allocation" for a detailed chart.

## Details
#### **How do I view the performance of the Crypto Basket?**
The performance appears automatically when you open the Crypto Basket.

You will see a **performance chart**, with the option to** filter results** over different periods: day, week, month, year or all time.

**How do I view the allocation of the Crypto Basket?**

By scrolling down the page, you will find an overview of the current allocation of the basket.

You can open a detailed chart by tapping on the title **Current basket allocation.**
 

#### **Good to know**
By tapping the “**i**” icon at the top of the chart, you can access additional information:

- An overview of the theoretical average annual performance of the basket (calculated based on the market, not on your personal investment)

- A reminder of the allocation

- Explanations on how the basket works
