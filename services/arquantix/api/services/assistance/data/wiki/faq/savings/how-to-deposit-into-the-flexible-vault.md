# How do I deposit into the Flexible Vault?

## Short answer
You can deposit into the Flexible Vault from **1 EUR** or approximately **10 USDC** (the exact USDC amount is shown at the moment of deposit). Deposits are processed immediately, while withdrawals can take up to **12 hours** to complete.

## Details
You can make a deposit into the Flexible Vault starting from **1 EUR** or approximately **10 USDC** (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).

While deposits are processed immediately, withdrawals from the Vault may require a processing time of up to **12 hours.** You'll receive an automatic notification once the operation is complete.
