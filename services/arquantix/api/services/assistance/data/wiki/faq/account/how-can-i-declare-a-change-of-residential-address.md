# How can I declare a change of residential address?

## Short answer
If you have moved to a new residence, it is important to update your address in our system to ensure your account remains compliant and to guarantee the proper delivery of your mail (including your Vancelian payment card). This modification cannot be made directly in the app: it must be submitted.

## Details
If you have moved to a new residence, it is important to update your address in our system to ensure your account remains compliant and to guarantee the proper delivery of your mail (including your Vancelian payment card).

This modification cannot be made directly in the app: it must be submitted to our support team for manual verification.

**How to submit the request**

To declare a change of address:

- Contact our **customer support team by email** at [support@vancelian.com](support@vancelian.com)

- Include your** new full address** in your message, with all relevant details (building, floor, access code, etc.)

- Attach a **proof of address** corresponding to this new residence

**Which documents are accepted?**

The proof of address must be in your **name**, dated within the last **3 months** (except for tax notices, valid for 12 months) and in its **original format.**

For more details on the full list of accepted documents and the criteria to follow, please consult this article:

[What are the documents accepted as proof of residence?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/5161612703505-What-are-the-documents-accepted-as-proof-of-residence)

**Processing times**

Once your document has been received, it will be manually reviewed by our dedicated team.

This verification may take up to **3 working days.**

You will receive an email as soon as the modification has been approved.
