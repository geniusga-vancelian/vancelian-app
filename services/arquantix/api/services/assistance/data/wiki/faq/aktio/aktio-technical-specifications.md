# AKTIO technical specifications

## Short answer
**AKTIO** is an **ERC-20 token on the Ethereum blockchain**, ticker **AKTIO**, with a **capped maximum supply of 100,000,000 tokens**. It was issued by Automata ICO Limited (Ireland) and first listed publicly on **13 April 2022**.

## Details

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| **Asset name** | Aktio Coin |
| **Ticker** | AKTIO |
| **Blockchain** | Ethereum |
| **Token standard** | ERC-20 |
| **Max supply (capped)** | 100,000,000 AKTIO |
| **Starting price at ICO** | €1.00 |
| **Issuer** | Automata ICO Limited (Ireland, Reg. No. 690280) |
| **First public listing** | 13 April 2022 |

Because AKTIO is an ERC-20 token, it can be stored in any Ethereum-compatible wallet and moved on the public Ethereum network. Transfers rely on standard Ethereum network fees ("gas").

## Why ERC-20 / Ethereum
Ethereum was chosen as the issuance blockchain for three main reasons cited in the whitepaper:
- **Interoperability** — ERC-20 is the most widely supported token standard, compatible with the majority of wallets, exchanges and decentralised applications.
- **Decentralised governance** — transactions are confirmed by the Ethereum network, with no central intermediary.
- **Transparent record keeping** — all AKTIO transactions, including disbursements from the Aktio Coin Reserve, are publicly recorded on the Ethereum ledger.

## Caveats
- AKTIO is a **utility token**, not a share or a regulated financial investment product.
- Ethereum network gas fees apply to any AKTIO on-chain transfer outside the Vancelian application.
