# How to pay with a project?

## Short answer

## Details
You can now use your Vancelian card to **pay directly with the returns** generated by your projects, without needing to convert the funds into EUR (or USDC) beforehand.

This feature allows you to use the **EURC available in your projects** (e.g. Cloud Mining, 7 Villas in Bali, etc.) to pay for your purchases easily, wherever the Vancelian card is accepted.

**Requirements to use this feature:**

- Your profile is **verified** (Level 2 validated)

- You have ordered and **activated** your Vancelian VISA card

- Your card is active and** not blocked**

- You have a **minimum of 50 EURC** available in the selected project

**How to pay with a project:**

**From the Savings Projects section:**

- Select the relevant** project**

- Click on **Pay with my project**

**Or from the Card shortcut at the bottom of the screen:**

- Under the picture of your card, select **Pay**

- Click on **Pay with a project**

**In both cases:**

- A **window** will open asking you to confirm your choice. You will see the maximum usable amount in crypto, expressed in euros (€).

- Click **Pay in [name of the chosen project]** to confirm and initiate the payment

- From this moment, a **15-minute countdown begins**: you must complete the payment before the countdown expires. If the time limit is exceeded, the transaction will be cancelled and the payment will automatically be redirected to your Euro Account.
 

#### **Maximum amount per payment (depending on your level):**

**Loyalty level**
**Maximum limit per payment**

Bronze
100€

Silver
1000€

Gold
2000€

Platinum
3500€

Elite
5000€

*(This amount corresponds to the maximum limit **per individual payment.** You can make multiple payments as long as you have sufficient funds.)*
 

#### **Applicable fees:**

- **1€ fee** for any payment up to 100€

- **1% fee** for payments over 100€

These fees apply only if the payment is successful. No fees will be charged in case of expiration or cancellation.
