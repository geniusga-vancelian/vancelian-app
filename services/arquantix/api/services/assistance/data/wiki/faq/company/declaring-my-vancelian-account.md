## Details
Vancelian acts solely as an outsourced service provider for **ModulR** to offer payment services, including VISA cards, SEPA transfers, and an EUR account with an IBAN domiciled in Ireland.

#### **Where can I find the information needed for my tax declaration?**

To facilitate the declaration of your account, go directly to the Vancelian app:

- From the homepage, click Profile (top left)

- Section My Account

- Click on Taxation

Here you will find all the necessary information to complete the annex for foreign digital asset accounts, including:

- Full name and address of the institution

- IBAN

- Account type (digital asset account)

- Account opening date

- Account number (alias)

- Name of the PSAN management organisation

You can also generate an **annual statement of your transactions** directly from the app. By clicking the Transaction Statement button, the file will be sent to your email.

How to declare crypto gains?

Crypto gains may be taxable depending on the **regulations in your country.** To simplify the process, Vancelian has partnered with[ Waltio.](https://www.waltio.com/)

- A 10% discount code is available for all new Waltio clients.

- Simply import your transaction statement into your Waltio account, and the tool will guide you in completing your declaration.

This option is only available for users residing in:

- **France**

- **Belgium**

- **Spain**

For step-by-step guidance, you can also consult Waltio’s guides:

- [Guide for the filing of your tax return's cryptocurrencies part](https://help.waltio.com/en/articles/4117254-guide-for-the-filing-of-your-tax-return-s-cryptocurrencies-part)

- [All you need to know](https://help.waltio.com/en/articles/3635124-all-you-need-to-know)

Important

Vancelian cannot provide tax advice or assist with declarations. For any precision on your taxation, obligations, or the interpretation of transactions on your statements, contact your tax administration. You may also use our partner Waltio for your crypto declarations.
