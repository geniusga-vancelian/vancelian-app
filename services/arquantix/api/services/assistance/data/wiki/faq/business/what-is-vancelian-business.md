# What is Vancelian Business?

## Short answer
**Vancelian Business** is Vancelian's offer for **companies that want to make their excess cash work**. It provides four corporate treasury products under one regulated European framework: a **Flexible Vault** (up to 5% APY, fully liquid, no commitment), a **Future Vault** (up to 7% APY, 12-month lock), **Exclusive Offers** (up to 11.1% APR, 18–48 months), and a **Strategic Crypto Reserve** (BTC/ETH allocation). All deposits and withdrawals are free; rewards are credited daily.

## Details

### Why Vancelian Business
> *"Your cash reserves deserve better than 0%."* — Vancelian Business homepage

The offer is designed for companies wanting to transform excess cash into a performance lever, with simple management and full European regulatory compliance.

### The four products

| Product | Top APR/APY | Lock-up | Compounding | Withdrawals |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Flexible Vault** | up to **5%** | None | Yes (daily) | 24/7 |
| **Future Vault** | up to **7%** | 12 months | Yes (daily) | Interests 24/7; capital at maturity |
| **Exclusive Offers** | up to **11.1%** | 18–48 months | No | Interests 24/7; capital subject to exit windows |
| **Strategic Crypto Reserve** | (variable, market) | None | N/A | 24/7 (BTC/ETH allocation, EURC deposit/withdraw) |

All four: **deposit and withdrawal free of charge.**

### Companion product: Crypto Baskets
Vancelian Business clients can also access the same Crypto Baskets as retail clients:
- **Top 2 basket:** BTC, ETH
- **Top 5 basket:** BTC, ETH, SOL, XRP, BNB
- 24/7 withdrawal, EURC for deposit/withdraw

### Key figures
- **450,000+ downloads** (across the broader Vancelian platform)
- **€100M+ assets under management**
- **€4M+ interest paid** (cumulative; the homepage shows €7M+ in a more recent snapshot)

## Caveats
- Rates are **live** and may move with market performance.
- Exclusive Offers carry capital risk and are subject to the same risk warning as the retail product — see [[../legal-compliance/risk-warning-summary]].
- The Strategic Crypto Reserve allocation (BTC, ETH) carries crypto market volatility — there is no capital guarantee.
