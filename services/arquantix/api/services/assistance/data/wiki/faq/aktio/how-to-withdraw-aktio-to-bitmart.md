# Withdrawal of AKTIO tokens to the BitMart exchange platform

## Short answer
**Scope:** this page covers **only** the withdrawal route for **historical ICO investors residing outside the EEA** who do not have access to the Vancelian application. They withdraw AKTIO from the original ICO Launchpad (`ico.akt.io`) to BitMart. **AKTIO acquired via the Vancelian app is not transferable to an external wallet for EEA clients** — there is no app-to-wallet withdrawal route for AKTIO.

If you are an ICO investor outside the EEA: use our partner exchange platform [**BitMart**](https://www.bitmart.com/login/en-US?goto=asset-overview-lang) to receive your AKTIO tokens. You may also register through the BitMart mobile application available on the Play Store or App Store.

## Details
If you reside **outside the European Economic Area (EEA),** you will not be able to access the Vancelian application. In that case, you must use our partner exchange platform to receive your AKTIO tokens: [**BitMart.**](https://www.bitmart.com/login/en-US?goto=asset-overview-lang)

 

#### **Here are the steps to follow:**

- Open the[ BitMart website](https://www.google.com/search?q=bitmart&rlz=1C1GCEA_enFR1154FR1154&oq=bitmart&gs_lcrp=EgZjaHJvbWUqCggAEAAY4wIYgAQyCggAEAAY4wIYgAQyDQgBEC4YxwEY0QMYgAQyBwgCEAAYgAQyBwgDEAAYgAQyBwgEEAAYgAQyBggFEEUYPDIGCAYQRRg8MgYIBxBFGD3SAQc4NTZqMGo3qAIAsAIA&sourceid=chrome&ie=UTF-8) or download the **BitMart** mobile application.

- Complete your registration on BitMart via the “Get Started” section. You will find BitMart’s [help centre here.](https://support.bitmart.com/hc/en-us)

You may also complete your registration through the BitMart mobile application available on the Play Store or App Store.

 

**If you are a UK resident, please read this BitMart article regarding limitations for new retail user registrations in the United Kingdom:**

[https://support.bitmart.com/hc/en-us/articles/19415219101467-IMPORTANT-Limitation-for-New-Retail-User-Registration-for-UK-](https://support.bitmart.com/hc/en-us/articles/19415219101467-IMPORTANT-Limitation-for-New-Retail-User-Registration-for-UK-)

- Once your registration is complete, you can generate your AKTIO wallet on BitMart. Please find below a BitMart Help Centre article explaining how to make a deposit in order to generate an AKTIO wallet address:

[https://support.bitmart.com/hc/en-us/articles/8367742241947-How-to-Deposit-](https://support.bitmart.com/hc/en-us/articles/8367742241947-How-to-Deposit-)

 
**WARNING: If you search for AKT instead of AKTIO, you will find AKASH, which is a completely different token.**
 

- **Copy** the **AKTIO **wallet address you have just generated.

- Go to: [http://ico.akt.io/](http://ico.akt.io/) to access your ICO Launchpad (the website used during your ICO registration) and **paste** your AKTIO wallet address into the “My wallets” section using the “Set up transfer” option. Once the address is provided, follow the instructions on the website.

**If you are unable to access it via your browser, please try again using private browsing mode.**

- Once your withdrawal request for AKTIO coins has been submitted to BitMart, our team will manually process the transfer of your AKTIO coins. This may take 2 to 3 working days, thank you for your patience and understanding.

Vancelian covers the fees related to AKTIO transfers to BitMart for all ICO investors who do not have access to the app.

 

**IMPORTANT: We cannot be held responsible for potential delays in processing by the platform or restricted access to the BitMart platform.**
