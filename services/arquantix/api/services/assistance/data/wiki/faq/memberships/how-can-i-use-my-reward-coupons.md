# How can I use my reward coupons?

## Short answer
Your referral rewards are automatically credited as coupons directly in your app's Offers and Rewards section. You can use coupons to buy crypto—currently BTC or USDC—with no expiration date and no fees. Coupons are personal and cannot be transferred; there is a minimum amount (around 10 USDC) to redeem.

## Details
Rewards earned through the referral program are now credited in the **form of** coupons, directly within your Vancelian application.

These coupons work the same way as previous reward credits: they are automatically deposited into a dedicated Rewards Vault and can be used to acquire **crypto assets.**
 
#### **How are coupons credited?**
Once your referee meets the conditions of the referral programme and begins generating rewards, reward coupons are automatically credited to the referrer’s account.

Each coupon appears in the Offers and Rewards section of your app.

The reason for the reward (related offer or activity) is shown in the coupon details.

Your Rewards Vault updates automatically with each new coupon issued.

You can check the total amount of coupons accumulated at any time in this section.

**How can I use my coupons?**

- From the home page, open the Offers and Rewards section.

- Go to the Reward Coupons tab.

- Select Use my coupons to open the transaction page.

- Choose the** asset** you wish to invest in:

- USDC

- BTC
*(Currently, coupons can only be used for these two crypto assets, but this may evolve in future updates)*

- **Confirm** the operation to convert your coupon value into a crypto asset. You will then find your new asset in your wallets under the Crypto section of the app.

 
#### **Good to know**

- Coupons do not have an expiration date.

- Using your coupons to acquire crypto assets is free of charge.

- A minimum amount applies to coupon use (usually around 10 USDC or equivalent), which will be shown on screen.

- Coupons can only be used to **purchase crypto assets** offered within the app (USDC and BTC).

- Coupons are personal and cannot be transferred to another user.

 

*For more details, please refer to the dedicated section of the Referral Program in our *[*Terms and Conditions.*](https://vancelian.com/en/terms-conditions-vancelian-platform)
