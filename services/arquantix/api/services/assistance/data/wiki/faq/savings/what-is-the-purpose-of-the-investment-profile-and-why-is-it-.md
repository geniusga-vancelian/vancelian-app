# What is the purpose of the investment profile and why is it mandatory?

## Short answer
An investment profile is a regulatory questionnaire assessing your risk tolerance and financial goals. It is mandatory because Vancelian must recommend suitable products under European regulation and protect you from unsuitable investments. Your profile is valid for 2 years and can be updated at any time in the app.

## Details
#### Why create an investment profile?
At Vancelian, we aim to offer solutions that are truly suited to your **situation, objectives, and risk tolerance.**

That’s why we ask you to define your investment profile before accessing certain products, such as **Flexible or Future Vaults.**

 

#### What is an investment profile?
An investment profile is a personalised assessment of your capacity and willingness to take risks, based on a structured questionnaire.

This profile considers several key aspects:

- Your **objectives** (growing your savings, protecting capital, generating income…)

- Your investment **horizon** (short, medium, or long term)

- Your **knowledge** of investments

- Your financial** situation**

- Your **sensitivity** to market fluctuations

This profile allows us to guide you towards the products most appropriate for your situation.

#### Why is it mandatory?
Because we have a responsibility to recommend suitable solutions.

When you invest in a product managed by Vancelian, such as our Vaults, we must ensure it matches your profile.

This is a requirement under the **European regulatory** framework, but above all, it is designed to protect you and prevent unsuitable investments.

 

#### What is the suitability report?
Once your profile is established, we automatically generate a **suitability report.**

This **regulatory document** summarises your objectives, risk tolerance, and explains why certain products (like our Vaults) are accessible to you.

It also records the logic behind the allocation applied within the portfolio management provided by Vancelian.

This report is available at any time from the **Investment Profile** section of your account.

How long is it valid?

Your profile remains valid for** 2 years.**

You will be notified when your profile needs to be updated.
