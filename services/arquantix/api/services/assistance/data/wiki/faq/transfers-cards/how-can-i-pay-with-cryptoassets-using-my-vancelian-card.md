# How can I pay with cryptoassets using my Vancelian card?

## Short answer
Your Vancelian VISA card allows you to pay directly from your cryptoasset balance. The mechanic is structured in **two legs**, not a single crypto-to-EUR sale: your crypto is **blocked as a temporary reserve** on your wallet, Vancelian **lends you an equivalent amount in EURC** (the euro stablecoin regulated under MiCA) which is **converted 1:1 without fees into EUR** to settle the card payment. After the payment clears, your crypto reserve is **exchanged into EURC** to repay the loan. From your wallet's perspective, the only movement on your crypto-asset is a **crypto → EURC exchange**. For the full technical mechanic and the tax-treatment context, see [[how-crypto-card-payment-works]].

## Details
Your Vancelian VISA card allows you to pay directly from your cryptoasset balance. You don't need to sell your crypto manually in advance — **Vancelian automatically converts the amount to euros at the point of payment** and settles the transaction in EUR via the VISA network. The conversion is not optional; what changes is that it is triggered by the payment, not by you ahead of time.

Thanks to the **Pay with cryptoassets** feature, you can pay for your purchases using the cryptocurrencies you hold in your application.

**Which cryptocurrencies can be used?**

You can currently pay with the following three cryptoassets:

- **BTC** (Bitcoin)

- **ETH **(Ethereum)

- **USDC **(USD Coin)

**How to activate the “Pay with cryptoassets” feature?**

- Go to the** Card** section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Under the photo of your card, select **Pay**

- Click on **Pay with cryptocurrencies**

- Choose the **cryptoasset** you want to use from the list

- Press** Next**

- A **window** will open asking you to confirm your choice. You will see the maximum usable amount in crypto, expressed in euros (€).

- Click **Pay in [chosen crypto]** to confirm and launch the payment

- Once this step is completed, a **15-minute countdown begins:** you must complete the payment before the time expires. Otherwise, the crypto transaction will be cancelled and the payment will automatically be redirected to your Euro Account.

**What is split payment?**

You can split a payment between your cryptoassets and your **Euro Account**, as long as you have sufficient funds in both.

**Example:** paying a €150 bill using €50 in Bitcoin and €100 from your EUR balance.

**Which fees apply?**

- **€1 fee** for any payment up to €100

- **1% fee** for payments over €100

No fees apply to expired or cancelled payments before validation.

**Conditions of use**

To use the Pay with cryptoassets feature, you must:

- Have validated your **Level 2** (Residence section) in the application

- Have an **active** Vancelian payment card

- Have a positive balance on your **EUR account**

- **Hold the chosen cryptocurrency** in your wallet at the time of payment

**Payment limits according to your loyalty level**

- Up to 80% of the value of the selected cryptoasset can be used for a payment (within the authorised limit), regardless of your loyalty level.

- You can read more about our [Privilege Club here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club)

- You may make several payments as long as your available crypto balance allows it.

- There is no minimum amount required for payments.
