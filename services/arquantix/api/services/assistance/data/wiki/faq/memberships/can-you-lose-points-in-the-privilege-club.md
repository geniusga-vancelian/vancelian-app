# Can you lose points in the Privilege Club?

## Short answer
Yes, you can lose points in the Privilege Club—specifically your variable points when you withdraw funds or unlock tokens. Permanent points stay locked in forever, but variable points decrease proportionally when you reduce your invested capital. However, you can regain points at any time by reinvesting or taking new actions within the app.

## Details
It is possible to lose some points depending on your activity within the application.

Permanent points are acquired definitively, while variable points may fluctuate according to your level of engagement and your active investments.

You may lose a portion of your variable points in the following situations:

- When **withdrawing funds** from your investment projects or savings vaults.

- When **unlocking** your Vancelian tokens, for example through **unstaking** or **unlocking** operations.

Since these points are directly linked to your invested capital and locked assets, they decrease proportionally when you reduce your level of engagement.

However, you can** regain points** at any time and [move back up to a higher level](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29319660844177-How-to-move-up-to-a-higher-level) by performing new actions within the application.
