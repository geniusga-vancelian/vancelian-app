# How to make a bank transfer from the Vancelian app

## Short answer
To make a bank transfer from Vancelian, you must first have added your beneficiary. Once your beneficiary is confirmed, navigate to the Transfers section, select your beneficiary, enter the amount, and confirm the transfer.

## Details
Before making a transfer, **you must first have added your beneficiary.**

Learn how to do this in our article: [How do I add a beneficiary?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4419832201489-How-do-I-add-a-beneficiary)

#### Steps to follow:

- From the home screen, select **"Send"** among the four buttons below your total funds

- Click **"**Bank Transfer"

- Select an **existing beneficiary** or **add** a new one

- Enter the desired **amount**

- Check the beneficiary name match
For more information, see our article: [What is beneficiary verification?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39614974441361-What-is-beneficiary-verification)

- Click **"**Send"

- Click **"**Confirm"

- Enter the **verification code** received via SMS to complete the transaction

## Processing time
A SEPA transfer can take **up to 5 working days** to be processed. If this delay is exceeded, contact `support@vancelian.com` and attach a proof of the transfer to facilitate the investigation.
