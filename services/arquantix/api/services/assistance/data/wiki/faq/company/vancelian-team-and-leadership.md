# Vancelian leadership team

## Short answer
Vancelian is led by **CEO and Founder Gaël Itier**, alongside co-founders **Jean-Yves Guillou** (Managing Director Europe) and **Julien Halimi** (Head of Real World Assets). The executive team also includes **Xavier Gomez** (Managing Director France & UAE/Dubai), **Pierre-Yves Dittlot** (Chairman of Automata France/Vancelian), **Rémi Gélibert** (CTO & CPO), **Mohesh Guness** (Head of Customer Service), and **Benjamin Messika** (Chief Compliance Officer).

## Details

### Founders
- **Gaël Itier** — Chief Executive Officer Group (CEO) & Founder
- **Jean-Yves Guillou** — Managing Director Europe & Co-founder
- **Julien Halimi** — Head of Real World Assets & Co-founder

### Executive team
- **Xavier Gomez** — Managing Director France & U.A.E. (Dubai)
- **Pierre-Yves Dittlot** — Chairman of Automata France / Vancelian
- **Rémi Gélibert** — Chief Technology Officer & Chief Product Officer
- **Mohesh Guness** — Head of Customer Service
- **Benjamin Messika** — Chief Compliance Officer
