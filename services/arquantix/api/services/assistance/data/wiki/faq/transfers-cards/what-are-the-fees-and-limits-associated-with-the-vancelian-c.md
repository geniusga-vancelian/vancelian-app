# What are the fees and limits associated with the Vancelian card?

## Short answer
Your Vancelian card has different fees and spending limits depending on your loyalty level, geographic location, and the type of transaction. ATM withdrawals, contactless payments, and foreign currency exchanges may each have different limits and fees.

## Details
Your Vancelian payment card has different limits and fees that depend on:

- Your **loyalty level**

- The **type of operation** (payment, withdrawal, payment in cryptoassets, etc.)

- The **technical limits** imposed by our banking partner

#### **What are the fixed technical limits of the card?**
Some limits do not depend on your loyalty level and cannot be modified:
 

- **ATM withdrawals**

300€ maximum per day and per transaction.

Even if your loyalty level gives you a higher monthly allowance, the technical withdrawal limit remains €300 per day.

**Example:** A Gold member may withdraw up to €600 free per month, but €300 maximum per single withdrawal.
 

- **Card payments**

10,000€ maximum per day and per transaction.

**How can I view my limits in the application?**

You can find your personalised limits directly in your application:

- Go to** Card **(shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Select **Spending and withdrawal limits**

**What are the fees for payments in cryptoassets?**

Some limits displayed for cryptoasset payments are subject to an additional restriction:

You may only use up to **80%** of the value of the selected crypto wallet.

See the dedicated article: [How can I pay with my cryptoassets using my Vancelian card?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/14065502328977-How-can-I-pay-with-cryptoassets-using-my-Vancelian-card)

***Fees applied:**

- **1€** for any payment up to 100€

- **1%** for any payment above 100€

**Good to know**

- The card limits and fees are also available on [our website.](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships)

- The number of free withdrawals and the withdrawal limit are calculated over a rolling 31-day period.

- You can find more information about the [Privilege Club here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club)

- Conditions may vary depending on the security policies applied by VISA and our banking partner Modulr.
