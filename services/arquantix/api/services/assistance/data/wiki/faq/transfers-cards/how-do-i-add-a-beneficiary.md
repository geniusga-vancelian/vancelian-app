# How do I add a beneficiary?

## Short answer
You can add a beneficiary directly from the Vancelian app.

## Details
You can add a **beneficiary** directly from the Vancelian app.

#### Steps to follow:

- From the home screen, tap **"More"** in the four options below your total funds.

- Select **"**Beneficiaries".

- Tap **"**Add +" at the top right of the page.

- Enter the requested information: full name, IBAN, and country of the bank account.

- Tap **"**Confirm" to save the beneficiary to your list.

Adding a beneficiary during a transfer:

You can also add a new beneficiary directly when **initiating a transfer** by entering their information.

 

#### Check the accuracy of the information:

When adding a beneficiary, it is essential to enter their name exactly as it appears on their bank account details to reduce the risk of errors and ensure proper verification of the information.

For more information, see our article: [What is beneficiary verification?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39614974441361-What-is-beneficiary-verification)
