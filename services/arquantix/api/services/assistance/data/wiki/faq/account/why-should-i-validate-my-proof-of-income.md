# Why should I validate my proof of income?

## Short answer
Validating your proof of income corresponds to level 3 in the Vancelian application, the highest account level. This unlocks higher limits on deposits and withdrawals in euros.

## Details
The validation of your proof of income corresponds to [level 3](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/7826692990225-Source-of-funds) in the Vancelian application.

It is the highest account level, allowing you to lift the limits on deposits and withdrawals in euros.

[Consult the different account levels.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/7035136686737-What-are-the-account-levels)

**Why might this step be necessary?**

The verification and validation of a proof of income are part of our regulatory obligations regarding anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing.
 

#### **How is this verification beneficial for you?**
This step allows us to better understand the nature of your transactions and to ensure a compliant and transparent environment for all users.

It helps maintain a safe and well-regulated use of our services, while enabling you to benefit from higher transaction limits.
