# What is rebalancing?

## Short answer
Rebalancing automatically adjusts your Crypto Basket allocation back to its target by selling overweight assets and buying underweight ones when prices shift. You can activate or deactivate it anytime by clicking “Next Rebalancing” in the app; rebalancing is free of charge.

## Details
#### **What is rebalancing?**
Automatic rebalancing within the Crypto Basket, once activated, **regularly adjusts your basket allocation** back to its **initial target** by selling and buying the necessary assets.

For example, if Bitcoin gains too much weight in a basket after an increase in its price, the system will sell a small portion to** restore balance.**

**Execution order:** rebalancing is executed **sells first, buys second** — overweight assets are converted to stablecoins first, then the proceeds are used to buy underweight assets to restore the target allocation. This order is set by Vancelian's execution policy.

The option can be activated or deactivated directly by clicking on **“Next Rebalancing”** within the Crypto Basket.

By default, **monthly** automatic rebalancing is set (on the 1st of each month), but you can later adjust the frequency in the app (weekly, monthly, or quarterly).

All rebalancing within the solution is** free of charge.**
 

#### What if I previously held an Automated Portfolio?
For users who already held the former Automated Portfolio, no action is required.

Your allocation will automatically switch to the “Top 5 Majors” Basket, with a default monthly rebalancing that you can adjust.

For those who had activated the **”Capital Preservation”** option, rebalancing will be disabled by default to maintain a portion of the allocation in stablecoin.

However, you can reactivate it if you wish to invest 100% of your allocation in the Crypto Basket.

#### Capital Preservation risk profiles

The Capital Preservation feature lets you reduce your exposure to crypto volatility by allocating a portion of your Crypto Basket to **stablecoins**. When you activate it, you choose one of **five risk profiles**, each determining a different crypto-to-stablecoin ratio. Lower-risk profiles allocate more to stablecoins; higher-risk profiles keep more in volatile crypto.

You can switch between profiles or deactivate Capital Preservation entirely at any time — the change takes effect at the next scheduled rebalancing window. Capital Preservation **reduces but does not eliminate** market risk.
