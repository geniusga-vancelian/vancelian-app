# How can I make a deposit?

## Short answer
You can fund your Vancelian account in three ways: via **bank transfer**, **bank card**, or **blockchain transfer**. Bank transfers require prior validation of level 2 on your account. The **first deposit** is specifically the account-verification transfer and must come from a bank account in your own name (see [Account verification by bank transfer](../account/account-verification-by-bank-transfer.md)); a joint account is possible via a manual check by support. **Subsequent deposits** are not bound by the same strict first-and-last-name matching rule — a transfer from a third party (spouse, family member) can be received, though additional compliance checks may apply before crediting.

## Details
You can fund your Vancelian account in three ways: **via bank transfer, bank card, or blockchain transfer.**

Deposits made by bank transfer require prior validation of [level 2](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/7834100629265-Address-verification-all-you-need-to-know-about-processing-times-and-rejections) on your account.

 

#### **1. Making a deposit by bank transfer**

**First deposit vs. subsequent deposits — different rules apply**

- **First deposit** (account-verification transfer). This initial SEPA transfer must come from a **bank account in your own first and last name**, from a bank listed in the application. The sender's name is checked against your Vancelian account details. Purpose: confirm that the account holder has a personal bank account before full access is activated. Specific case — **joint account**: you can still complete the first deposit from a joint account, but you must send the RIB in PDF to Vancelian support so that a manual verification can be performed. See [Account verification by bank transfer](../account/account-verification-by-bank-transfer.md).

- **Subsequent deposits** (after verification). The strict first-and-last-name matching rule applies only to the verification transfer. After the account is verified, incoming SEPA transfers from **a third party** (spouse, family member, joint account) can be credited. Additional compliance checks may be triggered on a case-by-case basis (LCB-FT / AML requirements) — when this happens, the transfer may be held in pending status and support may request supporting documents before releasing the funds. See [Why is my transfer pending?](why-is-my-transfer-pending.md).

For any specific situation (transfer held, third-party transfer requiring documentation, joint account), contact Vancelian support at support@vancelian.com.

**If this is your first deposit:**

Please consult [this article.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/28665769522321-Account-approval-via-bank-transfer)

 

#### **If you have already made a first deposit:**

You can find your IBAN at any time from several sections within the app:

- From the **home page**, below the total balance, tap **Deposit**

- Then select **Bank transfer**

- Your bank details will appear with a copy icon on the right to easily retrieve your IBAN

 

- From the **home page**, tap **More**

- Then select **IBAN**

- Your bank details will appear with a copy icon on the right to easily retrieve your IBAN

 

- From your **Profile** (top left)

- In the section **My Account**

- Then select **IBAN**

Please note that SEPA transfers can take up to **5 business days** to appear in your account, depending on interbank processing times.

#### **2. Making a deposit by bank card**

- From the **home page**, tap **Deposit**

- Select **Bank card**

- Enter the desired amount
*Minimum 50 EUR, maximum 1000 EUR*
*A 2.5% transaction fee applies to each deposit*

- The estimated fee amount will appear on the **Next** button

- Enter your **card information** (number, expiry date, etc.) to confirm the payment

The deposit may take up to **1 business day** to appear in your Vancelian account.

 

#### **3. Making a deposit by blockchain transfer**

- From the home page, tap **Deposit**

- Select **Blockchain transfer**

- Choose the **cryptocurrency** you wish to deposit
*[See the list of supported cryptoassets](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16583610575761-Which-cryptoassets-can-I-deposit-on-the-Vancelian-app)*

- Select the appropriate** network**

- Carefully **verify** your sending platform: transfers from unregulated or unsupported platforms will be blocked

- Your **deposit address** (wallet address) and a **QR code** will appear, with the option to copy the address easily
