# How can I modify my personal information?

## Short answer
Some personal information, such as your surname, marital status, or a typing error made during registration, cannot be changed directly from the app. To ensure the security and compliance of your account, any modification request must be reviewed manually by our team.

## Details
Some personal information, such as your **surname**, **marital status**, or a **typing error made during registration**, cannot be changed directly from the app.

To ensure the security and compliance of your account, any modification request must be reviewed manually by our **team.**
 

#### **How to request a modification**
To update your personal information:

- Contact our **customer support team** by email at: [support@vancelian.com](mailto:support@vancelian.com)[](mailto:support@vancelian.com)

- In your message, **specify the change** you wish to make (for example: name change, correction of an error, marital status update)

- Attach an **official document** that supports your request

 

#### Accepted supporting documents
Depending on the type of modification, you may be asked to provide:

- A valid **official ID** (for correcting an error or changing your name)

- A **marriage** or **divorce certificate** (for name changes related to marital status)

- Any other **legal or administrative document** justifying the change

**Documents must be:**

- Clear and complete

- In an accepted format (PDF, JPEG, JPG, or PNG)

- Valid and up to date

Our team will review your request and the documents you have provided.

If the supporting document is approved, the modification will be made **manually** on your account, and you will receive a confirmation email once the update is complete.
 

#### Good to know

- No modification will be processed without a valid supporting document

- Sensitive information ([email address](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/7081626802961-How-to-modify-the-email-address-linked-to-your-Vancelian-account), [phone number](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/7081982610449-How-to-modify-the-phone-number-linked-to-your-Vancelian-account), [residential address](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/41592667574161-Change-address)) follows separate procedures

- Vancelian reserves the right to refuse any request deemed non-compliant or incomplete
