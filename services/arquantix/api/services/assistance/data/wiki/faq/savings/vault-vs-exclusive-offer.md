# What is the difference between a Vault and an Exclusive Offer?

## Short answer

A **Vault** (Flexible or Future) is a **diversified pocket** that you open and manage simply: you deposit, you accept the terms, and your funds are allocated across underlyings **defined upfront at the moment of deposit** (Cloud Mining, Solaria, EURC reserve). The distributed rate is fixed and stable.

An **Exclusive Offer** is a **specific project** with its own counterparty, schedule, and dedicated rate. You participate in this single project — Cloud Mining (computing-power purchase contract operated by the Vancelian LTD JV with Hearst Solution FZCO), Dubai Villa Al Barari (BTC loan to Solaria), Munduk Premium Villa Bali (BTC loan to a real estate SPV) — under fixed contractual terms.

In short: a Vault is a **diversified envelope** managed through a pre-defined allocation that may evolve over time; an Exclusive Offer is a **targeted investment in a single project** with dedicated contractual terms.

## Details

### A Vault — diversified pocket, allocation defined at deposit

A Vault is a diversified pocket. You open it directly, you accept the terms and the framework of allocation, and you deposit your capital. Vancelian then allocates your funds across the underlyings **defined upfront at the moment of deposit**:

- Mining (Cloud Mining)
- The Solaria Exclusive Offer
- A non-remunerated EURC reserve (regulatory requirement under MiCA)

The allocation may evolve over time as Vancelian rebalances between the EURC reserve and the yield-generating underlyings, within the framework you accepted at subscription. The **rate distributed to you is fixed**: small variations on the gross yield generated by the rebalancing are absorbed by Vancelian's intermediation margin so that your distributed rate stays stable. See [[vancelian-rate-smoothing-and-margin]] for the full mechanic.

You manage your Vault simply by choosing the type that fits your liquidity preference:
- **Flexible Vault** — withdrawable at any time, lower indicative rate (more liquidity reserve).
- **Future Vault** — 12-month commitment, higher indicative rate (more capital can be allocated to yield products).

### An Exclusive Offer — a specific project with dedicated terms

An Exclusive Offer is a **single, dedicated investment programme** with its own structure, counterparty, schedule, and rate. You participate in this specific project under fixed contractual terms — commitment period, maturity date, payment frequency, exit windows. The current Exclusive Offers are:

- **Cloud Mining by Hearst Infrastructure** — a computing-power purchase contract. The contractual counterparty is **Vancelian LTD** (the Joint Venture between Automata Group UK and Hearst Solution FZCO). Hearst is the technical operator of the JV. The rate is **variable** by contract — see [[cloud-mining-yield-factors]].
- **Dubai Villa Al Barari** — BTC loan refinancing operation, with **Solaria** as the borrower SPV. Fixed contractual rate. See [[what-is-the-exclusive-offer-dubai-villa-al-barari]].
- **Munduk Premium Villa Bali** — BTC loan refinancing operation, with a Bali real estate SPV as borrower. Fixed contractual rate. See [[what-is-the-7-luxury-villas-in-bali-exclusive-offer]].

For real estate Exclusive Offers, the mechanic is documented in [[how-exclusive-offer-btc-lending-works]]. For the Cloud Mining mechanic, see [[how-cloud-mining-flow-works]].

### Two complementary products, not substitutes

A Vault and an Exclusive Offer are not the same product, but they are not exclusive either. Many clients hold both: a Vault for a diversified, flexible exposure with a stable rate; one or several Exclusive Offers for targeted, project-level participation with dedicated terms. Note that Vault allocations themselves include exposure to Cloud Mining and to the Solaria Exclusive Offer — the two product families share underlyings but do not have the same client experience, the same liquidity, or the same contractual framework.

## In summary

| | Vault (Flexible / Future) | Exclusive Offer |
|---|---|---|
| **Nature** | Diversified pocket | Single, targeted project |
| **Allocation** | Pre-defined at deposit, may evolve over time | Single counterparty, fixed by contract |
| **Liquidity** | Flexible: any time / Future: 12-month lock | Commitment for the offer's contractual term |
| **Distributed rate** | Fixed, smoothed by Vancelian's margin | Fixed (real estate) or variable (Cloud Mining) |
| **Counterparty** | Vancelian operates the allocation | Named per offer (Vancelian LTD JV, Solaria, Bali SPV) |
| **Documentation** | Vault terms in app | Dedicated brochure per offer |

## Caveats

- Rates are **indicative** and may change between subscription windows — always verify the live rate in the Vancelian app before opening or topping up.
- Both products carry their own underlying risks. For Vaults, see [[are-there-any-risks-of-capital-loss]]. For Exclusive Offers, see the risk page of each offer.
- Choosing between the two is a personal decision, possibly informed by external personalised advice. If you need help finding the comparison documentation, the FAQ, or the T&C in the app, contact Vancelian support.
