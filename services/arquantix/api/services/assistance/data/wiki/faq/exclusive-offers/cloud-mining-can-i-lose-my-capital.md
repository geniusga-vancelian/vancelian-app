# Are my Cloud Mining funds guaranteed? Can I lose my capital?

> **Important:** Cloud Mining is a **computing power purchase contract** — not a loan. Vancelian acts as intermediary between you and the mining programme. Your contractual counterparty is **Vancelian LTD** (UAE-ADGM). For full contractual details, refer to the CGUPM and the official offer documentation.

## Short answer
Two things must be distinguished. **The invested capital** (corresponding to the price of the computing power you purchased) is **contractually committed to be reimbursed by Vancelian LTD at the end of your engagement period**, under article 4.3 of the CGUPM. This means that at maturity, the capital is technically unlocked and made available for withdrawal in the app. **The rewards** (yields), on the other hand, are **variable and not guaranteed** — they depend on the Bitcoin market and mining economics. A **residual counterparty risk** remains: the contractual reimbursement is only as solid as the financial capacity of Vancelian LTD (a joint venture between Automata Group LTD UK and Hearst Solution FZCO).

## Details

### The question itself needs to be split in two
The honest answer to "can I lose my capital?" requires separating two things that are often blurred together in marketing language: **the capital you invest**, and **the rewards that capital generates**. They carry very different levels of protection, and conflating them is the single most common source of misunderstanding on Cloud Mining. We treat them separately below.

### The invested capital — a contractual reimbursement undertaking at maturity
The amount you invest in the programme corresponds to the **price of the computing power** allocated to you — it is the purchase price of a share of hashrate, not a deposit. Under **article 4.3 of the CGUPM** (Conditions Générales d'Utilisation du Programme de Minage), **Vancelian LTD undertakes to reimburse this capital at the end of your engagement period**. In practice, "reimburse" means that at maturity the capital is technically unlocked and made accessible for withdrawal from your Vancelian wallet. It is essential to read this for what it is: a **contractual commitment by Vancelian LTD**, not a deposit guarantee, not a regulated capital protection scheme, and not a state-backed protection.

### The rewards — variable and never guaranteed
The mining rewards distributed during your engagement period are **variable**, and the programme makes no commitment to any level of yield. Rewards move with the **Bitcoin price**, the **mining difficulty** (the level of global competition between miners), the **hashprice** (effective profitability per unit of hashpower), and the **operating costs** — primarily electricity. Any of these four can move sharply in either direction, and rewards with them. The yield observed in a given month is not a forecast. See [[cloud-mining-yield-factors]] for the full mechanics.

### The residual counterparty risk — what could still prevent reimbursement
The contractual undertaking under article 4.3 is only as solid as the **financial capacity of the entity that owes it**. Several real-world scenarios can affect that capacity, and it is fairer to name them openly than to hide behind contractual language. **Insolvency or bankruptcy** of Vancelian LTD or its operating partners would expose the capital to recovery proceedings, with no guarantee of full recovery. **Force majeure** — natural disaster, prolonged energy outage, geopolitical conflict affecting the mining sites — could materially damage the economics of the programme. **Mechanical or economic obsolescence** of the mining park could render the equipment unable to produce enough yield to sustain the programme. And a **definitive cessation of service** would trigger a search for compensation solutions, but continuity itself is not contractually guaranteed.

In every one of these scenarios, the contractual obligation remains intact — what changes is the **practical ability to honour it**, which is bounded by the **own funds and assets of Vancelian LTD** (the joint venture between Automata Group LTD UK and Hearst Solution FZCO).

### Who stands behind the contract — the joint-venture structure
It is worth stating clearly who the counterparty is, because this is where most client confusion sits. **Vancelian LTD** is the contractual counterparty of the Cloud Mining offer, structured as a **joint venture between Automata Group LTD (UK)** and **Hearst Solution FZCO**. **Hearst Solution FZCO** is the technical operator on the ground, responsible for mining-park efficiency and operational performance; Hearst was selected historically by Vancelian as the industrial partner to build this offer, based on its operational expertise in Bitcoin mining infrastructure. The JV structure is deliberate: it aligns the technical operator (Hearst) and the commercial issuer (Automata Group / Vancelian) on the same economic outcome, so both sides share the same incentive to make the programme perform.

### Managing your own exposure — the four principles
Given this framing, managing the risk is less about the programme and more about how you position it in your wealth strategy. Four principles matter. **Do not concentrate** your savings in Cloud Mining — treat it as one allocation among several. **Adopt a long horizon** consistent with mining cycles, particularly halvings and difficulty resets that only make economic sense over multi-year periods. **Track the market signals** that actually drive your returns — BTC price, difficulty, hashprice, energy costs — rather than only the advertised yield. And **only invest what you can afford to leave locked**, because capital is committed until contract maturity (31 December 2028 for all new and migrated positions; pre-migration contracts retain their original maturity, visible in your client account) with early-exit windows that depend on replacement investor demand.

## Caveats
The contractual reimbursement of capital under article 4.3 is a commitment from Vancelian LTD — it is **not** a regulated capital guarantee, a deposit guarantee scheme, or a state-backed protection. Rewards are never guaranteed — only the invested capital is subject to the contractual reimbursement undertaking, and only within the financial capacity of the counterparty. For any specific loss scenario or question on contractual interpretation, refer to a Vancelian advisor; the chatbot is not a substitute for personalised advice.
