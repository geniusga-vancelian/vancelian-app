# What is the Exclusive Offer - Cloud Mining by Hearst (infrastructure operated by Hearst)?

> **Important:** Cloud Mining is a **computing power purchase contract** — not a loan. Vancelian acts as intermediary between you and the mining programme operated by **Vancelian LTD** (UAE-ADGM), a joint venture between Automata Group LTD and Hearst Solution FZCO. For contractual details, refer to the CGUPM (Terms & Conditions of the Mining Programme).

## Short answer
Cloud Mining by Hearst is a Bitcoin mining offer backed by Hearst's **Ethiopia mining site at Legetafo** (20,000 m², 20 MW capacity, 100% hydroelectric energy from the Grand Ethiopian Renaissance Dam, equipped with **Bitmain S21 Pro+ machines** at 234 TH/s each). You earn a **daily reward in EURC** (a euro-pegged stablecoin) credited directly to your Vancelian balance, which you can withdraw in **EUR or USDC**. Hearst operates mining infrastructure across **7 countries**; Vancelian client funds are specifically deployed to the Ethiopia site.

## Details
A new exclusive Cloud Mining offer is available to users of the Vancelian app. This project is built on Hearst’s international infrastructure, a leading company in the Bitcoin mining sector. It provides access to a real mining operation, structured to deliver **performance and sustainability** over time.

The programme is based on a network of data centres located across** 7 countries,** selected for their favourable energy conditions. Each site runs on **renewable** or low-impact energy sources, such as **hydroelectric** power or **thermal recovery**, and operates under high standards of **security** and continuous monitoring.

As a subscriber, you purchase a share of **computing power** within this high-efficiency mining infrastructure, operated with **next-generation** machines designed to optimise output.

In return, you receive a daily reward in **EURC,** a stablecoin tied to the euro (€), paid directly into your available balance in the Vancelian app. This is **not a loan** — it is a computing power purchase contract governed by the CGUPM.

These rewards can be withdrawn in **EUR **or **USDC.**

You can find more details about this offer and our partner Hearst in the brochure.

#### Hearst at a glance (2025)
- Founded in **2021**, operates **2+ exahash** of total computing power.
- **10,000+ machines** across **7 countries**: USA, Brazil, Ethiopia, Oman, Kazakhstan, Norway, UAE.
- **500+ BTC mined in 2025** to date.
- Vancelian's specific deployment via Hearst represents approximately **€40 million of hardware assets**, concentrated on sites in **Ethiopia, the United States, and Kazakhstan** (geographic diversification to reduce single-location risk).

#### Machines and cooling
The fleet uses **Bitmain** equipment — global leader in mining hardware — with the **Antminer S21 Pro+** as the flagship model. Each site uses the cooling technology best suited to local conditions:
- **Air cooling**: classic fan-based, suited to moderate climates.
- **Hydro (liquid) cooling**: liquid circulation absorbs heat — more efficient in hot climates and high-density deployments.

#### Sustainability
Hearst prioritises low-impact energy sources where possible: **hydroelectricity** (Ethiopia, Norway), **flared gas recovery**, and **load balancing** (the "mining as a battery" concept — absorbing surplus grid energy and stabilising local networks). Sites are monitored 24/7 with automated alerts and real-time performance tracking.
