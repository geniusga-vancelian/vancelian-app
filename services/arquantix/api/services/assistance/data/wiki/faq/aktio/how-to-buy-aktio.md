# How to buy AKTIO tokens in the Vancelian application ?

## Short answer
AKTIO is available through **two channels**: (1) directly in the **Vancelian app** (Vancelian acts as counterparty via own-account interposition), or (2) on the **BitMart** exchange where AKTIO is listed publicly. In the Vancelian app, the trading page is accessible via the Buy button, More menu, Crypto section, or bottom navigation. Orders are **market orders only**, capped at **€2,000 per order** (indicative — this cap may be adjusted based on market volatility and available liquidity).

## Where to buy AKTIO

| Channel | How it works | Who it's for |
|---|---|---|
| **Vancelian app** | Vancelian acts as counterparty (own-account interposition). EUR → AKTIO or crypto → AKTIO, settled instantly in your Vancelian wallet. | Clients with a verified Vancelian account (KYC completed). Simplest route. |
| **BitMart exchange** | AKTIO is listed on BitMart as an ERC-20 token. You trade against market liquidity on the exchange. | Users outside the Vancelian app, or clients wishing to trade AKTIO at limit prices on a public exchange. |

See [[where-is-aktio-listed]] for the full list of external listing venues.

## Details — Buying in the Vancelian app
There are several ways to purchase AKTIO tokens on the Vancelian app. All options will take you to the same trading page, where you can select from your current currency (e.g., EUR or another crypto) and to **AKTIO.**

 

#### **1. From the home page**

- On the home screen, just below your Total funds, you will see four quick-access buttons.

- Tap Buy.

- Select the currency you want to exchange from and choose AKTIO as your to currency.

**2. From the “More” button**

- On the home screen, tap More (same row as the Buy button).

- Select Buy.

- Choose your from currency and AKTIO as the to currency.

**3. From the Crypto section on the home page**

- On the home screen, tap Crypto (between Savings Projects and Crypto Baskets).

- Tap Buy +.

- You’ll be redirected to the trading page to select your from currency and to AKTIO.

**4. From the bottom menu**

- In the bottom navigation menu, tap Crypto.

- Search for AKTIO and select it.

- On the coin’s page, tap Buy.

- This opens the trading page where you can complete the purchase.

**Purchase limits and conditions**

- The Vancelian app allows **market orders only** — all AKTIO purchases are executed at the current market price. Limit orders are not supported in the app.
- A **maximum of €2,000 per order** applies (indicative). This cap is a product parameter that Vancelian may adjust based on market volatility and available AKTIO liquidity.
- If you need to place larger orders or use limit pricing, trading AKTIO on **BitMart** is the recommended route — see [[where-is-aktio-listed]] and [[how-to-withdraw-aktio-to-bitmart]].

**Trading fees**

Buying AKTIO tokens is subject to the same transaction fees as other cryptoassets on Vancelian. Estimated fees appear on the validation button before completing the trade. [More information on trading fees.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4419810818193-The-trading-fees)

 

#### **Track your AKTIO tokens**
Once your purchase is complete, you can view your AKTIO tokens in the Crypto section. Here, you can monitor your wallet and track its performance.

 

**If you have not deposited funds into your account, you will be prompted with an error message asking you to make your first deposit before you can start trading AKTIO.*

## Caveats
- The **€2,000 / order cap is indicative** and may be adjusted by Vancelian depending on AKTIO market volatility and available liquidity. Confirm the live cap in the app at the moment of the trade.
- **Market orders only** in the Vancelian app — for limit orders or larger volumes, trade on BitMart.
- Buying AKTIO on Vancelian requires a **verified account (KYC completed)** and an initial fiat or crypto deposit.
- **AKTIO is not withdrawable to an external wallet from the Vancelian app** (EEA clients). You can however **buy, sell, and deposit** AKTIO freely in the app. To exit an AKTIO position and retrieve your capital, **sell AKTIO in-app** against EUR, USDC or another withdrawable asset, then withdraw that asset normally.
- The ICO Launchpad withdrawal route ([[how-to-withdraw-aktio-to-bitmart]]) is reserved for historical ICO investors **outside the EEA** who never had access to the Vancelian app.
