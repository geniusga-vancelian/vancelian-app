## Details
A recurring purchase is an automated investment strategy, also known as **DCA (Dollar Cost Averaging)**. It involves investing a fixed amount at regular intervals (daily, weekly, or monthly), regardless of market fluctuations.

**This approach offers several advantages:**

- Reduced timing risk: By investing regularly, you average out your entry price over time.

- Protection against volatility: In a downturn, you buy more units; in an upturn, you reduce exposure to high prices.

- Investment discipline: This regularity supports a long-term vision, independent of market variations.
 

#### **How do I activate and configure a recurring purchase?**
A recurring purchase can be configured directly from the crypto purchase window, even if you do not yet hold this asset.

- Initiate a crypto-asset **trade** from the application
*For more information on how to perform a crypto-asset exchange, *[*please refer here*](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4574862965393-How-can-I-trade-cryptoassets-on-the-Vancelian-app)

- By default, the selected option is **Instant purchase**

- Click on the** menu icon **(bottom left) then select** Recurring**

**Set up your purchase:**

- Purchase **amount**

- Funding **source:** EUR account or another crypto-asset already held

- Start **date**: Now or manual selection via the calendar

- **Frequency:**

- Every day

- Every week

- Every 15 days

- Every month

- Click **Verify **to display the summary: start date, frequency and any fees.

- Click **Confirm** to validate your recurring purchase plan.
 

#### **Everything you need to know about recurring purchases**

- **Minimum amount:**

A **minimum amount** is required to set up a recurring purchase. This threshold depends on the volatility of the selected crypto-asset and may vary from one crypto-asset to another.

If the entered amount is too low, the required minimum amount will be displayed directly in place of the confirmation button in the application.

- **Notifications:**

A notification will be sent to you **the day before each purchase** to remind you of the details of the upcoming operation.

Another notification will also inform you of **the result of the purchase**, whether it is successful or not.

- **Missed execution:**

If a scheduled purchase cannot be executed, **it will not be rescheduled for that occurrence**. The plan will automatically resume on the **next scheduled date **according to the selected frequency.

However, it is possible to manually retry once from the relevant automation:

**Home page > More > Automations > select the relevant automation**

An **immediate** retry can then be performed.

If this second attempt also fails, it will not be possible to retry a third time for the same occurrence.

- **Suspending a recurring purchase:**

It is possible to **temporarily suspend** a recurring purchase without deleting it. To do so:

**Home page > More > Automations > select the relevant automation > three-dot icon in the top right > Suspend**

A “**Suspended**” tag will then appear in the automation title.

To reactivate the recurring purchase, simply click again on the three dots and select **Resume.**

- **Modifying a recurring purchase:**

You can modify certain parameters of an existing recurring purchase. To access the modification:

**Home page > More > Automations > select the relevant automation > three-dot icon in the top right > Modify**

It is only possible to modify:

- The **amount**

- The **frequency**

The **source**, **currency**, or **selected asset** cannot be modified.

Once the changes have been made, you will need to **review** the information and then **confirm** the modification.

- **Fee discount:**

A **10% reduction** applies to transaction fees for all purchases made in recurring mode.

- **Eligible assets:**

This feature is **available for all cryptocurrencies offered in the application**, with the **exception of USDC.**

- **Cancelling a recurring purchase:**

You can cancel a recurring purchase plan at any time. To do so, go to **the page dedicated to the specific plan**, where its details are displayed, then click **Delete.**
