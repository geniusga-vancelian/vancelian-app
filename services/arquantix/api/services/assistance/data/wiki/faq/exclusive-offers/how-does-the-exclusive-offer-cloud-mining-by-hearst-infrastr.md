# How does the Exclusive Offer – Cloud Mining by Hearst (infrastructure operated by Hearst) work?

> **This fundraising round (€5 million) is now fully subscribed.** New deposits are no longer possible. Existing investors continue to receive daily returns. This is the second Cloud Mining by Hearst collection — the first was the *Eco-Friendly Bitcoin Mining in Ethiopia* round (€2.5M, closed 14 April 2025). See [[how-does-the-exclusive-offer-eco-friendly-mining-in-ethiopia|the Ethiopia offer page]] and [[migration-to-the-new-cloud-mining-program|the migration page]] for history.

## Short answer
The **€5M Cloud Mining by Hearst** round is **fully subscribed — no new deposits possible**; existing investors continue to receive daily payouts in EURC. Commitment is **4 years**, APR is **variable** (currently 2.65% Bronze → 3% Elite) depending on Privilege Club tier. Lock-up is **6 months minimum**, exit windows twice a year with a **5% early-exit fee before 24 months, free after**. Rates are live in the app — always verify in-app. See [[../memberships/privilege-club-tier-benefits|Privilege Club benefits]].

> **Rate is variable** and updated in the Vancelian app. This wiki may not reflect the latest figure — always verify in-app for the live rate. *Future versions of this wiki will connect directly to Vancelian's database for real-time rates.*

## Details
***This fundraising round (€5 million) is now fully subscribed. New deposits are no longer possible. Existing investors continue to receive daily returns credited to their available balance.***

Invest in Bitcoin mining through an international infrastructure optimised for energy efficiency and stable returns, in partnership with [Hearst.](https://www.hearstcorporation.io/)

> **Who does what?** The Cloud Mining programme is operated by **Vancelian LTD** (registered in Abu Dhabi, ADGM), a joint venture between Automata Group LTD (UK, 49 %), Hearst Solution FZCO (49 %), and a private individual (2 %). **Automata France SAS** provides only the exchange, custody, and display services within the Vancelian app — it does not operate the mining infrastructure itself. When you purchase computing power, you are contracting with Vancelian LTD; when you convert rewards or view your balances, those services are delivered by Automata France under its PSAN E2023-087 registration.

- Commitment period: 4 years

- Launch dates:

- Thursday 21 August 2025 for **Elite** and **Platinum** members

- Wednesday 27 August 2025 for **Gold** and **Silver** members

- Wednesday 3 September 2025 for **all** members

- Limited offer: €5 million

- Flexibility: Minimum lock-up period of 6 months. You may deposit and withdraw twice per year.

- Annual yield (APR): Depending on your [Privilege Club](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships) status

- Yield payment: Daily

 

#### **How does the investment cap work?**
Each investor is free to choose the amount they wish to allocate, within the overall limit of the offer.

You can make a deposit starting from **1 EUR** or **approximately 10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**** **

 

#### **Collection status and how to invest**
The collection (collecte) can be in one of two states:

- **Open**: the funding cap (€5M) has not yet been reached. You can deposit directly via the app — no need to wait for a window.
- **Completed / closed** (current status): the €5M cap has been reached. New deposits are only possible during a **deposit window**, which opens after an exit window closes, if exiting investors have freed up capital. The deposit window operates on a first-come, first-served basis and closes automatically once the freed amount is fully subscribed.

Check the offer page in the Vancelian app to see the current collection status.

#### **Exit windows and early withdrawal**
**Exit windows** open **twice a year** (typically May and November), for 2 weeks. Withdrawal requests are only possible during these windows, and only if the contracts have been held for at least **6 months.**

Please note that exit requests are subject to a matching system: for a withdrawal to be processed, a new investor must take over the outgoing contract via the deposit window that follows.

[For more information, please refer to our Terms and Conditions – Exclusive Cloud Mining Offer.](https://vancelian.com/en/terms-conditions-vancelian-platform)
 

#### **Are there any fees associated with withdrawals?**
After the initial **6-month** lock-up period, early exits are possible under preferred conditions, with a **5% fee.**

Whether you wish to exit partially or fully, the request must be made during the designated withdrawal windows.

**After 24 months** of commitment, **no withdrawal fee** applies.

#### **What are the formal exit windows?**
Early exits are concentrated into **two windows per year — May and November**. Investors must submit their request within the deadline communicated by Vancelian. An exit is only validated if **a new investor takes over the outgoing contract** (matched-supply queue, processed in chronological order). Investors who miss a deadline are automatically deferred to the next window; the contract continues normally in the meantime.

#### **How does the commitment clock work?**
**You do not choose your commitment duration.** It is determined by the offer's terms at the time of your deposit, and the Vancelian app shows you the exact commitment period and release date before each deposit. The only way to recover your capital before maturity is to exercise your right of **early exit via an exit window** (see above).

The maturity (end of commitment) depends on **when you subscribed**:

- **Before 24 September 2024**: commitment of **24 months** from your deposit date. Your capital may already be at or near maturity.
- **Between 24 September and 31 December 2024**: commitment of **48 months** from your deposit date. Capital is released on the 1st of the month following expiration.
- **After 31 December 2024** (entry via queue mechanism / deposit window): commitment until **31 December 2028** for all entrants, regardless of entry date.

At maturity, your committed capital is returned automatically — no exit window needed.

For multiple deposits: the commitment applies to **each deposit individually**. If you make a second deposit later, it does **not** extend the clock on your first deposit — the new amount simply boosts your computing power and starts its own clock. Capital is unlocked deposit-by-deposit, independently.

#### **What are the "Power" and "Available" wallets?**
- **Power wallet** — your locked capital, financing the share of computing power that backs your contract.
- **Available wallet** — receives the daily EURC rewards. Withdrawable at any time, no lock-up on the rewards themselves.

#### **What about transferring a contract (family, inheritance)?**
A contract can be transferred to a direct family member or heir, subject to Vancelian's validation and the required supporting documents. The original contract terms continue to apply unchanged.

#### **What is CGUPM?**
**CGUPM** = "Conditions Générales d'Utilisation – Programme de Mining" — the General Terms & Conditions for the Cloud Mining Programme. By subscribing, you accept that the yield is **variable and not guaranteed**, and that capital is locked over the subscription period. Vancelian may update the CGUPM; updates are communicated via email and the client area, and continued use requires acceptance of the new terms.

#### **What about force majeure or service interruption?**
In cases of force majeure (natural disaster, prolonged power outage, conflict) or definitive service cessation, Vancelian cannot guarantee continuity. Clients are notified through official channels, and Vancelian seeks compensation solutions where possible — but continuity is not contractually guaranteed in those scenarios.
