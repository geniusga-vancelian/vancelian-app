# Why is my outgoing transfer pending?

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### **What are the usual processing times?**

Withdrawals and transfers made from your Vancelian account are processed via the **SEPA** network and may take up to **5 business days** to be credited to the recipient’s account.

This timeframe depends on the banks involved, the date of the transfer (for example, one made at the end of the week or before a public holiday), and any ongoing compliance checks.

During this period, the transaction may appear as “**pending**” in your application.

**What are the possible reasons for a withdrawal or transfer being pending?**

- Normal interbank processing times between financial institutions.

- Compliance verification in progress to confirm the origin or destination of the funds.

- Non-compliant recipient account (for example, a non-SEPA account or one in a currency other than euro).

- Incorrect banking details entered (invalid IBAN or beneficiary).

**What should I do if the transfer has not been received?**

Before contacting our Customer Support, please check with the recipient’s bank or the account holder to confirm:

- That the transfer has been received or is being processed.

- That the bank details provided (IBAN, BIC, account holder name) are correct.

If the beneficiary still hasn’t received the funds after 5 business days, please contact our Support Team for verification.

**Information to provide to Customer Support**

To help us process your request efficiently, please include the following information:

- Date and time of the operation

- Exact amount sent

- Name and IBAN of the recipient account

- Full proof of transaction (in PDF format, including the transfer reference)

You can find out how to [contact Customer Support here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

**Good to know**

Transfers made on Friday afternoons, or before a weekend or public holiday, may be delayed until the next business day.
