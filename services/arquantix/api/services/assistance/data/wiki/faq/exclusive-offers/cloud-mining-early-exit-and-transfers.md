# Cloud Mining: how do early exits and contract transfers work?

## Short answer
Cloud Mining capital is locked until contract maturity (**31 December 2028** for all new and migrated positions; pre-migration contracts retain their original maturity, visible in your client account). Early exits are concentrated into **two windows per year — May and November**. Before 24 months, an exit costs a **5% fee**; after 24 months, exit is **free**. In all cases, an exit only completes when **a new investor takes over your contract** (matched-supply queue, processed in chronological order). Contracts can also be transferred to a direct family member or heir, subject to Vancelian validation.

## Details

### Early exit windows
- **Two per year**: May and November.
- Investors must submit their request within the deadline communicated by Vancelian for each window.
- Exits are validated only when a new investor takes over the outgoing contract.

### Exit fee schedule
- **0–24 months**: 5% fee on the exited amount.
- **24+ months**: no fee.

The 6-month minimum lock-up at the start of the contract still applies (no early exit possible in the first 6 months).

### How the matched-supply queue works
- Outgoing requests are processed **in chronological order** of submission.
- An investor who requests an exit during a window will only see it executed if a compatible new entrant subscribes within that window.
- If no replacement is found, the investor stays in the contract until the next window. The contract continues to generate daily rewards normally during the wait.
- Massive simultaneous exit demand can lengthen waiting times — first-come, first-served.

### Collection status and new deposits
The Cloud Mining collection (total target: €5 million) can be in one of two states:
- **Open**: the funding cap has not yet been reached. You can deposit directly via the app at any time.
- **Completed / closed**: the cap has been reached. New deposits are only possible during a **deposit window**, which opens after an exit window closes, if exiting investors have freed up capital. The deposit window operates on a first-come, first-served basis.

Check the offer page in the Vancelian app to see the current collection status. Even when the collection is closed, **exit windows continue to operate normally** for existing investors.

### Exit window duration
Each exit window stays open for **2 weeks**. You must submit your exit request within this period.

### Late or missed requests
If you miss a window's deadline, your request is **automatically deferred to the next window** (May or November). The contract continues normally in the meantime.

### Transferring a contract to family or an heir
A contract can be transferred to a **direct family member or legal heir**. Conditions:
- Vancelian must validate the transfer.
- The required supporting documents (proof of relationship, KYC of the new holder) must be provided.
- The original contract terms (commitment period, fee schedule, CGUPM) continue to apply unchanged.

### Reinvestment of rewards
EURC rewards credited daily to the **Available wallet** are unlocked. They can be:
- Withdrawn as EUR or USDC at any time.
- Reinvested into other Vancelian products.
- Converted into BTC or other supported cryptocurrencies (recommended by the team to optimise long-term yield given Bitcoin's structural scarcity).

A reinvestment **into a new Cloud Mining position** automatically opens a new commitment under the current CGUPM, with maturity set at 31 December 2028.

## Caveats
- Cloud Mining is **structurally illiquid**. Treat it as a long-horizon commitment.
- Exit completion is not guaranteed within a given window — it depends on matched demand from new entrants.
- For all contractual questions, refer to the latest CGUPM accessible from your Vancelian client area, or contact customer support.
