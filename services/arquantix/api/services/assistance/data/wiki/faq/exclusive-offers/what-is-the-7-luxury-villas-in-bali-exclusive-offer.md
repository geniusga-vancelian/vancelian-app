# What is the 7 luxury villas in Bali exclusive offer?

## Short answer
This offer is closed as of 6 October 2025 (€4M cap reached) — no new deposits are accepted. The 7-villa project at Munduk, Bali is scheduled for delivery end-2026 and will enter a rental phase 2026–2029 before resale 2028–2029. Existing investors continue to receive daily EURC returns throughout the 4-year commitment period.

## Details
As of Monday, 6 October 2025, the exclusive offer for 7 luxury villas in Bali, with a total amount of €4 million, was fully reached.

As a result, the offer is now closed, and it is no longer possible to make new deposits into this exclusive offer via the app.

For all clients who currently hold this offer, daily returns will continue to be credited directly to your available balance within the project.

 

This offer launched by Vancelian opens up new perspectives in terms of financing and investments in** real estate** development through crypto.

Participate in the crypto asset financing of a luxury real estate project (7 luxury villas) currently under development in **Bali,** at the heart of a rapidly growing market.

Unlike traditional solutions, where the investment value is in euros, this investment is made in a fully crypto environment while being **preserved from fluctuations** in the cryptocurrency market.

Deposits in this offer are made in EUR or USDC, converted to **BTC fixed at the EUR value** at the time of the transaction to avoid the risk of BTC fluctuations throughout the project's duration.

The distribution of earnings is done automatically in **BTC, converted to EURC** to preserve against volatility, at the value of the transaction day.

You can find more details about this offer in the brochure.
