# How to add a cryptoasset to your favourites

## Short answer
To add a cryptoasset to your favourites, simply go to the page of the cryptoasset concerned.  There are several ways to get there: **Once you are on the cryptoasset page, you can add it to your favourites by tapping the star icon in the top right corner.** If the cryptoasset is already in your favourites, the star will appear filled.

## Details
To add a cryptoasset to your favourites, simply go to the page of the cryptoasset concerned. There are several ways to get there:

 

#### 1. From the Crypto section

-  
- Tap Crypto in the shortcuts at the bottom of the screen

 

#### 2. From the Products section

-  

- Tap Products in the shortcuts at the bottom of the screen (on the right)

- Select Cryptoassets

- Tap the title Cryptoassets Market

 

#### 3. From the home page

-  

- Tap Crypto (between Savings Projects and Crypto baskets)

- Then select the Buy + button

 

#### 4. From the search bar

-  
- Search directly for **the name** of the cryptoasset in the **search bar** at the top of the application

**Once you are on the cryptoasset page, you can add it to your favourites by tapping the star icon in the top right corner.** If the cryptoasset is already in your favourites, the star will appear filled.

Adding cryptoassets to your favourites makes it easier to find them later in the cryptoassets lists within the app.

To **remove a cryptoasset from your favourites,** simply tap the star again and it will be removed from the list.
