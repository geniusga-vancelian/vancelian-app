# What fees apply to bank transfers?

## Short answer
The fees applied to bank transfers depend on the direction of the transfer (incoming or outgoing) and your membership status. Incoming bank transfers

## Details
The fees applied to bank transfers depend on the direction of the transfer (incoming or outgoing) and your membership status.

**Incoming bank transfers**

All incoming bank transfers are **free of charge**, regardless of your membership status.

**Outgoing bank transfers**

Fees for outgoing bank transfers depend on your status.

**Status**
**Bronze**
**Silver**
**Gold**
**Platinum**
**Elite**

**Fee**

For Bronze and Silver members, the €1 fee is a **fixed amount** and does not depend on the transfer value.

You can upgrade your status through our [Privilege Club](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club) to reach Gold level or above and benefit from fee-free outgoing transfers.

**Where can I find the full list of fees?**

All applicable fees are available on our website:
