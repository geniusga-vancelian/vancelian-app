# What is a Crypto Basket?

## Short answer
Rather than manually choosing each asset, the user invests in a predefined set of cryptocurrencies grouped within one basket.  Each basket is designed to reflect a **specific investment strategy**, such as exposure to market leaders, thematic diversification, or a strategic reserve in digital assets.

## Details
#### **What is a Crypto Basket?**
A **Crypto Basket** is a simple investment solution that allows you to buy, in a single operation, several cryptocurrencies selected for their solidity and market dominance.

Rather than manually choosing each asset, the user invests in a predefined set of cryptocurrencies grouped within one basket.

Each basket is designed to reflect a **specific investment strategy**, such as exposure to market leaders, thematic diversification, or a strategic reserve in digital assets.

#### **Who is this product for?**
Crypto Baskets are suitable for both **beginner and experienced investors.**

They are ideal for those who wish to invest in the long term without managing each cryptocurrency individually.

This concept is particularly suited to investors seeking a **passive, disciplined, and diversified** approach to the crypto market.
