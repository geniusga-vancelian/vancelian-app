# How can I view or change the PIN of my card?

## Short answer
Your PIN code is personal and confidential. You can view or change it at any time from your Vancelian application. Where can I find my PIN code?

## Details
Your PIN code is **personal** and** confidential.** You can view or change it at any time from your Vancelian application.

**Where can I find my PIN code?**

If you have forgotten your PIN code, you can view it directly from the app:

- Go to the **Card** section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Click on **Show card pin**

**Important:**

Never share your PIN code with anyone.

It is strictly personal and will never be requested by Vancelian or its partners.

**How can I change my PIN code?**

You can also change your PIN code from your application:

- Go to the **Card **section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Select **Change PIN code**

- Enter your **current PIN**

- Choose a new **PIN code** and confirm it

- **To finalise the update, make a withdrawal at an ATM using the new PIN**

**Good to know**

- If no withdrawal is made after changing your PIN, the old PIN will remain active.

- For security reasons, avoid simple combinations (e.g., 0000 or 1234).

- Customer support will never ask you for your PIN by email or phone.
