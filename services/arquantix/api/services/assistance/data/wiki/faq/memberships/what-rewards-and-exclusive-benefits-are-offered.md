# What rewards and exclusive benefits are offered?

## Short answer
The Privilege Club rewards you with higher savings rates, lower trading and card fees, increased card limits, and access to exclusive offers—all scaling up from Bronze to Elite tier. Platinum and Elite members also get priority customer support with responses under one hour. Visit your Profile section in the app to see your tier-specific benefits.

## Details
The Vancelian Privilege Club rewards members for their loyalty through exclusive benefits, optimised returns, and preferential conditions.

These advantages evolve according to your loyalty level, from Bronze status up to Elite.

What are the main benefits?

- Optimised savings ratesAccess enhanced returns on our various savings products, depending on your level within the Privilege Club.

- Reduced transaction feesEnjoy discounts on payment card fees, trading operations, and the management of your investment products.

- Increased card limitsBenefit from higher payment and withdrawal limits, offering greater flexibility in the use of your funds.

- Access to Exclusive OffersCertain projects and investment opportunities are offered exclusively to a select group of members.

- Dedicated customer serviceFrom the Platinum level onwards, you receive priority support, with a response time of under one hour during operating hours.

Rates, fees, limits, and benefits by level are available at any time on [our website](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships) and directly in the app under your Profile section.
