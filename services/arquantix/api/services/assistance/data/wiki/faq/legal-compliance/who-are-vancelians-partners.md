# Who are Vancelian’s partners?

## Short answer
Vancelian partners with Modulr (authorised electronic money institution handling EUR payments and safeguarding), Fireblocks (institutional-grade MPC technology used to secure crypto-asset wallets, where the depositary role is operated by Automata France SAS in Europe or Automata FZE in the UAE), and Stripe (online payment processing). For full details, see the Terms and Conditions on the Vancelian website. The complete custody architecture is documented in [[custody-architecture]].

## Details
**Our partners are as follows:**

- **Modulr Finance B.V.**, an authorised and regulated electronic money institution. Automata France is an outsourced service provider of Modulr and offers payment services to its clients through its partner Modulr (regulated as an electronic money institution by De Nederlandsche Bank (DNB)). Client EUR balances are held in **segregated accounts** at Modulr.

- **Fireblocks**, a leading provider of **MPC (Multi-Party Computation) custody technology**. The depositary role for client crypto-assets is operated by **Automata France SAS** (PSAN E2023-087, MiCA scope) in Europe or **Automata FZE** (VARA In-Principle Approval) in the UAE ; Fireblocks supplies the underlying key-management and transaction-orchestration infrastructure. The combination enables institutional-grade security (no single party holds a complete private key) while keeping the depositary responsibility with the regulated entities.

- **Stripe**, a company providing online payment services.

For more details, please consult our [Terms and Conditions](https://vancelian.com/en/terms-conditions) on our website or directly in the application.
