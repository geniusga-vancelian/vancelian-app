# How to deposit cryptoassets on the Vancelian app

## Short answer
To deposit crypto on Vancelian, complete KYC (Level 1) and proof of residence (Level 2) verification first. Select your asset and network (Ethereum ERC-20 or Base for USDC), copy your dedicated wallet address or scan the QR code, and send funds from your external wallet—confirmations typically take several hours depending on network activity.

## Details
Before you can deposit cryptoassets into your Vancelian account, your registration must be fully verified:

- Level 1 (KYC) validated

- Level 2 (Proof of residence) validated

Steps to make a cryptoasset deposit

- From the Home page, tap Deposit.

- Select Blockchain Deposit.

- Choose the **cryptoasset** you want to deposit.

You can consult the list of accepted cryptoassets [here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16583610575761-Which-crypto-asset-can-I-deposit-on-the-Vancelian-application)

- Select the available **network:**

Ethereum (ERC-20): all cryptoassets from [this list](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16583610575761-Which-crypto-asset-can-I-deposit-on-the-Vancelian-application) can be deposited via the Ethereum network.
BASE: only available for USDC deposits.

- Once the cryptoasset and network are selected, your **dedicated wallet address** will be displayed.

- You can copy it or scan the **QR code** to enter it in your external wallet from which you are making the transfer.

- By clicking Share your address, you can also share it if needed.

This address is unique to your Vancelian wallet. It functions like a bank account number, composed of unique letters and numbers.

**Processing time**

The confirmation time for a deposit may vary depending on network activity. In some cases, the operation can take several hours before it appears in your balance.

You can track the status of your transaction directly from the external platform used. If the status shows “failed” or “unconfirmed”, please contact that platform directly.

**Important points to remember**

- To consult the full list of accepted cryptoassets, please refer to the article [here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16583610575761-Which-crypto-asset-can-I-deposit-on-the-Vancelian-application)

- Only Ethereum (ERC-20) and BASE (USDC only) networks are accepted.

- Deposits sent from a **different network** or for a **cryptoasset not supported** will be **permanently lost and cannot be recovered.**

- We recommend always making a **small initial transfer** to familiarise yourself with the system before sending a larger amount.

Warning: if you send a cryptoasset on an unsupported network, or transfer a cryptoasset that is not available on our app, your funds will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.It is therefore essential to always verify and select the correct network when making deposits and withdrawals.
