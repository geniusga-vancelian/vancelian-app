## Details
For **security reasons,** screenshots are **disabled by default.** You can **temporarily** allow access to screenshots **until the next time the app is locked.**

By default, any attempt to take a screenshot in the app will result in a blank image. **This automatic restriction is in place to protect your personal information.**
 

#### **If you wish to temporarily enable this feature, follow these steps:**

- Go to the Profile shortcut from within the app.

- Scroll down to the Settings section.

- Select Security and Privacy.

- Tap on Enable Temporary Screenshot.

Once activated, you’ll be able to take screenshots or record your screen – for example, to show certain features to a friend or share specific information with our support team.

 

*Please note: screenshots may contain sensitive information (such as login details, balances, or transactions). Only share them with people you trust.*
