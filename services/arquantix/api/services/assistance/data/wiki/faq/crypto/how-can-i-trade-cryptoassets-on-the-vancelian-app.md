# How can I trade cryptoassets on the Vancelian app?

## Short answer
There are several ways to start trading cryptoassets on the Vancelian app. All options take you to the same trading page, where you select a “from” currency (the one you exchange) and a “to” currency (the one you receive). Automata France SAS acts as your sole counterparty on every trade — this own-account interposition model means you always deal directly with a regulated entity, not with other users or an anonymous order book.

## Details
There are several ways to start trading cryptoassets on the Vancelian app. All options will take you to the same trading page, where you can select your “from” currency (the one you want to exchange) and your “to” currency (the one you want to receive).
 

#### **1. From the home page**

- On the home screen, just below your Total funds, you will see four quick-access buttons.

- Tap Buy.

- Select your “**from**” currency and your “**to**” currency to set up the trade

**2. From the “More” button**

- On the home screen, tap More (same row as the Buy button).

- Select Buy.

- Choose your “**from**” and “**to**” currencies to continue your trade.

 

#### **3. From the Crypto section on the home page**

- On the home screen, tap Crypto (between Savings Projects and Crypto Baskets).

- Then tap Buy +.

- You’ll be redirected to the trading page where you can select your “**from**” and “**to**” currencies.

 

#### **4. From the bottom menu**

- In the bottom navigation menu, tap Crypto.

- Search for and select the coin you want.

- Once on the coin’s page, tap Buy or Sell.

- This will open the trading page where you can complete your exchange.

**Set up a recurring purchase**

From the crypto purchase window, it is also possible to set up a **recurring purchase.**

In the trading window, click on the **menu icon**, then select **Recurring** to configure your automatic investment plan.

This feature allows you to schedule regular purchases based on a defined amount and frequency.

To learn more about the configuration steps and available settings, please refer to the dedicated article:

[ How do I set up a recurring purchase (DCA) in the application? ](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/34653043561617-How-to-set-up-a-recurring-purchase-DCA-in-the-app)
 

#### **How your trade is executed**

When you place a trade on Vancelian, **Automata France SAS** is your direct counterparty. This is called an **own-account interposition model** (MiCA Article 78): Automata France buys or sells the cryptoasset on its own account and delivers it to you, rather than matching you with another user.

Behind the scenes, Automata France sources liquidity from regulated venues — primarily **Scrypt** (Swiss VQF/FINMA-regulated, handling ~65 % of transactions) and **BitMart**. A best-execution policy ensures the price and speed you receive meet regulatory standards.

**Settlement** works as follows: EUR movements are immediate (via Modulr segregated accounts), while crypto transfers are settled by end of day into your Vancelian-custodied wallet (key security via the Fireblocks MPC technology).

For a deeper explanation, see [How does Vancelian execute my crypto trades?](how-does-vancelian-execute-trades.md).

#### **Trading fees**
Trading cryptoassets on Vancelian is subject to transaction fees.

You can find all the details about fees [**here.**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4419810818193-The-trading-fees)

 

#### **Track your crypto portfolio**
Once your trade is complete, you can consult your holdings at any time in the Crypto section. There you can view your wallet and monitor its performance.

 

**If you have not deposited funds into your account, you will be prompted with an error message asking you to make your first deposit before you can start trading.*
