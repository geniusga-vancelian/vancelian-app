# How can I reinvest my returns into other projects?

## Short answer
For **Exclusive Offers** (such as Cloud Mining or real estate projects), you can reallocate all or part of your available balance to other projects directly from the Vancelian app. Reinvestment is possible only towards **Flexible Vaults** and the **Future Vault** — not into other Exclusive Offers.

## Details
**Which projects are concerned?**

Reinvestment is possible only towards:

- **Flexible Vaults**

- **The Future Vault**

It is not possible to reinvest into an Exclusive Offer.

**How do I reinvest from an Exclusive Offer?**

- Go to the relevant **Exclusive Offer** project

- Identify the **available balance**

- Click on **Withdraw** in the shortcuts

- A new page is displayed, offering the following destinations:
- Standard options (EUR or USDC)
- Eligible projects (Flexible or Future)

- Enter **all or part** of the available amount to reinvest

- Confirm the operation by clicking **Confirm**

When selecting a withdrawal to a **project**, the amount is transferred directly to the selected project.

**How can I use returns as a source when making a deposit?**

- Go to a **Flexible** or **Future** vault

- Click on **Deposit**

- The available sources include:
- Standard options (EUR or USDC)
- Eligible projects (Exclusive Offers)

- Select the **source project** and the **amount **to use

- Confirm the operation by clicking **Confirm**

You can therefore use an amount originating from an existing project to fund a **Flexible or Future vault.**
 

#### **Please note**

- Reinvestment is possible only using **available amounts**. Exclusive Offers include committed capital, subject to a lock-up period, which cannot be transferred before it is unlocked.

- Reinvestment is only possible towards **Flexible** or **Future** projects.

- The **Future** project involves a **12-month commitment** on deposited amounts.
