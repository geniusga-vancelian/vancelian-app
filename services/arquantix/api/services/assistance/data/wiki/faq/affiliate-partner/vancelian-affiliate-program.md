# Vancelian Affiliate Program

## Short answer
Vancelian's **Affiliate Program** is for **content creators and influencers** who want to monetise their audience by promoting Vancelian's savings, crypto and exclusive offers. Affiliates earn **a percentage of transaction fees and deposits** from their referees, paid **monthly**, with **no earning cap**. The program has supported **400+ collaborations in 2 years** and provides each affiliate with a dedicated account manager, a campaign-tracking dashboard, marketing assets, and Elite-level platform benefits (free metal card, reduced fees, boosted yields).

## Details

### Who it's for
**Content creators and influencers** with an audience interested in finance, crypto, or wealth management.

### How to join
1. Submit an application on [vancelian.com/en/private-partners/register](https://vancelian.com/en/private-partners/register) (form takes <2 minutes).
2. Vancelian's affiliate team reviews and onboards.
3. Create content showcasing Vancelian's solutions.
4. Earn monthly commissions on referee activity.

### Earnings
- **Percentage of transaction fees and deposits** made by referees on specific features.
- **No earning cap** — commission grows with the size of your network.
- Paid **monthly**.

### Benefits for affiliates
- **Dedicated account manager** + experienced customer support team
- **Real-time campaign tracking dashboard**
- **Marketing toolkit:** optimised advertising materials, complete media kit with guides, personalised marketing visuals
- **Elite-level platform perks** for the affiliate themselves: free metal card, reduced fees, boosted yields, premium platform conditions

### Why creators join
- Vancelian is **registered with the AMF** (DASP E2023-087)
- **Made in France**
- **Optimal asset protection** through regulated custody
- **Dedicated support service**

## Caveats
- The percentage of commission is not publicly disclosed on the affiliate landing page — the structure is "share of transaction fees and deposits" and depends on the specific features used by referees. Confirm the exact rate with the affiliate team.
- Affiliates promoting investment products must comply with their local jurisdiction's rules on investment promotion. Vancelian provides no legal advice on this.
- The 400+ collaborations figure is from the live website snapshot — verify before quoting publicly.
