# How can I invest in a closed exclusive offer via a deposit window?

## Short answer
Even when an exclusive offer has reached its funding cap and is marked as **closed**, you can still invest during a **deposit window**. Deposit windows open **twice a year** (typically in May and November), **after the exit window closes** — once Vancelian knows exactly how much has been freed up. The collection reopens in the app on a **first-come, first-served** basis until the freed amount is fully subscribed, then it closes automatically. Vancelian teams notify investors **by email** ahead of each deposit window opening.

## Details
When an exclusive offer (such as Cloud Mining by Hearst, 7 Luxury Villas in Bali, or Dubai Villa Al Barari) is fully subscribed, initial deposits are no longer available. However, **deposit windows** open periodically, giving new investors the chance to enter.

### How the mechanism works
The process has two sequential phases:

1. **Exit window opens** (2 weeks) — existing investors submit their exit requests. Requests are queued first-come, first-served. Once the window closes, all requests become final and Vancelian knows the **total amount freed up**.
2. **Deposit window opens** (after the exit window closes) — the freed amount is displayed as a new collection on the relevant offer's page in the app. New or existing investors can deposit on a **first-come, first-served** basis. Once the collection is fully subscribed, it **closes automatically**.

Key points:

- **Frequency**: twice a year, at the sole discretion of Vancelian (typically May and November)
- **Notification**: the Vancelian team sends an **email notification** to investors both ahead of and at the opening of each deposit window. There is no automated in-app alert or waiting list at this time.
- **Amount available**: corresponds exactly to the total amount freed by exiting investors during the preceding exit window
- **Partial deposits**: you can invest any amount up to the remaining available collection — you do not need to match a specific exiting investor's full position

### What you take over
When you invest via a deposit window, you are taking over the **remaining commitment** of the exiting investor. This means:

- You are bound by the **same end date** as the original project (the Duration does not reset)
- You benefit from the same **interest rate** as other participants
- Your funds are locked under the **same conditions** (6-month minimum before being eligible for an exit yourself)

### How to invest during a deposit window

1. **Watch for the email** — the Vancelian team notifies investors by email before the deposit window opens
2. **Open the app** — once the collection is live, go to the **page of the relevant exclusive offer** in the Vancelian app. The available collection amount is displayed
3. **Deposit** — select the amount you wish to invest (minimum thresholds apply per offer) and confirm
4. **Act quickly** — deposits fill the collection on a first-come, first-served basis. Once the freed amount is fully subscribed, the collection closes automatically

### When are the next deposit windows?
Deposit windows are opened at the **sole discretion of Vancelian**, typically in **May and November**, right after the corresponding exit window closes. The exact dates and eligible offers are communicated by email and visible in the app.

## Requirements / Documents
- Active Vancelian account with completed KYC
- Funds available in EUR or USDC
- Access to the Vancelian mobile app during the deposit window period

## Costs
- **No entry fee** is charged to the incoming investor (fees apply only to exiting investors)
- Standard conversion fees may apply if depositing in a different currency than the offer's denomination

> Indicative — verify in the app or contact Vancelian support to help locate the fee documentation for the specific offer.

## Caveats
- **No guarantee of availability**: if no investor exits during an exit window, there is nothing to collect. Deposit windows depend entirely on exit demand.
- **Remaining duration only**: you take over the remaining commitment — you do not get a fresh full-term period.
- **First-come, first-served**: the collection can fill up quickly. There is no reservation system — you need to deposit while the collection is still open.
- **No automated alerts yet**: notification is done by email by the Vancelian team. Make sure your email address is up to date in your account.
- **Not all offers may have deposit windows**: windows are opened at Vancelian's discretion and depend on the specific offer's terms.
- For any specific question about an upcoming deposit window, contact **support@vancelian.com**.
