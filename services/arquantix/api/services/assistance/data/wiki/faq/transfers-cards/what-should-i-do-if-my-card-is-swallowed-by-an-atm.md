# What should I do if my card is swallowed by an ATM?

## Short answer
If your card has been retained by a cash machine (ATM), Vancelian unfortunately cannot intervene to retrieve it. What should you do in this case?

## Details
If your card has been retained by a cash machine (ATM), **Vancelian unfortunately cannot intervene to retrieve it.**

**What should you do in this case?**

- **Identify the bank that owns the ATM**
*The name of the bank is usually displayed on the machine or near the screen*

- **Go to the branch** associated with that ATM
*Bring a valid identity document and explain the situation*
*Some banks may return the card after verifying your identity*

- **If the bank is unable to access the ATM** (common for independent or unattended cash machines), it will **not be possible to retrieve the card.**
*In this case, *[*immediately block your card *](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8669636395537-How-do-I-freeze-my-card)*from your Vancelian app to prevent any fraudulent use.*
