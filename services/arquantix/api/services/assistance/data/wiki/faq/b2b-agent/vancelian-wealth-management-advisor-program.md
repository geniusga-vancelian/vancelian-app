# Vancelian for Wealth Management Advisors

## Short answer
Vancelian offers a dedicated program for **independent wealth management advisors (CGP/IFA)** who want to add Vancelian's savings, exclusive offers and crypto products to their client offering. Partner advisors get a **dedicated team**, **priority and accelerated client onboarding**, **VIP support** for their clients, a **private dashboard** to track client activity and commissions in real time, and **commissions on every product** their clients use (savings, exclusive offers, trading) — structured as a percentage of interest paid, an upfront payment, or recurring remuneration depending on the product.

## Details

### Who it's for
**Independent wealth management advisors** (Conseillers en Gestion de Patrimoine in France, IFAs internationally) looking to extend their client offering with regulated digital-asset and high-yield savings products.

### What you get as an advisor
- **Dedicated expert team** for collaboration and onboarding
- **Priority and accelerated onboarding** for your clients
- **VIP client support** from day one
- **Private dashboard** to view client activity, performance, and your commissions in real time
- **Marketing/educational content** to support clients clearly and professionally

### Products you can offer your clients
- **Exclusive Offers** — funding and investments in digital assets, backed by real-world projects (real estate, mining)
- **Crypto Savings (Flexible & Future Vaults)** — yields up to **8.3% per year** (Future Vault, Elite tier)
- **Cryptocurrency trading** — 50+ cryptos, manual or automated portfolio
- **Euro account + payment card** — IBAN + VISA card

### Commission structure
Commission depends on the product, and may take the form of:
- A **percentage of the interest paid** to your clients
- An **upfront payment**
- **Recurring remuneration**

### How to apply
Contact a client manager at [vancelian.com/en/partner/register](https://vancelian.com/en/partner/register).

## Caveats
- Specific commission rates per product are not published on the partner landing page — they are agreed during onboarding with the partnerships team.
- Wealth management advisors must comply with their local regulator's rules on intermediation, advice, and disclosure of conflicts of interest. Vancelian provides no legal or regulatory advice on this.
- This program is distinct from the [[../affiliate-partner/vancelian-affiliate-program]] (which is aimed at content creators and influencers, not regulated advisors).
