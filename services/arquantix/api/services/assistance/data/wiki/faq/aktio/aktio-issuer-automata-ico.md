# Who issued AKTIO? Automata ICO Limited

## Short answer
AKTIO was issued by **Automata ICO Limited**, an Irish company (Reg. No. 690280) based in Dublin and Cork. It is a **wholly-owned subsidiary of Automata Group (UK)**, the holding company behind the Vancelian brand. Automata ICO is **registered with the Central Bank of Ireland (CBI)** as a Virtual Asset Service Provider (VASP) for AML/CFT purposes.

## Details

### Issuer identity
- **Legal name:** Automata ICO Limited
- **Registration number:** 690280 (Ireland)
- **Registered address:** 3rd Floor Ormond Building, 31-36 Ormond Quay Upper, Dublin 7, D07 Ee37, Ireland
- **Managing Director:** Stephane Perrin
- **Parent company:** Automata Group (UK) — 100% ownership
- **Jurisdiction for AKTIO:** Ireland

### Regulatory framework
- **VASP registration with the Central Bank of Ireland** for AML (Anti-Money Laundering) and CFT (Counter-Terrorism Financing) purposes, under the Criminal Justice (Money Laundering and Terrorist Financing) Amendment Act 2021.
- Commenced business in Ireland on **12 March 2021**.
- Automata ICO Limited holds a legal note explicitly qualifying AKTIO as a **utility token** — it is not a share and not a regulated financial investment product.

### Relationship to Vancelian
- Vancelian is the **current commercial brand** of the Automata Group ecosystem (previously Automata → Akt.io → RAYN → Vancelian since Q3/Q4 2024).
- Vancelian provides the mobile application and services through which clients can acquire, hold, lock and withdraw AKTIO.
- Automata ICO Limited remains the **sole issuer**; Vancelian is an operator of services using AKTIO.

### Position within group evolution
A new **Vancelian Group ADGM** (Abu Dhabi) holding is being set up. **Automata ICO Limited is not moving to the new ADGM holding** — it remains under Automata Group UK. This preserves the Irish regulatory base of the AKTIO token.

## Caveats
- Automata ICO Limited's CBI registration is an **AML/CFT registration**, not a banking licence or a prudential authorisation. It is specifically related to VASP obligations.
- AKTIO is decentralised on Ethereum: the token itself operates on public blockchain infrastructure and is not reliant on Automata ICO for technical operation.
