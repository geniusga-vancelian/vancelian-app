# What is the "Eco-friendly Bitcoin Mining in Ethiopia" exclusive offer?


## Short answer
This offer is closed as of 14 April 2025 (€2.5M cap reached) — no new deposits are accepted via the app. The Ethiopia mining site at Addis Ababa uses 100% renewable hydroelectric energy and Bitmain S21 Pro miners. Existing investors receive daily EURC rewards credited to their available balance, withdrawable in EUR or USDC (minimum €10). **Vancelian LTD (UAE-ADGM)** is the contractual counterparty; **Automata France SAS** is the PSAN-regulated intermediary; **Hearst Solution FZCO** runs the physical infrastructure.

## Details

### Who stands behind your contract?
**Vancelian LTD (UAE-ADGM)** is the **contractual counterparty** of the Cloud Mining programme. **Automata France SAS** operates as the **PSAN-regulated intermediary** for European clients (subscription flow, EURC custody via Fireblocks, app display). **Hearst Solution FZCO** is the **technical operator** on the ground, running the mining infrastructure in Addis Ababa.
*As of Monday, 14 April 2025, the exclusive offer for eco-friendly Bitcoin Mining in Ethiopia, with a total amount of €2.5 million, was fully reached following its availability to Elite members (on 7th April) and Platinum members (on 14th April).*

*As a result, the offer is now closed, and it is no longer possible to make new deposits into this project via the app.*

*For all clients who currently hold this offer, daily returns will continue to be credited directly to your available balance within the project.*

 

The mining site, located in Addis Ababa, **Ethiopia**, and powered by one of Africa’s largest dams, operates with **100% renewable energy.**

This opportunity is led by the [company Hearst](https://www.hearstcorporation.io/) and offers participation in Bitcoin mining using the latest generation **Bitmain miners.**

As an investor in this project, you participate in the crypto financing of **cutting-edge Bitcoin mining** infrastructure.

Your investment is backed by a share in the deployment and operation of Bitmain S21 Pro miners in Ethiopia.

In return for this contribution, you receive a daily reward linked to a portion of the mined bitcoins, **which is converted and paid in EURC** to your available balance.

EURC is a stable cryptocurrency tied to the Euro.

These earnings can be withdrawn in **EUR or USDC** once your balance reaches a minimum of €10 or the equivalent in USDC.
