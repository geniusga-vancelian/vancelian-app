# An unauthorised payment was made with my card

## Short answer
If you notice an unauthorised payment on your Vancelian card, act immediately: (1) block the card in the app via **Card > Block card**, (2) check whether a relative or someone with access to your card could be responsible, (3) contact the merchant directly for clarification or refund, then (4) email `support@vancelian.com` so we can investigate with our payment partner.

## Process
1. **Block the card immediately** — open the app, go to **Card > Block card**. This prevents any further unauthorised use while the investigation is ongoing.
2. **Quick internal check** — confirm the transaction was not made by a family member or someone with legitimate access to your physical card or details (recurring subscription, delegated purchase, etc.).
3. **Contact the merchant** — reach out to the merchant of the disputed transaction to request clarification or, if appropriate, a direct refund. In many cases, merchant-level resolution is the fastest path.
4. **Contact Vancelian** — email `support@vancelian.com` with the transaction reference, date, amount and any context. Our team will liaise with our payment partner to investigate and, where applicable, initiate a chargeback.

## Requirements
When contacting support, include:
- Transaction date and time
- Merchant name as it appears on the statement
- Amount
- Whether the card was in your possession
- Any supporting evidence (emails, receipts, screenshots)

## Caveats
- Blocking the card in-app is instant; however, any transactions already authorised at the terminal may still post for a short period.
- Chargeback outcomes depend on the card scheme rules (VISA) and the investigation conducted by our payment partner.
- If your card has been lost or stolen physically, also follow the steps in [[what-should-i-do-if-my-card-is-lost-or-stolen]].
