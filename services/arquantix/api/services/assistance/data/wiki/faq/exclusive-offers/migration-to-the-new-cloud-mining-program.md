# Migration to the new Cloud Mining program

## Short answer
Eligible clients holding Blockchain or Cloud Mining vaults can migrate to the new mining program, now operated by VANCELIAN LTD (UAE subsidiary). Migration benefits include enhanced returns with daily payouts, improved stability, and simplified app-based management. Your original entry date is preserved for exit window calculations, and existing history is fully retained.

## Details
As part of Vancelian’s upgraded infrastructure, clients who have subscribed to the **Blockchain** and **Cloud Mining** vaults can migrate to the new mining project, powered by green electricity and optimised for improved performance.

This migration is part of the enhancement of our infrastructure, now operated by our dedicated subsidiary **VANCELIAN LTD**, registered in the United Arab Emirates. This change allows us to offer a more **stable, secure, and high-performing** environment, while maintaining our commitment to **transparency** and **reliability.**

 

#### What are the benefits of this migration?

- An enhanced return rate, with daily interest payments.

- An optimised hosting structure for improved stability and security.

- Simplified investment management, directly through the app.

#### How does the Blockchain vault migration work?

- Deposits will be transferred to a new vault with a **4-year commitment.**

- The **original entry date** will be maintained for the calculation of exit windows.

- If you have been part of the project for over **2 years,** you will be able to exit **free of charge** during the next open period, under the usual conditions.

- Full access to your history **(transactions, past performance, earned gains)** will be preserved

**The migration is simple:** if your vault is eligible, the option will appear in the app. You will be able to review the new terms and confirm the migration in just a few clicks.

*If you choose not to activate this option, your current contract will continue until its end date, without the possibility of future integration.*

 

#### How does the Cloud Mining migration work?

- **The original entry date** will be maintained for the calculation of exit windows.

- The duration of the **commitment remains unchanged,** and the **same unlocking date** will be preserved.

- Full access to your history **(transactions, past performance, earned gains)** will be preserved.

*For more details on the features of the new offer and the terms of the migration, please refer to the email that was sent on this subject.*

*The new terms must be accepted within the app. If not validated within 30 days, acceptance will be considered tacit.*

 

*If you have any questions regarding the migration conditions, we invite you to contact our customer service at [support@vancelian.com.](mailto:support@vancelian.com)*
