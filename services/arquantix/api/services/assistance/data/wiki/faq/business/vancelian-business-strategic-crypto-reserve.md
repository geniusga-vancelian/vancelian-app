# Vancelian Business — Strategic Crypto Reserve

## Short answer
The **Strategic Crypto Reserve** lets a company allocate part of its treasury to **digital assets — currently BTC and ETH** — within Vancelian's regulated and compliant framework. Deposits and withdrawals use **EURC** (the euro-pegged stablecoin), and funds remain **withdrawable 24/7**. It is a way to diversify corporate cash into crypto without managing wallets or self-custody.

## Details

| Feature | Value |
|---|---|
| Available allocation | **BTC, ETH** |
| Withdrawal | 24/7 |
| Deposit & withdrawal currency | **EURC** (euro stablecoin) |
| Custody | Vancelian (DASP-regulated, AMF E2023-087) |

### Use case
- Corporate diversification into digital assets without self-custody risk
- Treasury hedge against fiat depreciation
- Long-term exposure to BTC/ETH within a compliant European framework

## Caveats
- **There is no capital guarantee.** BTC and ETH are highly volatile — capital loss is possible.
- This is **not a yield product** — returns depend solely on BTC/ETH price movement, not on interest payments.
- See [[../legal-compliance/risk-warning-summary]] for the full digital asset risk disclosure.
