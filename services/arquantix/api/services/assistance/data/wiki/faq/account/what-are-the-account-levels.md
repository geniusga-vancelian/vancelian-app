# What are the account levels?

## Short answer
The Vancelian application includes three account levels, each corresponding to a verification step. Each level determines the deposit and withdrawal limits, as well as access to certain features (such as the payment card or cryptoasset deposits).

## Details
The Vancelian application includes three account levels, each corresponding to a verification step.

Each level determines the deposit and withdrawal limits, as well as access to certain features (such as the payment card or cryptoasset deposits).

[**Level 1: Identity verification (KYC)**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/7775692875537-KYC)

This level is achieved once your identity has been validated in the application.

It is the first mandatory step to activate your account.

[**Level 2: Residence and bank account verification**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/7778248590609-Residence)

Once your Residence section is validated and your account is verified by [bank transfer](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/28665769522321-Account-verification-by-bank-transfer), you reach level 2.

This level allows you to unlock all the services of the application (deposits, withdrawals, ordering a payment card, etc.)

[**Level 3: Income verification**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/7826692990225-Source-of-funds)

Level 3 corresponds to the validation of the Income section.

It is the highest level in the application and offers access to special features and options.
