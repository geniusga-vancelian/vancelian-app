# How can I download the Vancelian app?

## Short answer
To install the Vancelian application, you must have a stable Internet connection and use one of the two official stores: the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). Download the app depending on your device

## Details
To install the Vancelian application, you must have a stable Internet connection and use one of the two official stores: the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).

**Download the app depending on your device**

You can use this QR code to download our application:

**iPhone (iOS):**

Download from the App Store:

[https://apps.apple.com/fr/app/id1569398594](https://apps.apple.com/fr/app/id1569398594)

**Android smartphone:**

Download from the Google Play Store:

[https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.automata.akt&hl=fr&gl=US](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.automata.akt&hl=fr&gl=US)

These links must be opened from your phone to be automatically redirected to the appropriate store.

**Important**

The application can only be installed from the App Store or Google Play Store.

De-Googled devices, or any version of Android without Google Play Services, are not compatible.

Check whether your device is compatible in [this article.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29341910617873-What-is-the-minimum-configuration-required-for-my-smartphone-to-install-the-application)
