# What are the fees for the Crypto Basket?

## Short answer
Crypto Basket fees include deposit/withdrawal fees (0.25% to 0.95%, based on loyalty level) and monthly management fees (0.10% to 0.20%, depending on membership tier). Rebalancing is now free of charge (indicative — verify current rates in the app or contact Vancelian support to help locate the fee documentation).

## Details
The Crypto Basket uses the fee structure below to make the experience clearer and more efficient:

- Deposits and withdrawals: according to standard conditions, adjusted based on your loyalty level (**0.25% to 0.95%**).

- Monthly management fees: **between 0.1% and 0.2%**, depending on your [Privilege Club](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/categories/29129169287057-Privilege-Cub) status, charged once per month on the total basket volume.

Details of **monthly **management fees by status:

- **Bronze: **0.20%

- **Silver:** 0.18%

- **Gold:** 0.12%

- **Platinum: **0.11%

- **Elite: **0.10%

For more information: [vancelian.com/fr/memberships](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships)[](https://vancelian.com/fr/memberships)

- Rebalancing: now free of charge. Learn more [here.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/39641593607953-What-is-rebalancing)
