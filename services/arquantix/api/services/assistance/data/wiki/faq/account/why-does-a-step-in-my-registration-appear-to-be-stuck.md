# Why does a step in my registration appear to be stuck?

## Short answer
During the registration process, a verification step may appear to be stuck in the application. For example, the app may continue to display a message indicating that you need to “complete your profile”, or show a step awaiting validation that does not seem to progress.

## Details
During the registration process, a verification step may appear to be stuck in the application.

For example, the app may continue to display a message indicating that you need to “**complete your profile**”, or show a step **awaiting validation** that does not seem to progress.

This does not necessarily mean there is a problem: a verification may simply still be in progress.

**What are the verification timeframes?**

Each step of the registration process (identity, residence, proof of address, etc.) is reviewed by our dedicated team.

These checks may take up to **3 working days**, depending on the volume of ongoing requests.

As long as a step has not been validated, the next one will not be accessible.

**Why can my verification remain stuck?**

Several reasons may explain a delay or blockage:

- A manual review is still in progress on your account

- A missing, incomplete or incorrect document is preventing the validation of the previous step

- A document has been rejected (illegible, expired or non-compliant)

**In these cases, we recommend that you:**

- Open the application and access your **Profile**

- Go to the **Account levels **section

- Check if a **document** is still requested or marked as rejected

If a document needs to be added, you can upload it directly in this section.

**What should I do if the verification remains stuck for more than 3 days?**

If your registration still appears to be blocked after 3 working days, or if you do not understand the reason for a rejection:

- Consult our FAQ to check the list of accepted documents for each type of proof

- If everything seems correct, contact our customer support so that a member of our team can review your file manually

Customer support can help you understand the reason for the delay or rejection, but cannot validate your documents directly.

[How to contact customer support](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

**Good to know**

- As long as a verification step has not been fully validated, certain app features may remain inaccessible.

- Once a step is validated, you will receive a notification or confirmation email, and you will then be able to move on to the next step of the registration process.
