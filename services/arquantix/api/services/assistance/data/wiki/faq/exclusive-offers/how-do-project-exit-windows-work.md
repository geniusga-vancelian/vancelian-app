# How do project exit windows work?

## Short answer
Exit windows open **twice a year** and apply to the **Mining program and Exclusive Offers** (Flexible Vault and Future Vault are **not** affected). Your funds must have been locked for **at least 6 months** to be eligible. Exit fee is **5% on any amount held less than 2 years**, then **0% after 2 years** (a partial holding is charged proportionally). Withdrawals are matched against new incoming investments and processed **first-come, first-served**; the request window stays open for **2 weeks**, you can cancel but not modify a request, and once the window closes all requests become final.

## Key concepts — do not confuse

Before reading the details below, it is essential to distinguish three separate concepts:

1. **Exit windows** — a permanent mechanism that opens twice a year (typically May and November, for 2 weeks). During an exit window, existing investors can submit a request to withdraw their committed capital. Exit windows exist on **all exclusive offers and the Mining program**, regardless of whether the collection is open or closed. Savings products (Flexible Vault, Future Vault) are **not** affected — they allow withdrawals at any time.

2. **Collection (collecte)** — the process of raising funds for an offer. A collection can be **open** (new deposits accepted — invest directly in the app, no need to wait for a window) or **completed/closed** (the funding cap has been reached). When an exit window produces capital freed by exiting investors, a **deposit window** opens after the exit window closes, and the collection temporarily reopens for that freed amount on a first-come, first-served basis, then closes again automatically once full.

3. **Maturity / commitment end date (échéance)** — the date at which your committed capital is released. **You do not choose your commitment duration** — it is determined by the offer's terms and is shown in the app before each deposit. This applies identically to all exclusive offers and the Mining program. The only way to recover your capital before maturity is to exercise your right of **early exit via an exit window**, which requires a new incoming investor to take over your position. Maturity details by offer:
   - **Mining program**: depends on when you subscribed — 24 months (pre-Sept 2024), 48 months from deposit date (Sept–Dec 2024), or until 31 December 2028 (post-Dec 2024 via queue mechanism).
   - **Dubai Villa – Al Barari**: fixed at **1 May 2027** for all investors, regardless of entry date (18-month project duration from initial launch).
   - **7 Luxury Villas in Bali**: duration specified in the platform interface at subscription.
   - At maturity, your committed capital is returned automatically — no exit window needed.

## Details
Exit windows allow you to request the withdrawal of your committed capital **before maturity**, under certain conditions.

They take place **twice a year** and apply to the **Mining program and all Exclusive Offers.**

To access an exit window, your funds must have been locked for more than **6 months.**

Withdrawals are then subject to new investments from incoming investors (via the deposit window that follows), which enables the progressive buyback of requests in the **order they were submitted.**

**Savings products (Flexible Vault, Future Vault) are not affected by exit windows** — withdrawals from these products are available at any time.
 

#### **What are the exit fees?**
A **5%** exit fee applies to any amount invested for **less than 2 years**.

**Beyond 2 years, no exit fee applies — withdrawals are free of charge.**

If your request concerns an amount that is only partially eligible (e.g. part of it has been invested for more than two years, the rest not), **the fee applies only to the ineligible portion.**
 

#### **How do I submit an exit request?**
When the exit window opens, a dedicated option will appear directly on the **page of the relevant project.** Simply click on it to start your request, this submission phase will last for **2 weeks.**

Before validation, a summary will be shown, including:

- **Amount requested**

- **5% exit fee,** if applicable (calculated immediately)

- **Conversion fees to USDC,** if applicable (this is an estimate — actual fees are applied at the time of the buyback)

- **Net amount after fees**
 

#### **What happens after I confirm my request?**
Once your request is confirmed, it is recorded in the app. Exit requests will continue to be collected over a 2 week period. In the meantime, your funds remain invested and continue to generate **interest.**

You may** cancel** your request at any time while the window is still open, but you **cannot modify** it directly. To change the amount, you will need to cancel and submit a new request.

It is not possible to know **your position in the queue in advance, and any cancellation will result in the loss of your place.**

Once the exit window closes, after 2 weeks, **all requests become final.**
 

#### **How does the fundraising process work?**
Each exit window is accompanied by a **new round of investments,** available via the app. This allows new investors to join the project and progressively buy back the portion of clients wishing to exit.

Buybacks are processed **gradually,** in the **order requests are received.** You will be notified as soon as your share is bought back, and the funds will be credited immediately.

**This product is eligible for deposit windows twice a year. The new collection and details of re-engagement will be available directly in the app.**
