# Guarantees and security of your investment (The Heights Bali)

> Scope: applies to the **7 Luxury Villas in Bali (The Heights Bali)** offer (closed since 6 October 2025).

## Short answer
The question is not one of guarantee but of the engagement mechanic on your capital. The Heights Bali is a **100% crypto loan** to the project's dedicated SPV, which finances the acquisition and construction of the villas. The counterparty has a **contractual obligation to repay the capital and interest** according to the offer's schedule. Specific elements relating to the project (tangible backing — Munduk land valued at ~USD 650,000, villas under construction; French legal structure with Indonesian subsidiaries; exit windows twice a year in May and November subject to a queue mechanism) are detailed in the commercial brochure and offer documentation in the app. **Absolute limit: no protection, whatever its form, covers a total or partial default of the counterparty.** Returns are indicative and not guaranteed.

## Details

#### Engagement mechanic and absolute limit

The investment takes the form of a 100% crypto loan from the investor to the project's dedicated SPV, which finances the acquisition and construction of the villas in Munduk. The SPV has a contractual obligation to repay the capital and interest according to the offer's schedule. **No protection, whatever its form, covers a total or partial default of the SPV as the counterparty.** This is the absolute limit and it cannot be softened by listing safeguards.

Within this limit, the project documentation describes the following elements relating to the project and its execution:

#### Tangible Assets

Your investment is backed by **The Heights** real estate project in Munduk (Bali):

- The land: Currently valued at approximately USD 650,000, with significant potential for appreciation. According to government sources, this land is expected to be rezoned from agricultural (green zone) to tourism (red zone), which should considerably increase its value.

- Villas under construction: As the project progresses, these tangible assets gain value and offer growing security.

 

#### Project Expertise and Strength

- Experienced team: The project is led by recognized professionals in international luxury real estate.

- Strategic location: The villas are located in a fast-developing region, with a new airport and various infrastructures planned.

- Secure legal structure: The setup involves a French company operating through Indonesian subsidiaries, ensuring a transparent and solid legal framework.

 

#### Exit Opportunities

Early exits are possible under specific conditions:

- Exit windows open **twice a year** (in May and November)

- “Queue Mechanism”: your exit is subject to a new investor taking your place

- First-come, first-served principle

- Partial exit possible if a new investor covers only part of your investment

 

#### Compliance and Regulation

Vancelian (Automata France SAS) is registered as a Digital Asset Service Provider (**DASP**) with the **AMF**, ensuring compliance with French regulations.
