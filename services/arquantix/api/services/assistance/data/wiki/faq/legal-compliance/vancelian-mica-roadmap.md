# Vancelian's MiCA roadmap: PSAN to CASP transition

> **Regulatory escalation:** the chatbot must escalate any specific regulatory or legal question to a Vancelian human advisor.

## Short answer
Vancelian is registered as a **PSAN** with the French AMF (Automata France SAS, AMF E2023‑087) and is preparing its transition to the new **CASP** (Crypto-Asset Service Provider) status under MiCA, ahead of the **1 July 2026** deadline. The transition involves a full upgrade of compliance procedures (AML/CFT, traceability and reporting, cybersecurity, mandatory external audits, minimum regulatory capital). In parallel, Vancelian is expanding internationally — notably with **Automata FZE in Dubai** in the process of obtaining a **VARA** (Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority) licence — combining a MiCA-compliant European footprint with a regulated international presence.

## Details

### Current status
- **Registration**: Vancelian operates through Automata France SAS, registered as a PSAN with the French AMF (AMF E2023‑087, SIREN 902 498 617).
- **Other regulated entities** in the Automata group:
  - **Italy**: Automata ICO Limited (OAM registration n° 96550860587).
  - **United Kingdom**: Automata Pay Ltd (authorised agent of Modulr FS, FCA-supervised).
  - **Ireland**: Automata Pay Europe Ltée (authorised agent of Modulr FS, CBI-supervised).

### MiCA application calendar
- **30 June 2024** — ART (Asset-Referenced Tokens) and EMT (E-Money Tokens) provisions enter into force.
- **30 December 2024** — full MiCA framework applies, including CASP obligations.
- **30 June 2026** — end of the PSAN transition period and regulatory deadline for Automata France's PSCA/CASP application. The file has been submitted to the AMF and is under instruction. Target obtainment: **June 2026**.
- **1 July 2026** — **hard deadline**: end of the 18-month French grandfathering window (which started on 30 December 2024). From 1 July 2026, only CASP-authorised providers may continue providing crypto-asset services in France. PSANs without authorisation must cease activity; those not pursuing CASP must start an orderly wind-down plan **no later than 30 March 2026**. **No extension beyond 1 July 2026** (AMF notice, February 2026).

### What the PSAN → CASP transition involves
A complete upgrade across multiple workstreams:
- **AML/CFT (LCB-FT)** — strengthening anti-money-laundering and counter-terrorist-financing obligations: KYC/KYB, transaction monitoring, suspicious activity reports to TRACFIN, ongoing staff training.
- **Traceability and reporting** — regular reporting to European regulators (ESMA, EBA).
- **Cybersecurity (DORA)** — see [[dora-cybersecurity-explained|DORA explained]]. Critical-function mapping, ICT incident management, advanced penetration testing, third-party provider oversight.
- **External audits** — mandatory independent audits (KPMG and others).
- **Minimum regulatory capital** — Vancelian's services (custody and exchange) require a minimum capital of **€125,000**; trading platform operations require **€150,000**. Equity must also exceed one quarter of the previous year's fixed costs if higher than the minimum.
- **Travel Rule (TFR)** — implementation of the EU Transfer of Funds Regulation 2023/1113, requiring originator/beneficiary information on all crypto transfers.
- **Governance & Fit & Proper** — strengthened governance, experienced and trustworthy directors, segregation of duties, internal control, conflict of interest management.
- **Sustainability disclosures** — publication of environmental impact indicators (energy, emissions, % renewable) per listed asset.

### Vancelian's 12–18 month MiCA roadmap
1. **Gap analysis PSAN → CASP** — services, capital, policies, IT, contracts.
2. **Policies & registers** upgrade — conflicts, complaints, market abuse, sustainability, listing, TFR, outsourcing, DORA.
3. **ICT/DORA** — critical function mapping, business continuity (BCP/DRP), SOC/EDR, immutable logging, advanced penetration testing.
4. **Market surveillance** — detection tooling (rules, scenarios, alerts, STOR), Chinese walls between functions.
5. **Sustainability data pipeline** — methodologies and per-asset web publication.
6. **TFR integration** — Travel Rule solution (VASP directory, sunrise compliance, self-hosted wallet handling).
7. **CASP application** — preparation of the Article 62 dossier (Programme of Operations), pre-filing with the AMF, calendar.
8. **Passport rollout** — selection of target countries, freedom of establishment / freedom to provide services, local product and marketing alignment.

### International expansion in parallel
- **Automata FZE (Dubai, UAE)** — received **VARA In-Principle Approval (IPA)** in December 2025. Full licence is imminent (as of April 2026). Services: Broker Dealer, Management & Investments, Lending & Borrowing, Advisory. See [Vancelian VARA licence](vancelian-vara-license.md).
- The strategy is to combine a **MiCA-compliant European footprint** with a **regulated international presence**, giving clients access to a full regulated stack across multiple jurisdictions.

## Caveats
- The roadmap is ambitious but the regulatory framework is still evolving (delegated acts, RTS, ITS). Specific milestones may shift.
