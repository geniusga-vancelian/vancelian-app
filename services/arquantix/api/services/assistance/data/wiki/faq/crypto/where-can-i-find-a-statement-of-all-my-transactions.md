# Where can I find a statement of all my transactions?

## Short answer
Go to Profile > My Account > Taxation > Transaction Statement in the app, and Vancelian will email you a complete statement of all your transactions. This is useful for tax preparation and can also be reviewed with our partner Waltio for tax guidance.

## Details
To access a complete statement of all your account transactions, for example for tax declaration purposes, follow these steps:

- Go to Profile

- Select the My Account section

- Go to Taxation

- Click on Transaction Statement

An email will then be sent to you with the document attached, giving you access to a detailed statement of all your transactions.

 

**For any precision on your taxation or on the interpretation of your transactions for your declaration, contact your tax administration. You may also use our partner **[**Waltio**](https://www.waltio.com/)** for your crypto declarations.**
