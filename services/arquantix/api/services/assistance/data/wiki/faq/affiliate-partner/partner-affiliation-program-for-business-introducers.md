# Partner Affiliation Program for Business Introducers

## Short answer
Partner status at Vancelian is for users who actively introduce companies, WMAs, and individual clients to the platform and support them long-term. As a Partner, you earn higher rewards than standard referrers (paid as coupons), get a dedicated tracking dashboard, access priority support, and build passive income proportional to your referees' activity. Apply through the Partner page on the website.

## Details
The **Vancelian Partner status** rewards users who actively contribute to the growth of the community.

It is much more than a simple rewards programme: it is a **virtuous collaboration** between you, your referees, and Vancelian.

You support your referees in their investments, and your involvement is valued as all of you grow together.

**What is the difference between a referrer and a Partner?**

The **Referrer** shares their invitation link and receives one-time bonuses.

The** Partner** becomes a true guide: they support, follow and assist their referees over time.

In return, they benefit from **more advantageous rewards**, dedicated support, and a key role in the growth of the Vancelian ecosystem.

It is a** win–win** model, where everyone’s success benefits the whole community.

The Partner status works in synergy with the [referral program](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/13790901302929-Referral-and-rewards) and the [Privilege Club.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club)

Your points, referees and rewards accumulate automatically.

**What are the benefits of the Vancelian Partner status?**

As a Partner, you benefit from:

- **Higher rewards than standard referral**, proportional to the activity of your referees

- **A dedicated platform** to track your referees, your earnings and your performance

- The ability to refer users, **but also companies and WMAs** (Wealth Management Advisors)

- **Priority support** from the Vancelian teams

**How do the program’s rewards work?**

Each time your referees invest on the platform, you receive rewards in the form of coupons, automatically credited to your **Rewards Vault.**

These coupons represent a share of the value generated by your referees’ activity and reflect your role in supporting the network.

They can then be used to **acquire crypto** directly from the application, with no additional fees associated with their use.

To learn how to view and use your coupons, you can read this article: [How can I use my reward coupons?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/40779024691473-How-can-I-use-my-reward-coupons)

**How to join the Partner Affiliation Program?**

The programme is** open to applications.**

You can **apply now from the Vancelian website**, via the form available on the **Partner** page.

Registration from the mobile application will be available soon.

Once your application is approved, the Vancelian team will contact you once your status has been validated.

The process is simple: you introduce Vancelian to your contacts and initiate the connection through your affiliation link.

We then take over: full support for your contacts, regulatory review, and product activation.

Each relationship is tagged to your introducer ID, **ensuring complete traceability and transparent follow-up from start to finish.**
