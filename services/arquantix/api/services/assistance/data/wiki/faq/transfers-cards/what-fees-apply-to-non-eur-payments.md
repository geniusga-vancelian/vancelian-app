# What fees apply to non-EUR payments?

## Short answer
When you use your Vancelian card to pay in a currency other than EUR, whether in-store or online, certain rules and fees apply. How does a foreign-currency payment work?

## Details
When you use your Vancelian card to pay in a currency other than EUR, whether in-store or online, certain **rules** and **fees apply.**

**How does a foreign-currency payment work?**

When the payment is **authorised**, a slightly **higher** amount may appear.

This mechanism helps secure the **exchange rate** while the transaction is being fully processed.

The final amount is then adjusted at the time of settlement **(up to 7 days after the authorisation)**. If the exchange rate has not changed, the difference will not be charged.

**What fees are applied?**

For every payment made in a currency other than euro:

- Approximately **3%** is automatically added by ModulR at the moment of authorisation, to cover potential exchange rate fluctuations

- Vancelian fees of **2.5%** apply to each foreign-currency transaction

- **Elite members:** 0% Vancelian fees
*(the conversion margin applied by ModulR still applies*)
