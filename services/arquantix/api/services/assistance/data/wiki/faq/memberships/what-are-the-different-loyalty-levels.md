# What are the different loyalty levels?

## Short answer
The Vancelian Privilege Club has 5 tiers: Bronze (0 points), Silver (1,000 points), Gold (5,000 points), Platinum (50,000 points), and Elite (300,000 points). You start at Bronze and automatically advance to higher tiers as you accumulate points through investments, referrals, and other actions. Each tier unlocks better rates, lower fees, and exclusive benefits.

## Details
The Vancelian Privilege Club includes **5 loyalty levels,** which are automatically accessible upon opening your account.

Each level reflects your engagement within the application and provides access to exclusive benefits, preferential rates, and personalised services.

Your level evolves automatically based on the total number of points accumulated through your investments and actions within the application.

How to check your level and associated benefits:

From the home page of your application:

- Click on your **Profile** (top left)

- Select your **current status** (Bronze, Silver, Gold, etc.)

Scroll down to view your benefits, upcoming milestones, and the full list of actions that earn points

This allows you to identify the requirements to [reach the next level](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29319660844177-How-to-move-up-to-a-higher-level) and enjoy additional benefits.
