# What is the AKTIO coin?

## Details
**AKTIO** is a **utility token**, issued on the Ethereum blockchain according to the ERC-20 standard.

It was designed to provide access to specific uses and benefits within the ecosystem initially developed under the **Akt.io** brand, now integrated into the environment whose new commercial brand is Vancelian.

AKTIO, as a utility digital asset, is neither a share nor a regulated financial investment product.

Issued by **Automata ICO Ltd**, AKTIO operates within a regulatory framework under Irish jurisdiction and is recognised as a utility token.

As such, **Automata ICO Ltd** holds a legal note explicitly qualifying AKTIO as a utility token.

This qualification notably enabled a first public listing (Introduction) on 13 April 2022 on the Bittrex Global platform.

The AKTIO white papers were published by **Automata ICO Ltd** in November 2021 (Hedera Hashgraph) and April 2022 (Ethereum ERC20) and remain accessible as reference documents.

They are available as attachments to this article.

The white papers, published in** 2021 and 2022**, aimed to present a usage token intended to support the launch and adoption of a next-generation financial application (Akt.io), combining crypto services, payments, and crypto asset management.

**They aimed in particular to:**

- Describe the functional role of the AKTIO token

- Explain its place within the ecosystem

- Frame its issuance and distribution (Tokenomics and Vesting programme)

- Inform participants about the associated risks

The white paper did not constitute a financial prospectus and did not promise any return. It remains valid and accessible today as a reference document.

**AKTIO** is now the native token of the [Vancelian](https://vancelian.com/en) ecosystem.

Designed as a lever for access and usage value, it plays a central role in the loyalty programme (Privilege Club) of the Vancelian application.

- **Utility token and loyalty accelerator: **Once purchased and held within the Vancelian application, AKTIO allows users to accumulate loyalty points granting access to exclusive benefits. By locking your AKTIO for a period of 3 or 12 months, you accelerate your progression within the Privilege Club, unlocking higher-tier privileges more quickly. [Learn more about locking AKTIO tokens](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/29339316761489-What-is-Vancelian-token-locking)

- **A secure and compliant asset: **AKTIO is an interoperable token, issued on the Ethereum blockchain, ensuring security, transparency, and compatibility with the most widely recognised wallets.

You can purchase and hold your AKTIO tokens directly via the Vancelian application, or through the [Bitmart](https://www.bitmart.com/en-US) exchange platform.

To learn more about loyalty points and their benefits, please consult the [Privilege Club](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/sections/29319046033553-Privilege-Club) section of our FAQ as well as [our website.](https://vancelian.com/en)
