# Vancelian Business — Flexible Vault

## Short answer
The **Flexible Vault** is the corporate version of Vancelian's open-ended savings product. It pays **up to 5% APY (daily compound interest)**, has **no commitment period**, allows **24/7 deposits and withdrawals**, and **deposit/withdrawal are free of charge**. It is designed for active corporate cash management or as an immediately-available security reserve.

## Details

| Feature | Value |
|---|---|
| Current rate | up to **5% APY** |
| Compounding | Yes (daily) |
| Earnings | Daily |
| Withdrawal | 24/7 |
| Deposit & withdrawal fees | **Free** |
| Commitment period | **None** |

### When to use it
- Active treasury management — park excess cash and reallocate as needed
- Immediate-access security reserve
- Bridging cash between operational expenses

## Caveats
- The 5% rate is the **top-of-range live rate** — actual rate may vary by tier and market performance.
- See [[../legal-compliance/risk-warning-summary]] — capital is exposed to digital asset risk.
