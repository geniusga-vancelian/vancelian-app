# AKTIO ICO sale rounds and prices

## Short answer
The AKTIO Initial Offering ran across **seven rounds** (Seed 1/2/3, Private Sale, Pre-Sale, Public Sale, Extra Sale) with prices from **€0.30 to €1.00** per token, corresponding to discounts of **0% to 70%** off the €1.00 reference price. **Total capital raised: ~€26.5 million** for 31,964,270 AKTIO distributed (32% of total supply).

## Details

### Sale rounds (per Whitepaper V.4.0.0, April 2022)

| Round | Price | Discount | AKTIO sold | Capital raised | Lockup |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Seed 1 | €0.30 | 70% | 2,333,310 | €700,000 | 21 months |
| Seed 2 | €0.50 | 50% | 2,000,000 | €1,000,000 | 21 months |
| Seed 3 | €0.67 | 33% | 1,600,000 | €800,000 | 21 months |
| Private Sale | €0.80 | 20% | 8,535,209 | €6,828,167 | 21 months |
| Pre-Sale | €0.89 | 11% | 7,397,044 | €6,583,369 | 21 months |
| Public Sale | €1.00 | 0% | 10,000,000 | €10,000,000 | Unlocked at 1st listing |
| Extra Sale | €1.00 | 0% | 598,707 | €598,707 | Unlocked at 1st listing |
| **Total** | — | — | **31,964,270** | **~€26.5M** | — |

### Lockup logic
- **AKTIO sold at a discount** (Seed 1/2/3, Private Sale, Pre-Sale) vests over **21 months** after the first exchange listing, in **quarterly 15% releases** (with an initial 10% unlock at listing).
- **Public Sale and Extra Sale AKTIO** are **unlocked at the first exchange listing**, with no vesting.
- The first AKTIO exchange listing took place in **April 2022** (Bittrex Global, 13 April 2022).

### Geographic restrictions during the ICO
Buyers were **not eligible** to purchase AKTIO in the Initial Offering if they were citizens, residents (for tax or other purposes) or green card holders of:
- United States of America
- People's Republic of China
- Hong Kong Special Administrative Region
- Malta

These restrictions were enforced via KYC/AML and via smart contract checks at the token purchase level.

## Caveats
- All figures reflect the Initial Offering as documented in the April 2022 whitepaper. Subsequent secondary market activity on exchanges (e.g. Bittrex Global, BitMart) is independent of the ICO and not covered by these rounds.
- Historical AKTIO purchases via the Initial Offering cannot be reopened; new acquisitions take place on secondary markets or via the Vancelian application.
