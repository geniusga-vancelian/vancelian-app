# Project counterparty & responsibilities — Dubai Villa Al Barari

## Short answer
The Dubai Villa – Al Barari project is operated by **Solaria** (founded 2022 by co-founders and historical shareholders of Vancelian), which **solely bears the debt, the risk, and the obligation to repay capital and interest**. Solaria oversees the acquisition and renovation of the villa in The Nest, Al Barari, working with local partners **Al Hilal Engineering Consultants** (construction/architecture) and **Maria Morris Global** (real estate). **Vancelian (Automata France SAS)** is a **DASP registered with the AMF** and only provides the regulated investment platform and digital asset custody — **Vancelian is not responsible for repayment of capital or interest**.

## Details
#### **Who is Solaria?**
The Solaria Group designs and develops **exceptional real estate projects** aimed at a prestigious clientele, embodying a balance between aesthetics, uniqueness, and long-term value.

**Founded in December 2021,** Solaria emerged from the convergence of market finance and investment real estate, extending the asset-based vision of the Vancelian Group. Created by several co-founders and historical shareholders of Vancelian, Solaria brings together dual expertise in **financial analysis and real estate execution** to serve a common goal: structuring and managing high-value projects anchored in markets with strong international potential.

**The company relies on a selective network of professionals** — brokers, architects, developers, and engineers — enabling it to focus resources on what truly matters: asset performance and value creation.
 

#### **For this Villa project in Dubai - Al Barari, Solaria:**

- Oversees the operation: the acquisition and complete renovation of a luxury villa located in The Nest residential development, at the heart of the prestigious green district of Al Barari.

- Manages relationships through renowned local partners (the construction and architectural firm Al Hilal Engineering Consultants, as well as the real estate agency Maria Morris Global).
 

#### **How are the responsibilities divided between Solaria and Vancelian?**
Our two-tier structure ensures a clear separation of roles and responsibilities:

**Solaria:**

- Solely assumes responsibility, including for the payment of interest and the repayment at maturity.

- Directly receives the loan amount upon subscription to the Exclusive Offer.

- Bears the debt and assumes the risks associated with the project.

- Commits, through a best-effort obligation, to securing your investment.

**Vancelian (Automata France SAS):**

- Is a Digital Asset Service Provider **(DASP)** registered with the **AMF**.

- Facilitates investment through its regulated and secure platform in France.

- Ensures the safekeeping of digital assets.

- Is not responsible for the repayment of capital or the payment of interest.
 

Thus, Vancelian guarantees transparency and reliability within the investment environment, while Solaria focuses its expertise on the operational success of the project.

As with any investment, the final performance will depend on the effective completion of the project and the market conditions within the real estate sector.

 

**You can find full details of the framework in the*[* Terms and Conditions of Use*](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers-tou/exclusives-offers-solaria)*, which you agree to upon making your investment.*
