# How can I raise my bank-transfer deposit limits?

## Short answer
To raise your deposit limits, you must provide a supporting document justifying the new amount. If the request does not appear automatically in the app, go to **Profile > My account > Deposit thresholds > Upload a supporting document**. Acceptable documents are the same as those for level-3 verification (proof of income).

## Process
1. Open the app and navigate to **Profile > My account > Deposit thresholds**.
2. Tap **Upload a supporting document**.
3. Attach a valid proof of income (see list below).
4. Our compliance team reviews the document; you will be notified of the outcome in-app and by email.

## Requirements / Documents

Accepted proof of income (one document, dated within 3 months except tax notices):
- Payslip (< 3 months)
- Social benefits certificate (unemployment, RSA, disability) + bank statement showing the credited amount
- Latest tax notice showing income of the previous year
- Proof of inheritance + bank statement showing the credited amount
- Proof of share sale + bank statement
- Loan agreement + bank statement showing the loan disbursement
- Proof of rental or investment income + bank statement
- Notarial deed for the sale of real estate + bank statement
- Crypto transaction statement in the client's name justifying realised gains
- Investment or placement statement + bank statement

### For corporate accounts (pro)
Submit at least two of:
- Latest company balance sheet
- Company bank statement (< 3 months)
- Tax notice

Corporate documents must be sent by email to `support@vancelian.com` (app uploads one file at a time only).

## Format
- File format: PDF, JPEG, JPG or PNG.
- Full name must appear on the document.

## Documents not accepted
- Single-transaction receipts
- RIB / IBAN documents
- Employment contract
- Invoices

## Caveats
- If the upload button does not appear automatically, contact `support@vancelian.com` and attach the document directly.
- Limits are tied to your account level and may also be influenced by your Privilege Club tier — see [[what-are-the-account-levels]].
