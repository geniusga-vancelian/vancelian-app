# Referral and rewards

## Short answer
Refer your friends to Vancelian using a referral link or code, and you'll earn a share of their transaction fees (1–15% depending on your tier) plus 300 loyalty points per referral. Your referees get 50 euros in fee credits for their first 3 months. Both you and your referees must complete registration and make your first deposit for the referral to activate.

## Details
Invite your friends to join Vancelian, earn **exclusive bonuses** and give them attractive **rewards** to get started.
 

#### **How can I be referred during registration?**
There are two ways to be referred when registering on the Vancelian application.

You can either use the referral link sent by your referrer or enter a referral code directly during the registration process.

- If you use a **referral link**, it must be used before downloading the application and starting the registration process. The referral will then be automatically associated with your account when it is created.

- If you use a **referral code**, you can enter it at any time during the registration process using the option located at the top right of the screen.

In both cases, once the referral has been correctly registered, you will be able to see it displayed in the same place throughout the registration process to confirm that it has been properly recorded before completing the registration.

**Once the registration process is completed, it is no longer possible to add a referral retroactively.**
 

#### **Steps required to validate a referral**

- Your referral has correctly used your referral link or code to register in the application.

- Both you and your referral have completed your registration in the application.

- Both you and your referral have made a first deposit into your Vancelian account.
 

#### Your rewards:

- Referral bonus:
For every transaction your referees make on Vancelian, a share of the transaction fees is credited back to you.
 

- Progress in the Privilege Club:
Earn 300 loyalty points for every new user you refer.

#### Tracking your referral earnings:
You will receive your referral earnings daily as soon as the accumulated amount reaches **€0.05.**

Your **Rewards** vault will be credited automatically once this threshold is reached.

If the threshold is not reached, your earnings will instead be credited once a week.

You can view your referrals, rewards, and active offers directly in the app under Offers and Rewards.

#### For your referral:

- €50 in crypto transaction fees offered:
Your referee will receive €50 in fee credits for their first crypto transactions, valid for **3 months,** to help them start their Vancelian journey with an advantage.

Important information:

- Our referral system is fully** automated **and we cannot intervene if the conditions have not been met.

- Once the registration process is completed, it is no longer possible to add a referral** retroactively.**

- We invite you to contact your referral to confirm that all the required steps have been completed on their side.

 

*For confidentiality reasons and in compliance with GDPR, you will not be able to access your referees’ transactions, and Vancelian will not be able to provide you with their details.
