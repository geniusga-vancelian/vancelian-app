# The Heights Bali — full project reference

> **Status:** the offer was **closed on 6 October 2025** at its €4M cap. This page is a **historical reference** for existing investors, advisors, and chatbot context. New deposits are not accepted.

## Short answer
The Heights Bali offer is closed (reached €4M cap on 6 October 2025) — this page is a historical reference for existing investors. The 7-villa project at Munduk costs €5.49M total and pays 10.2%–11% APR daily in EURC, plus a potential performance bonus on resale. Delivery is scheduled end-2026, with rental operations 2026–2029 and resale 2028–2029.

## Project overview

The Heights Bali is structured as a four-phase operation spread over five years. The **land phase** took place in 2024 with the acquisition and preparation of a 1.3-hectare plot at Munduk. The **construction phase** runs from 2025 to end-2026 for the seven villas and their shared premium infrastructure. The **operation phase**, from 2026 to 2029, monetises the assets through high-end rental income. The **resale phase** in 2028–2029 then realises the exit through optimised villa sales, capitalising on the operating track record established during the rental years.

The site sits at **Munduk, in North Bali**, on a 1.3-hectare plot valued at approximately **$650,000 USD** at the 2024 acquisition. The investment thesis rests on two catalysts. First, the anticipated **reclassification of the zone into a "red zone"** (tourism zone), which is expected to materially increase the land value. Second, macroeconomic tailwinds for North Bali specifically: a new international airport is planned for the region, and Bali as a destination already captures around **15 million annual visitors, versus roughly 10 million for the French Riviera** — a demand base that supports long-term luxury rental economics.

## Investor returns

### Fixed APR by Privilege Club tier
Returns are tiered by Privilege Club membership, ranging from 10.20% to 11.00% annually.

| Tier | APR |
|---|---|
| Bronze | 10.20% |
| Silver | 10.45% |
| Gold | 10.70% |
| Platinum | 10.95% |
| Elite | 11.00% |

Payouts are distributed **daily in EURC** (the euro-pegged stablecoin), credited to the Vancelian "Available" wallet from the day after deposit. The principal itself is **value-locked in EUR** at the deposit date — the BTC-denominated loan uses the EUR/BTC rate at the moment of deposit as a fixed reference, which removes crypto volatility from the capital side of the investment. Compounded over the four-year horizon, the fixed component alone delivers a cumulative return of **40.8% to 44%** depending on tier.

### Performance bonus on resale
Beyond the fixed APR, the project includes a **performance bonus** that activates only if a capital gain is realised when the villas are resold at the end of the operation. The typical structure redistributes approximately **6% of the realised plus-value** to investors, and can lift the cumulative four-year return to roughly **50% APR** in the favourable scenario. If an investor exits early through an exit window, the bonus attached to their position is **transferred to the new investor** who takes over the contract — it follows the position, not the person.

### Rental projections (operation phase)
During the operation phase, the projected rental economics are a **13.72% annual ROI**, based on an **82% average occupancy** and an **average nightly rate of €774 per villa**. These figures are stated net of operating costs, OTA platform fees, and management — they represent what is expected to flow through to the project, not gross booking revenue.

## Lock-up and exit

The target duration is **four years**, with a minimum lock-up of **six months** running from the end of the fundraising window. **Exit windows** open twice per year, typically in **May and November**, and are always conditional on a new investor stepping in to take over the contract — the programme does not guarantee liquidity, only the opportunity to transfer the position. If an early exit is executed before 24 months of holding, a **5% fee** applies; after 24 months, **the exit is free**. As noted above, the performance bonus attached to the position transfers to the incoming investor.

## Project team and partners

### Project counterparty
The project is carried by **The Heights Bali SAS**, a French SAS created specifically to structure and own the operation. It is the sole entity legally responsible for principal repayment and interest payments on the loan — see [[../../entities/the-heights-bali-sas|The Heights Bali SAS entity page]] for the full structural detail. **Julien Halimi**, Vancelian co-founder, personally supervises critical project milestones — land securing, construction kick-off — which anchors a direct line of accountability between the offer and the leadership of the group.

### Distribution
The distribution layer is handled by **Automata France SAS (Vancelian)**, a French PSAN registered with the AMF under SIREN 902 498 617 and registration E2023-087. It is important to be explicit about the legal role: Automata France acts as the **regulated distribution intermediary** only. It **does not bear any liability for principal repayment or interest payment** — that liability sits exclusively with The Heights Bali SAS. This separation between distributor and project issuer is standard practice for regulated co-financing structures.

### Construction and design
The construction is led by **Conceptive Construction**, a French high-end developer based in Bali with more than 25 years of experience. Its track record in the region includes The Heights Bingin (8 villas), the Apricot / Riptide / Kasadya / Barsa villas in Bingin and Seminyak, and The Heights Melasti in Ungasan. Conceptive provides a **ten-year structural guarantee** and end-to-end supervision across planning, execution and finishings — see [[../../entities/conceptive-construction|Conceptive Construction entity page]] for the detailed profile. Design is led by **Eben Lontoh**, a notable Indonesian architect whose vision centres on modern tropical luxury — noble materials, home automation, open spaces with panoramic views — and who has previously worked for Alila and prestigious private residences across Asia. Execution on the ground is carried out by **HMK** as general contractor, with **Magnitude Construction (US)** acting as sustainability and energy performance advisor, specialising in sustainable architecture and integrated project management.

### Premium client experience
The on-site experience is designed for the upper end of the Munduk rental market. Guests access the villas via a **suspended road through the jungle**, enter through a **wood-panelled lobby with fireplace**, and have access to a **24/7 butler service** and a **private chef on demand**. Only **electric vehicles** are permitted on-site, which preserves the quiet that defines the Munduk positioning.

## Competitive positioning
The closest local benchmark is **"Munduk Cabins"**. The Heights Bali differentiates on amenities — pool, sauna, butler service — and targets a higher segment of the market. This positioning justifies both a **price premium** and a **higher rental yield** relative to the benchmark, which is the commercial thesis underlying the rental projections above.

## Risk framing

### What is secured
The risk profile combines several structural protections that are worth naming individually. The invested capital is **value-locked in EUR at the deposit date**, so the principal is insulated from BTC volatility. The project is **backed by tangible assets** — full freehold land, villas under construction, and premium infrastructure — which means that even in adverse scenarios, recoverable value exists. The operation is wrapped in a **French legal envelope** via the SAS structure, and the distribution layer is under **AMF PSAN supervision** (Vancelian as regulated intermediary). Finally, **independent due diligence** was conducted on the project and its developer, and Conceptive Construction carries its **ten-year structural guarantee** on the built asset.

### What is not guaranteed
None of the above amounts to a formal capital guarantee — that is the first point to state clearly. **No banking deposit guarantee fund applies** to this offer. Returns are **indicative, not contractual**, and in extreme scenarios — project default or a severe imbalance in early-exit demand — the CGU explicitly foresees that distributed returns could fall to **zero**. **Counterparty risk on The Heights Bali SAS** is the principal residual exposure: in case of bankruptcy of the project entity, investors would stand as standard project-debt holders, with the tangible assets (land, villas) liquidated by judicial administrators — this partially mitigates loss but does not eliminate it. **Counterparty risk on Vancelian itself is structurally limited**, because client funds are legally segregated from Automata France's own assets and the loan contract is direct between the investor and The Heights Bali SAS; that said, a Vancelian bankruptcy could still **temporarily block movements** during the duration of legal proceedings. Beyond those, standard real estate execution risks apply: **construction delays**, **occupancy shortfalls**, and **market shifts in luxury rentals** over the four-year horizon.
