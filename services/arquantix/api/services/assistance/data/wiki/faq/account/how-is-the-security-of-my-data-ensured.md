## Details
The security and confidentiality of your personal data are at the heart of Vancelian’s priorities.

**To guarantee their protection, we apply:**

- **The General Data Protection Regulation** (GDPR), which strictly governs the use and processing of your data within the European Union.

- The recommendations of the **CNIL**, in order to adopt best practices in terms of privacy protection.

To learn more about how Vancelian collects, processes and protects your data, you can consult our [Privacy Policy.](https://vancelian.com/en/privacy-policy)
