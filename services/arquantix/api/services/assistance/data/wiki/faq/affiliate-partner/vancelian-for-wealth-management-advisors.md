# Vancelian for Wealth Management Advisors

## Short answer
Vancelian's WMA affiliation program is designed for wealth management advisors and family offices who want to offer their clients savings, crypto trading, and exclusive investment products. Partner advisors get a dedicated CRM platform, competitive commissions, fast onboarding, and personalised support. Clients access an EUR account, VISA card, and priority membership benefits—all through Vancelian's regulated platform.

## Details
The Vancelian affiliation program for **WMAs (Wealth Management Advisors)** and **Family Offices** is designed to support professionals wishing to offer their clients an innovative savings and investment solution that combines traditional finance and blockchain technologies, through a **high-performance** interface and an** attractive commission structure.**
 

#### **What are the advantages for partner advisors?**
Partner advisors benefit from the following services:

- **A dedicated CRM platform** to efficiently manage their clients and operations.

- **Fast and personalised onboarding** to start without complexity.

- **A competitive commission system** offering attractive remuneration.

- **Personalised support** to ensure tailored follow-up for each partner.

**What benefits for savers?**

Clients supported by Vancelian’s partner WMAs gain access to a complete offer:

- **Simplified management through our mobile application.**

- **An EUR account and a VISA card**

- **Exclusive access to our savings and investment products.**

- **Priority access to the application and the privilege program.**

**Steps to register for the affiliation program:**

Registration for WMAs in the Vancelian affiliation program is completed in 5 simple steps:

- [**Online registration:**](https://vancelian.com/en/partner/register) Fill in this [registration form.](https://vancelian.com/en/partner/register)

- **Personalised presentation:** You will receive a detailed presentation of our products and services.

- **Signature and validation:** Finalise your registration by signing the affiliation contract.

- **Training and onboarding:** Complete our training to fully understand the program and how to maximise your revenue.

- **Client activation: **Once registered and trained, you can begin activating clients and generating revenue.
 

Join Vancelian as a Wealth Management Advisor by submitting [your application here.](https://vancelian.com/en/partner/register)
