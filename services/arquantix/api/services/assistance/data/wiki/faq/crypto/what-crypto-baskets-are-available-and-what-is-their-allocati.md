# What Crypto Baskets are available and what is their allocation?

## Short answer
Vancelian offers two Crypto Baskets: the Top 2 Majors (70% BTC / 30% ETH) focused on market leaders, and the Top 5 Majors (50% BTC / 20% ETH / 10% each of XRP, BNB, SOL) for broader diversification. Both are allocated by market capitalisation and liquidity.

## Details
#### **What is the Top 5 Crypto Basket?**
The Top 5 Majors Crypto Basket is a basket of cryptocurrencies that groups together five digital assets: **Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB), Solana (SOL) and Ripple (XRP).**

**Why were these five cryptoassets chosen?**

The selection is based on objective criteria:

- **Market capitalisation: **to ensure market representativeness.

- **Liquidity:** to ensure efficient and transparent transactions.

These five cryptoassets therefore form the pillars of the market, recognised for their weight and their central role in the ecosystem. Their significant market capitalisation and liquidity enable them to absorb volatility shocks, thereby helping to smooth performance over time.

**How is the capital allocated within the basket?**

The capital is allocated according to a predefined target allocation, with a significant share assigned to Bitcoin, which is historically the least volatile asset among the five.
 

**Indicative allocation:**

- 50% BTC

- 20% ETH

- 10% XRP

- 10% BNB

- 10% SOL

**What is the Top 2 Crypto Basket?**

The Top 2 Crypto Basket is a basket of digital assets composed exclusively of **two major cryptocurrencies:**

**Bitcoin (BTC):** reserve asset, digital safe haven (the first cryptocurrency, often considered as digital gold).

**Ethereum (ETH):** driver of Web3 innovation, leading decentralised platform (DeFi, NFTs, smart contracts).

**Why were these two cryptoassets chosen?**

Because they combine the key criteria of a robust basket:

- **Major weight in overall market capitalisation**, ensuring strong representativeness.

- **Very high liquidity**, allowing for fast and transparent transactions.

- **Superior resilience**, thanks to market depth that helps to cushion peaks in volatility.

**How is the capital allocated within the basket?**

The capital is allocated according to a predefined target allocation, with a significant share assigned to Bitcoin, which is historically the least volatile asset.

**Indicative allocation:**

- 70% BTC

- 30% ETH

You can view the allocation of your basket at any time from within the application (go to your Crypto Basket and check the allocation breakdown).

## Risks
Crypto Baskets invest in digital assets which are subject to significant price volatility:
- **The value of your basket can go down as well as up**, and you may not recover the amount you invested.
- Crypto assets are not covered by any deposit protection scheme.
- Rebalancing (see [[what-is-rebalancing]]) helps maintain target allocations but **does not eliminate market risk**.
- Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future performance.
- For fees, see [[what-are-the-fees-for-the-crypto-basket]].
