# How can I change the app language?

## Details
You can change the language of your application at any time from your profile settings.

**Steps to follow:**

- From the home page, click on **Profile** (top left)

- Scroll down to the **Settings** section

- Select **Customisation**

- Click on **App language**

- Choose your preferred language:** French, English** or **Italian**

The change will be applied immediately across your entire interface.
