# Why is my transfer pending?

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### **Why is my transfer pending?**

Transfers made to your Vancelian account are processed through the **SEPA** network.

On average, a SEPA transfer is credited within 1 to 3 business days, but in some cases, it can take up to **5 business days.**

This delay is due to interbank processing times between financial institutions and, in certain situations, internal compliance checks.

When such a verification occurs, a notification will appear in your application to inform you.

In most cases, you simply need to wait until the end of the processing period.

 

#### **Why can my first deposit be pending?**

During your first deposit, **additional verification** is carried out to confirm that the bank account used to send the funds belongs to you.

This may cause a short delay before the funds are credited, particularly in the following cases:

- The transfer was made from an account where the **sender’s name does not exactly match** the name on your Vancelian account

- The transfer comes from a **joint account**, which requires confirmation of account ownership

**If you are using a joint account**, please send the bank account’s RIB in PDF format to our customer service team so that your deposit can be validated.

 

#### **What should I do if the transfer is still not credited after 5 business days?**

If your transfer has not been credited after 5 business days, or if you need to provide an additional document (such as a joint account RIB), [please contact our support team.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

Our team will review the situation and update you as soon as possible.
