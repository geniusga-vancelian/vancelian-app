# How does the Exclusive Offer – Eco-Friendly Mining in Ethiopia work?


## Short answer
As of 14 April 2025, the eco-friendly Bitcoin Mining in Ethiopia offer reached its €2.5 million cap and is now closed — no new deposits are accepted via the app. Existing investors continue to receive daily returns in EURC credited to their available balance throughout the investment period. **Vancelian LTD (UAE-ADGM)** is the contractual counterparty of the programme, with **Automata France SAS** operating as the PSAN-regulated intermediary for European clients and **Hearst Solution FZCO** running the physical infrastructure in Addis Ababa.

## Details

### Who stands behind your contract?
**Vancelian LTD (UAE-ADGM)** is the **contractual counterparty** of the Cloud Mining programme. **Automata France SAS** operates as the **PSAN-regulated intermediary** for European clients (subscription flow, EURC custody via Fireblocks, app display). **Hearst Solution FZCO** is the **technical operator** on the ground, running the Bitmain S21 Pro miners powered by 100% renewable hydroelectric energy in Addis Ababa.
*As of Monday, 14 April 2025, the exclusive offer for eco-friendly Bitcoin Mining in Ethiopia, with a total amount of €2.5 million, was fully reached following its availability to Elite members (on 7th April) and Platinum members (on 14th April).*

*As a result, the offer is now closed, and it is no longer possible to make new deposits into this project via the app.*

*For all clients who currently hold this offer, daily returns will continue to be credited directly to your available balance within the project.*

 

Invest in Eco-friendly **Bitcoin Mining** in Ethiopia, powered by **100% renewable energy,** and generate daily income while supporting a sustainable economy through high-performance machines.

The mining site is located in **Addis Ababa, Ethiopia**, operated by our partner [**Hearst**](https://www.hearstcorporation.io/) and powered by **Bitmain S21 Pro** miners running on one of Africa's largest hydroelectric dams.

This new Cloud Mining program will be initially available to our Elite members, with early access starting on April 7, 2025, and will then be open to Platinum members from April 14, 2025.

- Commitment period: maturity 31 December 2028 for all migrated positions (pre-migration contracts retain their original 4-year term from deposit, visible in your client account)

- Limited offer: 2.5 million euros

- Flexibility: Minimum 6-month lock-up period. You can deposit and withdraw twice a year

- Annual return (APR): Depending on your [Privilege Club](https://vancelian.com/en/memberships) status

- **Reward distribution**: Daily

#### How does the investment cap work?
Each investor is free to choose the amount that suits them, within the limit of the available offer.

Investments are available starting from **1 EUR** when depositing in EUR, or **approximately 10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**

 

#### What is the deposit and withdrawal policy?
Initial deposits are possible from the launch of the offer until it is fully subscribed.

Subsequently, deposit windows will be opened** twice a year.**

Withdrawal requests are allowed twice a year only if the contracts have been held for at least **6 months.**

Please note that deposit and withdrawal requests are subject to a supply and demand system.

For a withdrawal or deposit to be possible, another investor must wish to deposit or withdraw an equivalent amount.

[*For more information, please refer to our Terms and Conditions – Exclusive Mining Program Offer.*](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers-tou/exclusives-offers-mining)

 

#### Are there fees associated with withdrawals?
After an initial **6-month** commitment, you can benefit from preferential early exit conditions, subject to a **5% fee.**

Whether you choose to partially or fully exit this offer, the exit must occur during the designated exit windows.

After **24 months of commitment,** there will be **no exit fees.**
