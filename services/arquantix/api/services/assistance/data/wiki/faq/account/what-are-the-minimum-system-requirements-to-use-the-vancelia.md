# What are the minimum system requirements to use the Vancelian app?

## Short answer
To use the Vancelian app, your smartphone must run iOS 14 or later (for Apple devices) or Android 8 or later (for Android devices). Check your phone's operating system version in settings before downloading the app.

## Details
To ensure smooth and secure use of the Vancelian application, your smartphone must meet the following minimum requirements:
 

#### **Minimum system requirements**
**iOS**

- iOS 14 or later

- Compatible devices: iPhone 6s and newer models

**Android**

- Android 8.0 (Oreo) or later

- The application does not work on de-Googled devices or on versions of Android without Google services

**Why these requirements?**

These minimum versions ensure:

- Better technical performance

- Access to essential security updates

- Compatibility with the application’s features

**Best practices to avoid issues**

To ensure the app works correctly, we recommend:

- Keeping your operating system up to date

- Installing available app updates from the App Store or Play Store

- Restarting your smartphone regularly

- Avoiding modified or unofficial versions of Android (custom ROMs, de-Googled systems, etc.)

**Still experiencing an issue?**

If the application does not function properly despite meeting the minimum requirements, we invite you to consult this article:[ What should I do if I experience issues with the application?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/17466278678545-I-m-facing-issues-accessing-the-application)
