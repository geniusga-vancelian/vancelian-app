# How to order your payment card?

## Short answer
Once your account is verified, you can order the Vancelian payment card of your choice directly from the application. Steps to order your card

## Details
Once your account is verified, you can order the Vancelian payment card of your choice directly from the application.

**Steps to order your card**

- From the main page, click on **Card** (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Select the** card of your choice** and the desired colour
*To learn more about our different cards, *[*consult this article*](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8666567993745-Which-cards-does-Vancelian-offer-and-what-are-their-differences)

- Carefully check that the **delivery address** displayed is correct (see tips below)

- Choose your **secret question** and enter the answer

- **Confirm** the payment of the delivery fees

The delivery fees are €9.90, deducted directly from your Euro Account at the time of the order.

Once this step is completed, your order is confirmed.

**Delivery details**

Once ready, your card is shipped by Royal Mail Post and then transferred to your national postal provider in your country for final delivery.

- **Delivery cost:** €9.90 (automatically deducted from your Euro Account)

- **Delivery time:** between 12 and 20 working days from the order validation
*If you have not received your card after 20 working days, please *[*consult this article*](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8668383348497-I-never-received-my-card)

You will receive a notification and an email as soon as your card is ready for shipment.

**Delivery address**

Your card can only be delivered to the address registered on your Vancelian account.

If you need to update this address before ordering, consult this article:

[How can I declare a change of residential address?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/41592667574161-How-can-I-declare-a-change-of-residential-address)

Please note that it is not possible to modify the delivery address once the card has been dispatched.
