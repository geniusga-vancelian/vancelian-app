# How can I close my account?

## Short answer
You can close your Vancelian account directly from your application, provided that all your wallets and vaults are empty. We recommend downloading your statements before completing the closure.

## Details
You can close your Vancelian account directly from your application, provided that all your wallets and vaults are empty.

We recommend downloading your statements before completing the closure.

**Steps to close your account:**

- From the home page, click on **Profile** (top left)

- Scroll down to the** Settings** section

- Select** Security and privacy**

- Click on **Close account** (bottom of the page)

- Confirm your choice **twice** to finalise the request

**What conditions must be met before closing?**

Before you can close your account, you must:

- Empty all your vaults and wallets, including your balances in euros and cryptoassets

- Ensure that no assets are held on your account

**Without this, the account closure will not be possible.**

**If you hold projects with a commitment period**

If you are participating in a project with a fixed commitment period (for example an Exclusive Offer or an investment product with a fixed duration), the account closure cannot be completed **until the end of the commitment period.**

Once the project has matured and the funds have been released, you will be able to submit the closure request from the application.
