# Privilege Club: complete benefits and rates by tier

## Short answer
The Vancelian Privilege Club has **5 tiers** — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Elite. Higher tiers unlock **better savings/investment yields, lower trading fees, free cards, larger card limits, and higher referral commissions**. Top-line APYs at Elite: **Flexible Vault 6.43%**, **Future Vault 8.31%**, **Dubai Villa 11.5%**, **Bali Villa 11%**, **Mining 3%**. Top-line trading fee at Elite: **0.25%** (vs 0.95% Bronze). Referral commission at Elite: **15%** of trading revenues from your referees.
