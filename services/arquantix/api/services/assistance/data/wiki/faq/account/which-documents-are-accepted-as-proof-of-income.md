# Which documents are accepted as proof of income?

## Short answer
Please refer to the detailed instructions below.

## Details
#### **Please find below a list of documents accepted as proof of income:**

- Payslip (dated less than 3 months ago)

- Income from social benefits such as unemployment, RSA, disability (statement from the last completed month) + beneficiary bank statement showing the income received

- Most recent tax notice showing income earned over the past year

- Proof of inheritance + beneficiary bank statement showing the inheritance received

- Proof of sale of company shares + beneficiary bank statement showing the sale

- Loan agreement + beneficiary bank statement showing the loan payment

- Property or investment income + beneficiary bank statement showing the income received

- Notarial deed or certified notarial statement for the sale of a property + beneficiary bank statement showing the sale

- Cryptocurrency transaction statement in the client’s name showing the profits obtained

- Investment or savings statement + beneficiary bank statement showing the funds received

#### **If you own a company:**
To validate your proof of income, you must provide at **least 2** of the documents listed below:

- Most recent company financial statement

- Company bank statement dated less than 3 months ago

- Tax notice

*You may only submit these documents by email to: support@vancelian.com, as the application currently allows you to upload only one document at a time.*

**Please note that the proof of income submitted must be dated less than 3 months ago and be in PDF, JPEG, JPG, or PNG format.**

**Please also note that we do not accept the following documents:**

- A statement of a single transaction

- Bank account details (RIB)

- Employment contracts

- Invoices
