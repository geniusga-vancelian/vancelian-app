# How do I activate my card ?

## Details
Once you receive your card, you must activate it directly from the Vancelian application before you can use it.

**Steps to activate your card:**

- Go to the **Card **section (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Click on **Activate card**

- Enter the last **4 digits of the PAN** (the 16-digit card number shown on the front)

- Click the confirmation button to **validate** the activation

Once the activation is confirmed, a confirmation message will appear on the screen and your **PIN code** will be displayed immediately.

**Good to know**

It is not necessary to make a cash withdrawal at an ATM to activate your card.
