# Where is AKTIO listed?

## Short answer
AKTIO is currently listed on BitMart and available directly in the Vancelian app. Vancelian manages liquidity through Automata ICO Ltd via OTC exchanges and the BitMart order book to ensure stable pricing and controlled availability.

## Details
Today, AKTIO remains a public-facing token, with:

- Access via a centralised exchange platform ([BitMart](https://www.bitmart.com/en-GB))

- Direct access via the [Vancelian](https://vancelian.com/en) application, which acts as an account intermediary

Within this framework, Vancelian may, through [**Automata ICO Ltd**](https://vancelian.com/en), carry out over-the-counter (OTC) exchanges, notably by relying on its own token reserve.

The detailed history of AKTIO token listings and delistings is available below.
 

#### **What is the listing history of the token?**

- 13/04/2022: [Bittrex](https://bittrexglobal.com/) (AKTIO/USDT – ICO, closed in 2023)

- 18/07/2022: [MEXC](https://www.mexc.com/) – AKTIO/USDT

- 07/06/2023: [BitMart](https://www.bitmart.com/en-US/trade/AKTIO_USDC) – AKTIO/USDT

- 29/08/2023: [Coinstore](https://www.coinstore.com/home?ts=1766487231755) – AKTIO/USDT

- 02/04/2024: [AscendEX](https://ascendex.com/en),[ XT.COM](https://www.xt.com/en), [DigiFinex](https://www.digifinex.com/en-ww/) – AKTIO/USDT

- 24/04/2024: [Biconomy](https://www.biconomy.com/en) – AKTIO/USDC

- 30/04/2024: XeggeX – AKTIO/USDC (closed in 2025)

- 06/05/2024: [WhiteBit](https://whitebit.com/) – AKTIO/USDC

Since May 2024, all USDT pairs have been replaced by USDC pairs, a stablecoin regulated in Europe.

Since 2025, AKTIO has no longer been available on exchanges other than [**BitMart**](https://www.bitmart.com/en-GB), in order to control liquidity and facilitate its management.
 

#### **How is token liquidity ensured?**
Liquidity is ensured exclusively by Vancelian via Automata ICO (Ireland), in AKTIO and USDC, for:

- The Vancelian application

- The BitMart platform (order book)
 

#### **Please note:**
AKTIO primarily serves to access the Privilège programme of the Vancelian application; current liquidity does not allow for active trading of large volumes.

Without mass adoption and sufficient volumes, there is a significant imbalance in large-amount transactions involving the AKTIO token, with risks of slippage, loss, and illiquidity for high-volume operations.

It is strongly recommended to use the Vancelian application for exchange operations in order to limit these risks.
 

#### **Will the token be listed elsewhere?**
New listings may be considered if:

- The liquidity to be provided is viable and remains consistent

- The adoption of the application progresses rapidly

**Objective: **maintain a balance between price, utility, and adoption of Vancelian products.
 

#### **How can I stay informed about upcoming listings?**

- Follow Vancelian on social media

- Subscribe to our email communications
 

**Important: **Vancelian will never contact you privately to propose a new listing.

In case of doubt, contact [customer support](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support) directly.
