# How do I copy and share my IBAN?

## Short answer
You can easily find and share your IBAN from the Vancelian app.

## Details
You can easily **find** and **share** your IBAN from the Vancelian app.

#### Steps to follow:

Option 1:

- From the home screen, tap** "More"** in the four options below your total funds.

- Select **"**IBAN".

Option 2:

- Tap your **profile** icon at the top left of the home screen.

- In the **"**My Account" section, select **"**IBAN".

Share or copy your IBAN:

From the IBAN page, you can:

- - **Copy** your IBAN to use it in another application.

- **Share** your IBAN directly with your contacts via message, email, or other available channels.
