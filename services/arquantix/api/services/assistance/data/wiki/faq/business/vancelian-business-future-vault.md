# Vancelian Business — Future Vault

## Short answer
The **Future Vault** is Vancelian's structured corporate placement for **medium-term excess cash**. Each deposit is locked for **12 months**, in exchange for a **higher rate of up to 7% APY (daily compounding)**. Interest can be withdrawn 24/7; deposit and withdrawal are **free of charge**. Designed to support planned financial projects (future expenses, investments, acquisitions) while optimising the return-to-safety balance.

## Details

| Feature | Value |
|---|---|
| Current rate | up to **7% APY** |
| Compounding | Yes (daily) |
| Earnings | Daily |
| Interests withdrawal | 24/7 |
| Deposit & withdrawal fees | **Free** |
| Commitment period | **1 year (12 months)** |

### When to use it
- Treasury earmarked for known future expenses (acquisitions, capex, planned investments)
- Optimising return on cash that you do not need to touch for 12 months
- Layered approach: Flexible Vault for liquidity + Future Vault for medium-term

## Caveats
- The 7% rate is the **top-of-range live rate** — actual rate varies by tier and market performance.
- The 12-month lock is per deposit. Deposits made later have their own 12-month clock.
- See [[../legal-compliance/risk-warning-summary]] — capital is exposed to digital asset risk.
