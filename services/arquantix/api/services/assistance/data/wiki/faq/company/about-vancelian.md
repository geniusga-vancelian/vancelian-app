# About Vancelian

## Short answer
Vancelian is a **French-regulated savings and investment app** combining traditional finance with blockchain technology. Operated by **Automata France SAS**, it is registered as a **Digital Asset Service Provider (DASP) with the AMF under number E2023-087** and is preparing to operate in the UAE through Automata FZE (in-principle approval from VARA). As of the latest snapshot: **500,000+ downloads**, **€100M+ assets under management**, **€7M+ interest paid to clients**, **50 employees** across Europe and the UAE.

## Details

### Mission
> *"At Vancelian, our mission is to shift savings and investment into the finance of tomorrow."* — Gaël Itier, CEO

Vancelian's stated ambition is to combine the foundations of traditional finance with the new mechanisms of decentralised finance and blockchain, with planned integration of artificial intelligence for personalised advice. The roadmap relies on rigorous mastery of emerging technologies and continuous progress within an evolving regulatory framework.

### Geographic structure
- **Europe (France):** Automata France SAS — DASP registered with the AMF (E2023-087)
- **United Arab Emirates:** Automata FZE — In-Principle Approval from VARA for Broker Dealer, Management & Investments, Lending & Borrowing, and Advisory (full licence pending — see [[vancelian-receives-vara-in-principle-approval]])

### Trusted infrastructure partners
- **AWS** (cloud infrastructure)
- **PCI-DSS** (payment security standard)

## Caveats
- Vancelian is operated by Automata France SAS. Any reference to "Vancelian's" regulatory status, T&Cs or liability points back to Automata France SAS unless explicitly stated otherwise.
- Vancelian's UAE entity (Automata FZE) holds an **In-Principle Approval** from VARA, **not a full licence** — regulated VA activities cannot start in the UAE until VARA grants final authorisation.
