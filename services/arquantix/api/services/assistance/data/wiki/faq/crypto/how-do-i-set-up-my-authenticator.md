## Details
**Please note that you will need to download and configure an Authenticator (for example: *****Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, etc.*****) in order to be able to withdraw cryptoassets from the Vancelian application.**

Once you have downloaded the Authenticator of your choice from the Google Play Store or App Store, please follow the steps below to link it to your Vancelian application:

 

#### **Step 1: On the Vancelian application**

- Go to your Profile

- Scroll down to the Settings section

- Select Security and Privacy

- Tap Authenticator App

- An explanatory window will appear with a 32-character key

- Tap the **copy icon** on the right of the 32-character key

**Step 2: On your Authenticator**

- Add a new account by pasting the 32-character key you copied from the Vancelian app into your Authenticator app and follow the instructions:

- For [Microsoft Authenticator](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-gb/entra/identity/authentication/how-to-register-passkey-authenticator?tabs=iOS)

- For [Google Authenticator](https://support.google.com/accounts/answer/1066447?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&sjid=18438367550210540492-EU)

**Step 3: Back on the Vancelian application**

- Scroll down to the bottom of the page

- Tap Activate Authentication App

- Enter the **first code** displayed in your Authenticator

- Then enter the **next code** generated by your Authenticator

- Once you have entered the second code, tap Next to confirm the setup

- If successful, a window will appear confirming that your authentication app has been activated

#### **Important:**

- When setting up your Authenticator, make sure to select a time-based key and not a counter-based one, otherwise it will not work.

- Please note: if you completely close your Vancelian app during this process, you will need to **restart the operation** in full with a new key.

- If you need to switch to a different Authenticator app, please contact [our support team](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-can-I-reach-Vancelian-customer-support). An identity verification will be required so we can **remove the old key** before you can register a new one.
