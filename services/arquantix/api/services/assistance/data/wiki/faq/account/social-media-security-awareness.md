# Social Media Security Awareness

## Short answer
The security of your account is a priority. On social media, fake accounts or malicious individuals may attempt to impersonate Vancelian or a member of our team. Here are the best practices to follow to keep yourself protected.

## Details
The security of your account is a priority. On social media, fake accounts or malicious individuals may attempt to impersonate Vancelian or a member of our team.

Here are the **best practices** to follow to keep yourself protected.

**Vancelian will never contact you on social media**

For your security:

- We will never initiate a** private conversation** on Telegram, Instagram, Facebook, X/Twitter, WhatsApp, or any other social network.

- We will never ask you to:

- **Transfer funds** to an external account

- Share your **PIN** or** login** details

- **Download** a file or install third-party software

- Access a **non-official website**

If you receive a private message claiming to be from Vancelian: **do not reply and do not click on any link.**

**How does Vancelian actually contact you?**

The Vancelian team may contact you only **by email** or **by phone call**, depending on the situation. Never through private messages on social media.

If you receive a message and doubt its authenticity, **contact our **[**customer support**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)** directly through official channels** to verify whether we attempted to reach you.

We can confirm immediately whether the contact was legitimate and take action if needed.

**How to stay vigilant on social media?**

Here are some simple rules to reduce risks:

- Follow only our **official accounts** (check links from our website or app).

- Be cautious of **fake profiles**: fraudsters often use our logos or names.

- Never share** sensitive information** in private messages, even if the account appears legitimate.

- Avoid **publicly posting** your email address, phone number, or screenshots of your app.

**What should you do if you receive a suspicious message?**

If someone contacts you claiming to be from Vancelian:

- **Do not reply**

- **Do not click on any link**

- **Do not provide any personal information**

- **Immediately report the attempt to our team by contacting **[**customer support**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

We will confirm whether the message was legitimate.

**General best practices to secure your accounts**

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your social media accounts.

- Use strong, unique passwords.

- Regularly check active sessions on your apps and log out of unknown devices.

- Keep your smartphone and applications up to date to avoid security vulnerabilities.
