# How does the 7 luxury villas in Bali exclusive offer work?

## Short answer
**Closed since 6 October 2025** at the **€4 million** cap — no new deposits accepted. For existing investors: total commitment is **4 years**, daily returns at an **APR of 10.2%–11%** (depending on Privilege Club tier), paid in BTC and automatically converted to EURC. Withdrawal windows open **twice a year** after a **6-month minimum lock-up**; early exit costs **5%** until 24 months held, then **no exit fee**.

## Details
As of Monday, 6 October 2025, the exclusive offer for 7 luxury villas in Bali, with a total amount of €4 million, was fully reached.

As a result, the offer is now closed, and it is no longer possible to make new deposits into this exclusive offer via the app.

For all clients who currently hold this offer, daily returns will continue to be credited directly to your available balance within the project.

### The loan mechanic in one paragraph
This project is structured as a **BTC-denominated loan to The Heights Bali SAS** (the borrower SPV) with a **EUR reference value locked at deposit**. When you deposited in EUR or USDC, your funds were converted to BTC at the market rate at that time, and that EUR amount was locked as the reference value that will be reimbursed at maturity — regardless of where the BTC price moves during the life of the loan. The **interest is generated in BTC** by the borrower and automatically converted into EURC, credited daily to your Available wallet. At maturity, your **capital is returned in BTC calculated on the basis of the initial EUR reference** — so you recover the same euro value initially invested. This is why the product is described as *"crypto-native, insulated from BTC volatility"*. For the full mechanic across all BTC Lending projects, see [[how-exclusive-offer-btc-lending-works]].

You can make a deposit from as little as 1EUR or **approximately**** ****10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**

#### What are the main features of this offer?

- **Commitment period:** 4 years.

- **Offer limited** to 4 million euros.

- **Flexibility:** Minimum 6-month lock-up period. You have the option to deposit and withdraw twice a year.

- **Annual return (APR):** Between 10.2% and 11%, depending on your Privilege Club status.

- **Payment frequency of returns:** Daily.

How does the investment cap work?

Each investor is free to choose the amount that suits them, within the available offer limit.

Investments are available starting from** ****1 EUR** when depositing in EUR, or **approximately**** ****10 USDC (the exact amount will be displayed at the moment of deposit, depending on the applicable conversion limits).**

 

#### What is the deposit and withdrawal policy?

- Initial deposits are possible from the launch of the offer until the offer is fully subscribed. After that, deposit windows will open **twice a year for 15 days.**

- Withdrawal requests are only available** twice a year,** provided the contracts have been held for at least **6 months.**

Please note that withdrawal and deposit requests are subject to a supply and demand system.

In order for a withdrawal or deposit to be possible, another investor must express the intention to deposit or withdraw an equivalent amount.

For more information, please refer to our[ *General Terms and Conditions of Use – Exclusive Offer The Heights Bali,*](https://vancelian.com/en/exclusives-offers-tou/exclusives-offers-theheightsbali) in particular the section *Operation of exit windows and queuing mechanism,* section 4.1 *Overview of the features of the Exclusive Offers Service.*

#### Are there any fees associated with withdrawals?
After an initial commitment of 6 months, you will be eligible for privileged early exit conditions, subject to a **5% fee.**

Whether you choose to exit partially or fully from the offer, the exit must occur during the designated exit windows.

After **24 months** of commitment, there will be **no exit fees.**
