# How are the returns generated on the Dubai Villa — Al Barari exclusive offer?

## Short answer
The Dubai Villa — Al Barari offer follows the same 100% crypto BTC loan mechanism as all Vancelian real estate Exclusive Offers (see [[how-exclusive-offer-btc-lending-works]]). You lend directly to **Solaria**, the project counterparty, who uses those funds to refinance the acquisition and complete renovation of **Villa A22** in The Nest, Al Barari (Dubai). The return comes from the projected revenue of that real estate operation. Interest is paid daily at **10.7% to 11.5% APR** (indicative, depending on Privilege Club status). Maturity: **1 May 2027**. The full financial details are in the official commercial brochure, available in the offer detail within the Vancelian app and on the Vancelian website.

## Details

### The generic mechanism — applied to Dubai Villa

The refinancing mechanism, why interest starts from the day after your deposit, the counterparty's exit strategy (off-plan sale, post-completion sale, or rental), and the reason for the attractive rate are explained in detail on the shared reference page: **[[how-exclusive-offer-btc-lending-works]]**.

For this specific offer, the project counterparty is **Solaria** (RCS Antibes 908 978 893) and the underlying asset is **Villa A22** — a lakeside 5-bedroom property in The Nest, Al Barari, expanding from 693 m² to 1,142 m² through a complete architectural transformation.

### What is specific to the Dubai Villa offer

- **Project counterparty:** Solaria, via a dedicated SPV for the project
- **Underlying asset:** Villa A22, The Nest, Al Barari — Dubai
- **Total financing:** €11,600,000
- **Commitment maturity:** 1 May 2027 (fixed for all investors)
- **APR:** 10.7% to 11.5% (indicative, depends on Privilege Club tier), paid daily
- **Operational partners:** Al Hilal Engineering Consultants (design & execution), Maria Morris Global (brokerage & resale)
- **Projected timeline:** works Jan 2026 → completion Sept 2026 → estimated sale Jan 2027

### Where to find the full financial details

The official commercial brochure contains the complete financing plan, cost breakdown, projected gross margin, timeline, and partner credentials. It is available directly in the offer detail within the Vancelian app and on the official Vancelian website. Always consult this documentation before investing.

## Caveats
- Returns are indicative and not guaranteed. The investment carries a risk of partial or total capital loss.
- Solaria bears the debt and the obligation to repay; Vancelian does not guarantee repayment.
- For any question about a specific contractual clause or scenario, contact `support@vancelian.com`.
