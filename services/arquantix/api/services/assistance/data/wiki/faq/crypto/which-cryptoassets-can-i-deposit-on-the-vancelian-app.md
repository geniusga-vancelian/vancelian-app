# Which cryptoassets can I deposit on the Vancelian app?

## Short answer
Vancelian supports deposits of AKTIO, AAVE, LINK, ETH, FET, PEPE, SHIB, USDC, and UNI primarily via Ethereum (ERC-20). USDC can also be deposited via the BASE network. Always verify the correct network when depositing to avoid permanent loss of funds.

## Details
You can deposit the following cryptoassets into your Vancelian wallet:

- **Vancelian (AKTIO)**

- **Aave (AAVE)**

- **Chainlink (LINK)**

- **Ethereum (ETH)**

- **Fetch.ai (FET)**

- **Pepe (PEPE)**

- **Shiba Inu (SHIB)**

- **USD Coin (USDC)**

- Uniswap (UNI)

#### **Which networks are available for deposits?**

- Ethereum (ERC-20): all of the above cryptoassets can be deposited via the Ethereum network.

- BASE: only available for USDC deposits.

This means that USDC can be deposited either via the Ethereum (ERC-20) network or via the BASE network.

To better understand the difference between the two networks, you can consult [this article.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/38330541078417-Understanding-the-Ethereum-Base-Blockchains)

 

For detailed instructions on how to make a deposit, please refer to this article: [How to deposit cryptoassets on the Vancelian app.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/16583428350865-How-to-deposit-cryptoassets-on-the-Vancelian-app)

 

Warning: if you send a cryptoasset on an unsupported network, or transfer a cryptoasset that is not available on our app, your funds will be permanently lost and cannot be recovered.It is therefore essential to always verify and select the correct network when making deposits and withdrawals.
