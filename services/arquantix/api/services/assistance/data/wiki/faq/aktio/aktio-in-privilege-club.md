# How does AKTIO work in the Privilege Club?

## Short answer
AKTIO is the **fuel of the Privilege Club loyalty programme** at Vancelian. You earn **Loyalty Points** by **holding AKTIO** in your Vancelian wallet, and you earn **more** by **locking AKTIO for 3 or 12 months**. Loyalty Points determine your tier (Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Elite), and higher tiers unlock better rates, lower fees and premium benefits.

## Details

### The two AKTIO-to-Loyalty-Points mechanisms

**1. AKTIO Holding Service**
Simply holding AKTIO tokens in your Vancelian User Digital Wallet — without locking or staking them — allocates Loyalty Points. The number of points is scaled to the **volume of AKTIO held**.

**2. AKTIO Locking Wallet Service**
You can choose to **lock** AKTIO for a fixed duration to earn more Loyalty Points than by simple holding. Two durations are available:

- **3-month lock** — shorter commitment, fewer Loyalty Points
- **12-month lock** — longer commitment, more Loyalty Points

You can mix durations: locking half your AKTIO for 3 months and the other half for 12 months creates **two separate locking wallets** in the Mobile Application.

### How Loyalty Points translate into tier

Loyalty Points accumulate and determine your **Privilege Club tier**. The tiers (from lowest to highest) are:

**Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Elite**

Each upward move unlocks better benefits — see the full matrix in [[privilege-club-tier-benefits]].

### What the tier change gets you

Moving from Bronze to Elite, here are a few headline improvements (full table in the linked page):

| Benefit | Bronze → Elite |
|---|---|
| Flexible Vault APY | 5.10% → **6.43%** |
| Future Vault APY | 6.59% → **8.31%** |
| Trading fees | 0.95% → **0.25%** |
| Referral commission | 1% → **15%** |
| Monthly ATM allowance | €200 → **€1,500** |
| Digital asset payment limit | €100 → **€5,000** |

### Typical user journey

1. You acquire AKTIO either on the Vancelian app or on an external exchange (BitMart, Bittrex Global) and deposit it to your Vancelian wallet.
2. You choose between holding (flexible) or locking (3 or 12 months) to decide your Loyalty Points strategy.
3. Your Loyalty Points accumulate; your tier updates accordingly.
4. The benefits of your current tier automatically apply to your other Vancelian services (vaults, trading, cards, transfers).

## Requirements / Documents
- A **verified Vancelian account** (KYC completed).
- AKTIO tokens — acquired either through the Vancelian app or deposited from an external wallet.

## Caveats
- **Staked AKTIO cannot simultaneously earn Loyalty Points via the Holding Service**. The services are exclusive: holding, locking and the legacy Staking Service are distinct.
- The **legacy Staking Service is closed to new subscriptions since 21 October 2024**. Current tier acceleration uses the Holding and Locking services.
- Exact Loyalty Points formulas may evolve — see the app and Privilege Club documentation for live values.
- **AKTIO is not withdrawable to an external wallet from the Vancelian app** (EEA clients). You can buy, sell and deposit AKTIO in the app, but external withdrawal of the AKTIO token itself is not supported. To retrieve the capital, sell AKTIO in-app against EUR, USDC or another withdrawable asset and withdraw that asset normally.
