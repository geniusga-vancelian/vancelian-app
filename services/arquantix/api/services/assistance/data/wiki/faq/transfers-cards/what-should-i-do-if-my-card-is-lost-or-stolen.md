# What should I do if my card is lost or stolen?

## Short answer
If you believe your card is lost or has been stolen, it is essential to act quickly to secure your funds and prevent any fraudulent use. Step 1: Immediately block your card

## Details
If you believe your card is lost or has been stolen, it is essential to act quickly to secure your funds and prevent any fraudulent use.

**Step 1: Immediately block your card**

To block your card, open the Vancelian app and follow the instructions detailed in this article:

[**How do I freeze my card?**](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/8669636395537-How-do-I-freeze-my-card)

Once the card is blocked, no new payment can be made, but operations already authorised before the block may still be charged.

**Step 2: Permanently cancel your card**

If you are certain that your card is lost or stolen, you can permanently cancel it from the app.

**Important:** this action is irreversible.

**Here’s how to proceed:**

- Go to the **Card** tab (shortcut at the bottom of the screen)

- Scroll to the bottom of the page

- Select **Cancel my card**

- **Confirm** your choice

**Once cancelled, the card can no longer be used or reactivated.**

**Step 3: Order a new card**

After cancellation, you can order a new card directly in the app:

[How to order your payment card?](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/26957729446545-How-to-order-your-payment-card)

**Good to know**

- Blocking your card is reversible, unlike cancellation.

- Cancellation should be a last resort: if you are not sure the card is lost, start with a temporary block.

- If you believe you have been a victim of fraud, immediately contact the relevant authorities to report the incident.
