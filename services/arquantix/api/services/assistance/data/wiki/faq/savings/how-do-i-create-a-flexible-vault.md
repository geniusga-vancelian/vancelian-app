# How do I create a Flexible Vault?

## Short answer
To create a Flexible Vault, go to Savings Projects, tap the + icon, select Flexible Vault, accept the Terms and Conditions, name your project (and optionally add a photo), then make your first deposit in EUR or USDC. You can create up to 5 Flexible Vaults to organize savings around different goals.

## Details
#### How to create a Flexible Vault from the app:

- From the main page, tap on Savings Projects

- Tap the + icon in the top right corner and select Flexible Vault

- A window will appear with information about this Vault

- You must **accept** the Terms and Conditions

- Tap on Open a Flexible Project

- Give it a **name** of your choice, you can also personalise it with a **photo** now or later

- Tap on Make my first deposit

- Read the information window, then tap Accept and Deposit

- Choose whether to deposit in **EUR** or **USDC**, and enter the amount*

 

#### How many Flexible Vaults can I create?
You can create up to **5** Flexible Vaults.

You can personalise your project at any time if your savings goal changes, by clicking on the three dots in the top right corner, then selecting Customise.

 

#### Can I close a Flexible Vault?
If there are no funds left in the Vault, you can close your Flexible solution directly in the app.

Go to your Flexible Project, scroll to the bottom of the page, then select Close the Flexible Vault.

 

**There is no investment cap. However, a technical limit may apply per deposit or withdrawal depending on the amount. This will be shown at the time of the operation. You can make multiple transactions if needed.*
