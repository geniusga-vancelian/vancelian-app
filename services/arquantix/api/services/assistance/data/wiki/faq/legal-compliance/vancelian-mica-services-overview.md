# What MiCA-licensed services does Vancelian provide?

## Short answer

Automata France (the entity operating Vancelian in Europe) is registered as a PSAN (E2023-087) with the AMF and licensed as a Crypto-Asset Service Provider (PSCA) under MiCA. The license covers seven core services: custody and administration, exchange crypto↔fiat, exchange crypto↔crypto, order execution, crypto advisory, portfolio management, and crypto-asset transfers.

## Details

### The European operating entity
The entity that carries the Vancelian service in Europe is **Automata France SAS**, registered as a **PSAN** (Provider of Services on Crypto-Assets) with the French AMF under licence number **E2023-087** and licensed as a **Crypto-Asset Service Provider (PSCA)** under MiCA. Automata France's business registration is RCS **902 498 617**, and the company is headquartered at Coloft – Arteparc – Bâtiment A Sophia, 965 Avenue Roumanille, 06410 Biot, Antibes, France. All European-client services operate under this legal envelope.

### The seven services authorised under MiCA
The licence covers seven distinct services, each mapped to a specific article of MiCA. Together they define exactly what Vancelian is allowed to do for its European clients.

**1. Custody and administration** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(a)) — safeguarding your crypto-assets and private keys, either through direct custody operated by Vancelian or through delegation to institutional partners.

**2. Exchange crypto ↔ fiat** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(b)) — buying crypto using EUR or other fiat currencies, and selling crypto to receive EUR or other fiat currencies.

**3. Exchange crypto ↔ crypto** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(c)) — trading between different crypto-assets inside the platform without going through a fiat leg.

**4. Order execution for clients** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(e)) — executing your trading orders at the best available prices under formal best-execution obligations.

**5. Crypto advisory** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(h)) — providing personalised recommendations on crypto-assets, tailored to your profile and investment needs.

**6. Portfolio management** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(i)) — discretionary management of your crypto portfolios, including the professional management of Vaults and other structured products.

**7. Crypto-asset transfers** (MiCA Art. 3(16)(j)) — executing address-to-address transfers on DLT (blockchain), covering on-chain movement of your assets.

## Caveats

- This license covers European operations (EU/EEA) under MiCA. Specific restrictions may apply depending on your residency.
- Some services may be available only to certain client categories (individuals, institutional investors, etc.).
- The license scope is subject to ongoing AMF supervision and may be updated.
- For specific questions about which services are available in your jurisdiction, contact Vancelian support.
