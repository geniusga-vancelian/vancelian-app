# Are there any risks of capital loss?

## Short answer
Like any product that delivers a return, Vancelian savings vaults (Flexible Vault, Future Vault) carry risk — this is inherent to any investment mechanism. The risks are identified and structurally limited: your funds are allocated across underlying programmes (Exclusive Offers, Cloud Mining) and an EURC liquidity reserve, and at no point are they allocated to volatile crypto-assets such as BTC or ETH. What limits the risk is the composition of the allocation itself and Vancelian's ability to adjust it over time.

## Details

**How the vault works — understanding the risk starts here**

A vault is an allocation. Your deposit in EURC is split by Vancelian across two components:
1. **An EURC liquidity pocket** — held in EURC (a stablecoin pegged to the euro), not invested, used to process deposits and withdrawals.
2. **Yield-generating allocations** — deployed into underlying programmes (Cloud Mining, Exclusive Offers such as Solaria) that generate the return.

You deposit and withdraw in EURC. You are never directly exposed to cryptocurrency price fluctuations — the vault's mechanics are based entirely on EURC.

**What are the risks — by component**

*On the EURC liquidity pocket:* the risk is limited to an extreme theoretical scenario where the EURC stablecoin loses its peg to the euro. This is a residual risk covered in the General Terms and Conditions of Use.

*On the yield-generating allocations:* the underlying programmes (Cloud Mining, Exclusive Offers) carry their own risk profiles — primarily counterparty risk (the programme operator's ability to perform and repay) and performance risk (the programme may deliver more or less than the indicative return). These are the risks that determine the vault's overall risk profile.

*On liquidity:* the EURC pocket ensures on-demand access to your funds under normal conditions. In a period of exceptionally high withdrawal demand, liquidity may be temporarily reduced — your withdrawal would remain queued until funds become available.

**What limits the risk**

The same elements that compose the vault also limit its risk exposure:
- The underlying programmes are selected and monitored by Vancelian.
- Vancelian can modify the allocation or its weighting over time to maintain control over the product's trajectory.
- The vault is structurally not allocated to volatile crypto-assets (no BTC, no ETH) — it is insensitive to crypto market fluctuations.

Returns are indicative and may evolve — verify the current rate in the Vancelian app. For more detailed questions on a specific allocation or the risk profile of a particular underlying programme, contact the Vancelian support team.
