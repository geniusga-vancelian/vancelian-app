# Account verification by bank transfer

## Short answer
To ensure user security and compliance with applicable regulations, Vancelian performs an account verification by bank transfer when you register. This step confirms that the account holder has a bank account in their own first and last name before full access to all app features is activated.

## Details
To ensure user security and compliance with applicable regulations, Vancelian performs an account verification by bank transfer when you **register.**

This step confirms that the account holder has a bank account in their own first and last name before full access to all app features is activated.

**Why is account verification by bank transfer necessary?**

This process ensures that funds originate from a personal bank account belonging to the user.

It strengthens security, prevents fraud, and ensures compliance with regulatory obligations related to **anti-money laundering and counter-terrorism financing.**

**How to complete your account verification?**

During registration, you will be prompted to complete your account verification directly from the **home page** of the application.

- Click on **Make my deposit** in the notification related to your registration

- Read the steps provided, then select** I understand**

- Click on **Select your bank** to access the list of accepted banks

- We will then display your **IBAN** and the instructions

- Make a **SEPA** transfer from a bank account in your name

Once the transfer is received, we will verify that the sender account information (first name, last name, IBAN) matches the details registered on your Vancelian account.

If the information matches, **your account will be verified** and you will gain access to all the features of the application.

**What if my bank is not on the list?**

The verification transfer must come from a **bank listed** in the selection provided in the application.

If your bank does not appear, you will need to use another eligible bank account to complete this first transfer.

**What if my first deposit comes from a joint account?**

If your first deposit is made from a joint account, you must send a PDF version of the **bank account details **(RIB) of the sending account to our customer support team.

This document must clearly display your information so we can perform a manual verification.

[How to contact customer support.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

**What are the processing times?**

Vancelian currently operates on the **SEPA network** for bank transfers.

A SEPA transfer may take up to **5 working days** to be credited.

If the bank account information does not match, the funds will be automatically returned to the sending account within **1 to 5 working days** after receipt.
