# How does Vancelian execute my trades?

## Short answer

When you buy or sell crypto-assets on Vancelian, the platform's operating entity (Automata France) acts as your direct and sole counterparty through a model called "own-account interposition." You never trade with other users or external platforms — Automata France determines the price, executes the transaction on its own account, and delivers the assets to you. This model is compliant with MiCA regulation and subject to best execution obligations.

## Details

**Execution model**
- Automata France provides two services: (1) exchange crypto↔crypto, and (2) buy/sell crypto↔fiat — both with systematic own-account interposition
- This is NOT order execution for third parties (RTO) per MiCA Art. 3.1.21
- You trade directly with Automata France, not with other users or external exchanges

**Best execution factors**
Vancelian ensures best execution by considering:
- Price
- Cost
- Speed
- Custody conditions

**Client consent and confirmation**
- You consent to the own-account interposition model via Terms & Conditions acceptance at registration
- Post-trade, you receive immediate confirmation including: date and time, crypto-asset identifier, buy/sell indicator, quantity, unit price, fees applied, and total price

**Transparency and reporting**
- Quarterly: Vancelian publishes average price and volume per crypto-asset on the app and reports to the AMF (French financial authority)
- This ensures you can verify pricing consistency over time

**Execution venues**
When Automata France executes your trade, it uses regulated liquidity providers:
- Scrypt (Swiss FINMA-regulated venue, qualifies as virtual asset trading facility under MiCA)
- BitMart (established DeFi liquidity source)

## Caveats

- This is a regulatory model description. Actual pricing is determined at the moment of trade and may vary based on market conditions.
- For detailed fee information and current price spreads, see the trading fees page.
- Vancelian reserves the right to adjust execution venues to maintain best execution standards.
- If you have questions about a specific trade execution, contact Vancelian support.
