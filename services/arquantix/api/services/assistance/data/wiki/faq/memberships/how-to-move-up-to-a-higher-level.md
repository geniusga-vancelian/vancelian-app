# How to move up to a higher level?

## Short answer
You level up in the Privilege Club by earning points through activities like making deposits, investments, referrals, and holding Vancelian tokens. You can check which actions earn points in your Profile section under "Earn points," where a full table shows points for each activity. The more points you accumulate, the higher your tier—from Bronze up to Elite.

## Details
Your progression in the Vancelian **Privilege Club** depends on the number of points you accumulate through your activities within the Vancelian application.

Each action, whether it is an investment, a referral, or a specific operation, allows you to increase your total points and reach a higher loyalty level.

#### How to check which actions earn points:

From the home page of your application:

- Click on your **Profile** (top left)

- Select your current** status** (Bronze, Silver, Gold, etc.)

- Scroll down and tap on **Earn points**

You will find a **full list of actions** that allow you to collect points, along with a summary table showing the points assigned to each action.

This will help you identify the most effective ways to progress to the next level in the Privilege Club.
