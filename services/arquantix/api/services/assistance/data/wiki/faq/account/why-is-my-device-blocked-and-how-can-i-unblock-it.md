# Why is my device blocked and how can I unblock it?

## Short answer
For security reasons, Vancelian only allows access to the application from one device at a time. This protects your account and prevents any unauthorised access.

## Details
For security reasons, Vancelian only allows access to the application from **one device at a time.**

This protects your account and prevents any unauthorised access.

When you log in for the first time, your phone automatically becomes the trusted device associated with your account.

**Why does the message “New device detected” appear?**

This message appears when you try to log in from a different device than the one registered as your trusted device.

For example, this may occur after:

- **A phone change**

- **A reinstallation of the application**

- **Other possible reasons: operating system update, app reset, or device reset**

To ensure your account’s security, the old device will be automatically deactivated, and only the new device can be activated once identity verification is completed.

#### **How to unblock access from the new device?**
To confirm that it is really you, identity verification will be requested directly in the application.

- Open the **Vancelian app** on the new device

- Follow the on-screen **instructions** to complete the identity verification

- Once verification is successful, your device will be registered as your **new trusted device**

Your account will then be unlocked and fully accessible from this new phone

**I cannot complete the identity verification: what should I do?**

If you encounter a technical issue, if verification fails, or if you are unable to proceed from your device: [Contact our customer support here](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/4409555298577-How-to-contact-Customer-Support)

Our team will assist you with the unblocking procedure.
