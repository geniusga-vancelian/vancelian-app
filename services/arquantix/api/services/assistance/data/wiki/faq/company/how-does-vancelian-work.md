# How does Vancelian work?

## Details
#### **What Does Vancelian Do?**

Vancelian is a **French fintech** company that designs savings and investment solutions accessible to everyone, starting from just €1.

Our mission is to **democratise the finance of tomorrow** by making the world of digital assets simple and secure.

We offer a complete ecosystem that enables both **individuals and professionals** to use, manage, and grow their digital assets on a daily basis, including an integrated wallet, tailored savings products, an euro account and card, as well as dedicated services for professionals.

To learn more about our philosophy, you can read our article on our [core values and commitments.](https://support.vancelian.com/hc/en-gb/articles/40174935110801-Our-Core-Values-and-Commitments)

#### **About Vancelian**

**Vancelian is not an online bank.**

We are a Digital Asset Service Provider ("PSAN"), offering a range of specialised services related to the management and use of digital assets.

As part of our activities, Automata France S.A.S acts as an Outsourced Service Provider for ModulR, allowing our users to access **payment services**, including SEPA transfers, VISA cards, and an euro account with a dedicated IBAN.

*Automata France S.A.S – acting as an Outsourced Service Provider for ModulR – offers its clients payment services through its partner ModulR (authorised and regulated as an electronic money institution by the Nederlandsche Bank (DNB) under registration number R182870 and by the FCA via ModulR FS Ltd under registration number 900573).
